Ecosystem Unification Benefits

The transition to a unified production environment on Vercel brings several improvements to the end-user experience.

  • Single Sign-On (SSO) One account for all Budol services. Sign in once to access Shopping, Payments, and Loans seamlessly.
  • Real-Time Sync Transaction updates in BudolPay are reflected immediately in BudolShap and your accounting statements.
  • Enhanced Reliability Production-grade hosting ensures that services are available 24/7 with minimal downtime during updates.

User Experience Impacts

During the deployment phases (Days 1-12), users may experience intermittent maintenance windows. These will be scheduled during off-peak hours and communicated via the application dashboard.
