User Manual - Version v255

What's New in v1.2.20

🚀 Improved Real-Time Reliability

Your wallet balance and transaction notifications are now more reliable. The app can now switch its communication technology in the background to ensure you always receive updates instantly, even if one service is undergoing maintenance.

✨ Splash Screen Updates

The app version is now clearly displayed at the bottom of the splash screen (v1.2.20), ensuring you know you are running the latest, most secure version of BudolPay.

How to Verify the Update

  1. Open the BudolPay app.
  2. On the splash screen, look for budolEcosystem 1.2.20 at the bottom.
  3. Login to your account and perform a test transaction to see instant balance updates.

Known Issues

None. This update focuses on internal stability and connectivity performance.
