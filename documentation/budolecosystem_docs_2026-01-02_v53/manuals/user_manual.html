User Manual - v53

Summary of Changes

Version v53 introduces behind-the-scenes improvements to how payments and wallet balances are calculated. While you won't see new buttons or menus, your transactions are now more secure and accurate.

What This Means for You

  • Precise Balances: Wallet balances and transaction histories are now calculated with higher precision, ensuring every centavo is accounted for.
  • Reliable Payments: Large orders or withdrawals are less likely to encounter rounding errors during the checkout process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Has my balance changed?

No, your balance remains the same. These changes only affect how new calculations are performed moving forward.

Do I need to do anything?

No action is required from users. The system has been updated automatically.
