User Manual: Professional Browsing Experience

Welcome to the new and improved BudolShap category experience. We have upgraded our visual identity to provide a cleaner, more professional interface.

What Has Changed?

Modern Design

Replaced old emojis with sleek vector icons from the Lucide library.

Consistency

Icons now look the same whether you are on a phone, laptop, or tablet.

Clarity

Categories are easier to distinguish, helping you find products faster.

Navigation

You will see these new icons in several places:

  • Homepage: The "Shop by Category" section uses icons with distinct background colors.
  • Shop Page: The sidebar filter displays icons next to each category name.
  • Product Listings: Look for the small icon badges on product cards to identify their category at a glance.
