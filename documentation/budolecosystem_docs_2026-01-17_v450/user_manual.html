In this version, we've updated the visual indicators for our marketing ads to provide a more modern and responsive experience.

The dots at the bottom of the marketing ads screen now animate smoothly as you swipe through the offers. This helps you clearly see which ad you are currently viewing and how many more are available.

Responsive Design

The new indicators are designed to work perfectly on all screen sizes, ensuring a consistent experience whether you're on a mobile phone or a desktop browser.
