User Manual: KYC Data Privacy

We've updated the identity verification process to better protect your personal information.

What's New?

When you upload your ID documents for verification, our AI still works behind the scenes to extract the necessary details. However, to ensure your privacy, the raw results of this extraction are now handled in a confidential processing area.

What You'll See

  • Confidential Processing Badge: A new indicator confirming that your data is being handled securely.
  • Direct Submission: You no longer need to review the raw text extracted by the AI. This simplifies the process and reduces the time you spend on the verification screen.

Why the Change?

Your privacy is our priority. By hiding the raw AI output, we prevent sensitive details like your full name and ID number from being visible on-screen longer than necessary, reducing the risk of accidental exposure in public places.

Next Steps

Simply upload your ID and click "Submit for Verification". Our KYC officers will handle the rest!
