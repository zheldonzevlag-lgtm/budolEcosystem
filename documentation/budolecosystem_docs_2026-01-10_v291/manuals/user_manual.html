User Manual - v291 Updates

Login Loop Resolved

We have resolved the issue where users were being redirected back to the login screen or "Sign Modal" even after successfully logging into budolID. This fix applies to all platforms including web, mobile, and containerized environments.

What's Fixed:

The "Signature Verification Error" has been eliminated. Your login session from budolID is now correctly recognized by budolShap and budolPay services, regardless of how they are hosted.

Steps for Users

If you still experience issues, please follow these steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies for budolID and budolShap.
  • Go to the login page and sign in again.
  • You should now be redirected to the dashboard immediately without any error messages.

Need Help?

If the error persists, please contact your system administrator or take a screenshot of the server console logs for further investigation.
