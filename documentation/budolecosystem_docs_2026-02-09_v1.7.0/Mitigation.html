Risk Mitigation Strategy (v1.7.0)

M-SEC-01: Balanced Risk Scoring

Risk: False Positives blocking real users.

Mitigation: The AI Engine uses a weighted scoring system rather than binary "all or nothing" filters. A threshold of 70/100 ensures that a user must fail multiple patterns (e.g., disposable email AND suspicious name) before being blocked.

M-SEC-02: Human-in-the-Loop Override

Risk: Automated system making incorrect final decisions.

Mitigation: Every block is logged in the Forensic Audit Trail. Admins can review these blocks and manually whitelist identifiers if necessary, ensuring a safety net for legitimate users.

M-COMP-01: Compliance-First Logging

Risk: Regulatory audit failure.

Mitigation: Blocked events capture metadata required by BSP Circular 808, including the specific AI rationale. This provides full transparency for internal and external auditors.
