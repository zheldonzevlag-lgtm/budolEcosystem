Development Issues Log (v1.7.0)

I-1.7.0-01: Risk Score Bypass (Initial Implementation)

Description: The initial risk scoring logic was too lenient, allowing bots with simple numerical emails to register if they had valid-looking names.

Impact: Low (Detected during Phase 5 Testing).

I-1.7.0-02: Port Conflict (Auth Service)

Description: Port 8001 was held by a stale Node.js process during deployment, preventing the updated auth-service from starting.

Impact: Medium (Blocked testing for 15 minutes).

I-1.7.0-03: Case Sensitivity in Pattern Matching

Description: Name consistency checks (FirstName === LastName) were case-sensitive, missing bots using "bot" and "Bot".

Impact: Low (Caught during unit testing).
