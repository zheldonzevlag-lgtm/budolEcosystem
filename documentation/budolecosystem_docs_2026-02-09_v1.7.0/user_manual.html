User Guide: Account Registration

Welcome to BudolPay. To ensure the security of all our users, we have implemented advanced security measures for account creation.

Creating Your Account

Follow these best practices to ensure a smooth registration process:

  • Use a Valid Email: Avoid using temporary or disposable email addresses. These are blocked by our security system.
  • Real Name: Enter your legal first and last name as they appear on your government-issued ID.
  • Unique Password: Use a strong, unique password to protect your wallet.
Security Tip: Our system uses AI to detect and block suspicious registration patterns. If you receive a "Security Policy" error, please ensure you are using a personal email address and your correct name.

Need Help?

If you encounter issues during registration, please contact our support team with the error message provided.
