How to Remove Background from Variant Images

You can now easily remove the background from product variation images (e.g., different colors of a shirt) directly within the Variation Matrix.

Steps:

1. Upload an Image:

Upload an image for a variant as usual by clicking the image icon in the Variation Matrix table.

2. Hover over the Image:

Once the image is visible in the table cell, hover your mouse cursor over it. You will see an "Eye" icon appear in the center.

3. Open Preview:

Click on the "Eye" icon to open the Image Preview modal.

4. Remove Background:

In the modal, click the "Remove Background" button. Wait for a few seconds while the AI processes the image.

5. Verification:

Once completed, the button will change to "Background Removed" with a green checkmark, and the image will update automatically. You can then close the modal.

Tips:

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable as this feature requires uploading the image to the cloud.
  • The background removal works best on images with a clear subject and distinct background.
