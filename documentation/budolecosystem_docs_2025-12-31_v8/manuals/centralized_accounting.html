Centralized Accounting Strategy

A Centralized Accounting System is the only viable route for a multi-app ecosystem. It ensures financial integrity, prevents double-spending, and provides a "Source of Truth" for every cent moving under the Umbrella.

The Core Ledger (budolPay)

Instead of each app having its own "wallet" logic, they all report to the Core Ledger in budolPay. This uses Double-Entry Bookkeeping.

  • Chart of Accounts: A unified list of all accounts (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Income, Expenses).
  • Atomic Transactions: A purchase on budolShap triggers a single atomic event that debits the User Wallet and credits the Merchant Account.
  • Immutable History: Every financial move is recorded as a LedgerEntry that cannot be deleted or modified.

Transaction Flow: The Umbrella Model

1. [budolShap] → "User buys item for 100 PHP"
2. [budolAccounting] ← Validates funds in Core Ledger
3. ATOMIC ENTRY:
    - DEBIT User Wallet (Liability)   -100.00
    - CREDIT Merchant Wallet (Liability) +100.00
4. [budolPay] → Updates balances and notifies both parties

Why this is the Best Route:

  • Auditability: One single report can show the financial health of the entire ecosystem.
  • Reduced Complexity: budolShap and budoLoan don't need to worry about currency math or transaction security. They just "ask" the Accounting Block to move money.
  • Compliance: It's significantly easier to implement KYC/AML and tax reporting if all money flows through one central ledger.
Pro Tip: Think of the budolID as the Passport and the budolAccounting as the Central Bank of your Lego City.
