Checkout and Cart Management

Partial Payments

The checkout process has been improved to support partial payments. This means you can select specific items in your cart to purchase while leaving others for later.

How to Purchase Specific Items

  1. Go to your Cart.
  2. Select the checkbox next to the items you wish to purchase.
  3. Click the "Checkout" button.
  4. Review your order summary. Only the selected items will be listed.
  5. Complete the payment process.
Note: Items that you did not select will remain in your cart for future purchase. This allows you to manage your shopping list more effectively.

Account Management

Order History

You can view your past orders in the "My Orders" section. Each order will detail the specific items purchased, payment method, and status.
