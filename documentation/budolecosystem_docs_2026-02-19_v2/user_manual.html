Mobile Application: Editing Your Profile

You can now easily update your profile information in the BudolPay mobile app.

To edit your profile:

  1. Navigate to the Profile screen.
  2. Tap on the "Edit Profile" button.
  3. Wait for your profile information to load. If it doesn't appear immediately, please be patient as the app synchronizes with the server.
  4. You can edit your First Name, Last Name, and Email Address.
  5. Viewing Your Name: By default, your name is hidden for privacy. Tap the eye icon next to the name field to reveal your full name. Tap it again to hide it.
  6. Tap "Update" to save your changes.
Note: Changes to your legal name may require manual verification if your account is fully verified. Please contact support for assistance.
