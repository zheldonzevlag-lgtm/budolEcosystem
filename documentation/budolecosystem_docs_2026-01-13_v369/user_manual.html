Biometric Stability Update

This update includes critical fixes for the face registration process:

  • Improved Face Capture: Fixed an issue that prevented some devices from successfully saving your face profile.
  • Better Feedback: If your face isn't clearly visible, the app will now provide clearer instructions on how to fix it.
  • Optimized Performance: The login process is now smoother and more consistent across different lighting conditions.
