Getting Started

The latest version of budolPay Mobile brings improved stability and faster connection times to the ecosystem. Follow these steps to install the app on your Android device.

1

Download the APK

Locate the budolPay 1.2.6.apk file in the android-installer folder of your distribution package.

2

Enable Unknown Sources

If you are installing manually, your phone may ask for permission to "Install from Unknown Sources". Enable this in your Android settings to proceed.

3

Launch & Connect

Once installed, open budolPay. The app will automatically attempt to find your local payment gateway. If it cannot find it, you can manually enter your server's IP address in the settings.

What's New in 1.2.6

  • Faster Start: Refined network discovery logic reduces initial loading time.
  • Better Stability: Fixed several minor crashes related to socket reconnections.
  • Updated UI: Minor alignment fixes in the Transaction Details screen.
