User Manual v94: budolPay QR Scanning

QR Code Payment Workflow

  1. Open the budolPay app on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to the Payments section.
  3. Tap on Scan QR Code.
  4. Grant camera permissions when prompted.
  5. Scan the merchant's QR code to proceed with the payment.

Troubleshooting

  • Scanner not starting: Verify that no other app is using the camera.
  • Permission denied: Go to your phone's App Settings and manually enable Camera permission for budolPay.
