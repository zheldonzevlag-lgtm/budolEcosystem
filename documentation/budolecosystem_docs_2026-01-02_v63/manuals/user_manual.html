New Features

  • Mobile Wallet: Access your budolPay balance anywhere, anytime.
  • Scan to Pay: Use your phone's camera to pay instantly at budolShap merchants.
  • Quick Send: Instant money transfers to other budolEcosystem users.

Common Workflows

1. Logging In

Open the app and use your existing budolID credentials (email and password). If you don't have an account, you can create one via the registration link.

2. Making a Payment

From the home screen, tap the 'Scan QR' icon. Point your camera at the merchant's QR code, enter the amount, and confirm the payment.
