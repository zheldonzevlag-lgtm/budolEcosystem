What's New in Version 1.2.25

⭐ Fixed Favorites Management

We've fixed the issue where users were unable to add recipients to their favorites list. You can now easily save frequent recipients for quicker access.

💸 Quick Send from Favorites

Tapping on a favorite recipient in your list will now automatically take you to the Send Money screen with their information already filled in. No more re-typing emails or phone numbers!

How to Use Favorites

  1. Complete a transaction or view an existing one in your History.
  2. Tap on the transaction to see Details.
  3. Tap Add to Favorites to save the recipient.
  4. Access your saved recipients by tapping the Favorites icon on the Home screen.
  5. Tap any favorite to start a new transfer immediately.

Compliance Note

All transactions, including those initiated from favorites, remain subject to our security checks and BSP regulations. Ensure you verify the recipient's name before confirming any transfer.
