What Happens After Rejection?

If your KYC application is rejected by an administrator, your account status will revert to "Unverified". You will notice the following in the BudolPay app:

1. Status Check: Go to the "Settings" screen. The "Verify Account" option will be available again.
2. Re-submission: Tap on "Verify Account" to start the process again. You will need to upload new photos of your ID and document.

Tips for a Successful Application

  • Ensure your ID is not expired.
  • Take photos in a well-lit area without glare.
  • Ensure all text on the ID is clearly readable.
  • If the Admin panel displays "Extraction in progress", wait a few moments before checking your status again.

FAQs

Why was I rejected? Currently, the app does not show the specific reason. Common reasons include blurry photos or mismatched names. Please re-upload clear documents.

Can I still use the app? You can use basic features, but your transaction limits will remain restricted until your KYC is approved.
