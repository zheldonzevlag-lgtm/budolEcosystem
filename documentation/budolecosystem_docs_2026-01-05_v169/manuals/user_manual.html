User Manual: Wallet & KYC

Comprehensive guide for end-users on utilizing the BudolPay mobile financial ecosystem.

1. Purpose & Overview

Purpose: To provide users with a secure, intuitive interface for managing digital assets and verifying their identity to comply with financial regulations.

Explanation: This manual covers the two primary pillars of the v169 update: the Wallet Quick Actions (Cash In/Out) and the Tiered KYC (Know Your Customer) verification flow.

Reason: As BudolPay transitions from a prototype to a production-ready ecosystem, users require robust tools to move money and establish trust within the network.

2. Wallet Quick Actions

💰 Cash In (Add Money)

Top up your balance instantly.

  • Bank: Link your BPI/BDO account.
  • OTC: Pay at any 7-Eleven or Cebuana.
  • InstaPay: Receive from other banks.

💸 Cash Out

Withdraw your funds with ease.

  • e-Wallets: Send to GCash or Maya.
  • Bank: Direct to any local bank.
  • Fee: ₱15.00 flat rate per transaction.

3. Account Verification (KYC) Required for Limits

Our smart verification system uses AI to process your application in minutes.

  1. Step 1: Personal Details - Input your legal name and DOB.
  2. Step 2: ID Scan - Use the camera to scan a valid Gov't ID (UMID, Passport, Driver's License).
  3. Step 3: Selfie - A quick photo to verify your identity.
Level Requirement Monthly Limit
Basic Email Verified ₱10,000
Verified ID + Selfie ₱100,000

4. Troubleshooting (Issues & Fixes)

Issue Reason Fix
ID Scan Failed Blurry image or low light. Place ID on a flat surface with good lighting.
Face Not Detected Obstructions (glasses/masks). Remove obstructions and ensure face is within the frame.
Transaction Failed Insufficient balance or limit reached. Check your balance or complete KYC for higher limits.

5. Dependencies & Prerequisites

  • Mobile Device: Android 8.0+ or iOS 13.0+ required for ML Kit features.
  • Internet: Stable connection (LTE/Fiber) for real-time balance sync.
  • Permissions: Camera access for ID scans and Biometrics for quick login.

6. Risks & Mitigations

  • Risk: Account Takeover. Mitigation: Enforced Logout after inactivity and biometric confirmation.
  • Risk: Identity Theft. Mitigation: Multi-factor verification including ID OCR and Liveness detection.

For the best experience, we recommend:

  • Keep your app updated to the latest version (Current: 1.1.8).
