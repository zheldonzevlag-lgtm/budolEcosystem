New Feature: Tiered KYC Verification

We have introduced a tiered verification system to better track your account status and unlock higher transaction limits.

1. Basic Tier BASIC

This is the default status for all new accounts. You can perform limited transactions while you prepare your documents.

2. Semi-Verified Tier SEMI VERIFIED

Once you upload your identification documents through the mobile app, your account is automatically upgraded to Semi-Verified. This status remains while our administrators review your application.

3. Fully Verified Tier FULLY VERIFIED

After an administrator approves your KYC application, you become Fully Verified, unlocking all ecosystem features and maximum transaction limits.

Admin Interface Updates

Administrators can now see the current KYC Tier for every user in the User Management table. The tier is also displayed prominently when reviewing verification documents.
