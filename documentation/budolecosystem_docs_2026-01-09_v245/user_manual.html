User Manual: New Onboarding Experience

What's New?

In this version, we've introduced a quick introduction to the BudolEcosystem services right after you open the app. This helps you stay informed about all the features available to you.

1. Marketing Slides

After the splash screen, you will see three slides:

  • budolLoan: Learn about our quick loan options.
  • budolShap: Discover exclusive shopping deals within the ecosystem.
  • budolExpress: See how we handle your deliveries and remittances.

2. Navigation Controls

You can swipe between slides or use the NEXT button at the bottom. If you're in a hurry, you can use the Skip button at the top right to go straight to the login or home screen.

How to Get Started

  1. Open the budolPay app.
  2. Watch the splash screen finish loading.
  3. Browse through the marketing slides to learn about our services.
  4. Tap GET STARTED on the last slide to enter the app.
