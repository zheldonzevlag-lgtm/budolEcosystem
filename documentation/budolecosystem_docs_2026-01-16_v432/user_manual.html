User Manual - v432

What's New

In this version, we have updated our branding within the app. All mentions of our service name in transaction descriptions have been updated to a new, modern look.

Branding Update

You will now see budolPay instead of BUDOL_PAY in your:

  • Home Screen (Recent Transactions)
  • Transaction History
  • Transaction Details

This change is purely visual and does not affect your transactions or account security.

Support

If you have any questions about this update, please contact our support team through the Help section in the app.
