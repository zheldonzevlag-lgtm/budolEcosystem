User Manual: Real-time BudolPay

Guide for end-users on the latest real-time features in BudolPay Mobile.

1. What's New in v409?

Real-time Transaction Updates

No more pulling to refresh! Your transaction history now updates automatically as soon as a payment is completed. Whether you send money or receive it, you'll see the change instantly.

Enhanced Logout Security

Every time you log out, we securely record the event to ensure your account remains protected. If you notice any suspicious activity, our support team can verify the exact time of your last session.

2. Sending Money to Favorites

Sending money to your favorite recipients is now faster and more reliable.

  1. Go to the Send Money screen.
  2. Select a recipient from your Favorites list.
  3. Enter the amount and confirm.
  4. The transaction will appear in your History immediately after completion—no refresh required!

3. Security Best Practices

  • Always log out when using BudolPay on a shared device.
  • Enable biometric authentication for faster and more secure access.
  • Monitor your real-time transaction history for any unauthorized activity.
