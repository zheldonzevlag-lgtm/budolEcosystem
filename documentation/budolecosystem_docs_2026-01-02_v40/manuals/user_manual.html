User Manual: New budolID Login Experience

Version: v40.0.0 | Date: 2026-01-02

What's New?

We've redesigned the budolID login portal to provide a more secure and modern experience when accessing the budolEcosystem.

1. Secure Shield Branding

A new Blue Shield Icon is now prominently displayed at the top of the login form. This icon signifies that you are on the official, secure budolID authentication portal.

2. Improved Clarity

The form now features clearer labels and a cleaner layout, making it easier to see where to enter your credentials. The input fields are larger and provide visual feedback when selected.

How to use:

  1. Click "Login with budolID" from any application in the ecosystem.
  2. You will be redirected to the new, blue-branded login page.
  3. Enter your registered Email Address and Password.
  4. Click "Sign In" to be securely logged in and redirected back to your application.
