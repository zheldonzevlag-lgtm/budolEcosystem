User Manual (v3.4.0)

Effective Date: 2026-02-16

New Feature: Enhanced Shopping Cart

This update brings improved handling of product variations (sizes, colors) and keeps your cart saved even if you close the browser or switch devices (when logged in).

1. Adding Products to Cart

Products with Variations (e.g., Clothing, Gadgets)

Step 1: Navigate to a product page.
Step 2: You must select all required options (e.g., Color and Size). The "Add to Cart" button will remain disabled until you make these selections.
Step 3: Click "Add to Cart". A confirmation message will appear, and the cart badge in the navigation bar will update immediately.

Standard Products

For products without variations, simply click the "Add to Cart" button. No extra selection is needed.

2. Managing Your Cart

Click the Cart icon in the top right corner to view your items.

  • Adjust Quantity: Use the (+) and (-) buttons to change how many items you want. The total price updates automatically.
  • Remove Items: Click the trash bin icon or "Delete" button to remove a specific item.
  • Selection for Checkout: Use the checkboxes next to each item to select which ones you want to buy now. You can select items with different variations of the same product independently.
Note: If you are logged in, your cart is saved to your account. You can access it from any device. If you are a guest, your cart is saved on your current device.

3. Checkout Process

Step 1: Select the items you wish to purchase in the Cart page.
Step 2: Click "Checkout".
Step 3: Review your Order Summary. It will clearly list the specific variations (e.g., "T-Shirt - Red, Large") you selected.
Step 4: Proceed with payment and shipping details.

Troubleshooting

Issue: My cart items disappeared.

Solution: Ensure you are logged in. If you were browsing as a guest, your cart is stored in your browser. Clearing browser cache might remove guest carts.

Issue: "Add to Cart" is grayed out.

Solution: Check if the product has variations (Size/Color) that need to be selected. Also, check if the item is out of stock.
