New Checkout Flow

We have improved how you access your account during checkout. If you are not signed in when you click Place Order, you will now be automatically taken to the Login page.

Steps:

  1. Add items to your cart.
  2. Proceed to the My Cart page.
  3. Click Place Order.
  4. If not logged in, you will be redirected to the Login page.
  5. Enter your credentials or use budolID to sign in.
  6. After a successful login, you will be automatically returned to your cart to complete your purchase.

Benefits

This change ensures that you don't lose your progress and makes it easier to complete your orders securely.
