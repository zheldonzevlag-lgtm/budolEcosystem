What's New in v263?

Faster Transfer Confirmation

We've streamlined the "Send Money" experience. Instead of a popup window that you have to close, you will now see a sleek notification bar at the bottom of your screen immediately after a successful transfer.

How to read the new Confirmation

After you tap Send Now and the transaction completes:

  1. You will be automatically returned to your Home or Favorites screen.
  2. A green notification bar will appear at the bottom for 4 seconds.
  3. The notification includes:
    • Transfer Successful title.
    • The exact amount sent (e.g., ₱100.00).
    • The recipient's name.
    • A Reference Number for your records.

Note: If you need to keep a record of the reference number, we recommend taking a screenshot while the notification is visible, or checking your Transaction History.
