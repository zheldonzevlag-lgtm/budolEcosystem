PIN Reset Notification

Your security PIN has been reset to allow you to choose a new one. This ensures you can access your account with a PIN you recognize.

How to Set Your New PIN

  1. Go back to the Phone Number screen.
  2. Enter your mobile number.
  3. Check your terminal logs for a new 6-digit OTP.
  4. Enter the OTP.
  5. You will be prompted to Create your 6-digit PIN. Enter your desired PIN here.
  6. Confirm and log in.
