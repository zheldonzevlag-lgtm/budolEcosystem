For Buyers: Tracking Your Payment

Once you complete a payment via QR or budolPay, your order will automatically move from the "To Pay" tab to the "To Ship" tab. You no longer need to refresh the page manually.

For Sellers: Managing Paid Orders

Orders will now appear consistently in your "To Ship" tab as soon as payment is confirmed. You can then proceed with booking shipping for these orders immediately.
