Issue #1: Paid Orders Hidden from "To Ship" Tab

Description: Users reported that after paying via budolPay, orders remained in the "All" tab and did not appear in the "To Ship" tab until a manual refresh or sometimes not at all.

Root Cause: Status mismatch between PAID (set by webhooks) and PROCESSING (filtered by the UI) + Missing realtime sync for buyers.

Issue #2: Shipping Service Inconsistency

Description: Some orders marked as PROCESSING were not appearing in the "Orders Needing Booking" view for sellers.

Root Cause: shippingService.js was hardcoded to look for status: 'PAID' only.
