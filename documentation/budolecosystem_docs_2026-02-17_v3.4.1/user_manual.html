User Manual (v3.4.1)

Welcome to the updated Budol Ecosystem. This release focuses on improving your shopping experience, specifically making the checkout process smoother and more reliable.

1. Shopping & Checkout

Adding Items to Cart

When you add an item, you can select specific variations (e.g., Size, Color). These choices are now tracked more precisely in your cart.

Payment with GCash / Maya / GrabPay

  1. Proceed to Checkout and select your preferred e-wallet.
  2. You will be redirected to the secure payment page.
  3. Complete the payment on your phone or browser.
  4. Important: After payment, wait to be redirected back to our store. However, even if you close the window early, our system now automatically confirms your order and updates your cart.
Did you know? Your cart will now automatically remove only the items you successfully paid for. If you have other items in your cart (like a different color of the same shirt), they will remain there for your next purchase.

2. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I paid for my order, but the items are still in my cart. What should I do?

A: This should be resolved in the new update. If it happens, try refreshing the page. If they persist, please contact support with your Order ID. You do not need to pay again.

Q: Can I cancel an order after paying?

A: Once paid, an order is processed immediately. You can request a cancellation or refund through the "My Orders" page if the item has not yet been shipped.

4. System Requirements

To ensure the best shopping experience, please use:

  • Internet Connection: Stable broadband or mobile data (4G/5G).
  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge.
  • Payment Apps: Updated GCash, Maya, or GrabPay apps for seamless payment.

5. Data Privacy & Security

We value your privacy. Your cart and order data are processed securely. We do not store your payment credentials (credit card numbers or MPINs). All payments are handled by our secure partner, PayMongo.
