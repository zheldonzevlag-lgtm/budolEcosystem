Known Issues (v3.4.1)

A list of reported issues, their status, and impact on the current release.

Resolved: Cart Persistence After Payment

Description: Users reported that items remained in their cart even after a successful payment via GCash/PayMongo.

Root Cause: The initial cart clearing logic in createOrder was unreliable for async payments, and the webhook handler had cart clearing disabled.

Status: Fixed in v3.4.1
Open: Webhook Latency

Description: In rare cases, the PayMongo webhook might be delayed by several minutes, meaning the cart items remain visible until the webhook arrives.

Impact: User might try to pay again (though the system prevents double payment for the same order ID).

Status: Monitoring / Acceptable
Open: Abandoned Cart Cleanup

Description: There is no automated process to clear items from carts that have been abandoned for long periods (e.g., > 30 days).

Status: Scheduled for v3.5.0
