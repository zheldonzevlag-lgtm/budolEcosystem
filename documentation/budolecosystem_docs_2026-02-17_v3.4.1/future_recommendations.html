Future Recommendations (v3.4.1)

Proposed enhancements to further improve the system based on findings from this release.

High Priority

Implement Webhook Retry Logic

Rationale: Currently, if a webhook fails, there is no automatic retry mechanism on our end (though PayMongo might retry). We should log failed webhooks and have a scheduled job to re-process them.

Medium Priority

"Save for Later" Feature

Rationale: Allow users to move items out of the active cart without deleting them entirely, reducing cart abandonment friction.

Medium Priority

Advanced Stock Reservation

Rationale: Reserve stock for a short period (e.g., 10 mins) when a user enters the payment gateway to prevent overselling during high traffic.
