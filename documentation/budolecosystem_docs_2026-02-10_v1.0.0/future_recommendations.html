Future Enhancements & Recommendations

1. Real-time Fraud Detection

Implement a fraud detection layer that analyzes transaction velocity and geographic patterns to flag suspicious merchant payments.

2. Biometric Confirmation for QR

Integrate FaceID/Fingerprint confirmation in the BudolPay app before finalizing the /process-qr call to ensure the physical owner is authorizing the debit.

3. Signed QR Payloads

Transition from plain JSON QR data to JWS (JSON Web Signatures) for the QR payloads, ensuring that the QR data itself cannot be generated by unauthorized parties.
