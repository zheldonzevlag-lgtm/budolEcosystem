New Branded Order Numbers & Descriptions

What's New?

We've updated how order numbers and payment names appear in the app to make them easier to recognize and more consistent with our brand.

Old Appearance

Order #BUDOL_PAY_12345

New Branded Appearance

Order #budolPay_12345

Where will I see this?

1. Order Headers

At the top of your order details page on budolShap, your order number now uses the signature rose red "Pay" branding.

2. Payment Instructions

When you choose to pay via QR, the instructions will now clearly state: "Use your budolPay app".

3. Transaction History

In your budolPay mobile app, your transaction titles and descriptions will now show the branded names instead of plain text codes.
