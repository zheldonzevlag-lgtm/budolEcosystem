New Features & Improvements

Improved Recipient Selection

When you select a recipient from your Favorites list, the app will now automatically use their phone number to fill in the "Email or Phone Number" field. If the recipient doesn't have a phone number saved, their email address will be used instead.

How to use Favorites

  1. Open the Favorites screen from the home dashboard.
  2. Tap on a favorite recipient's name or the Send icon.
  3. You will be taken to the Send Money screen.
  4. Notice that the "Email or Phone Number" field is now pre-filled with the recipient's phone number.
  5. Enter the amount and message, then tap Send Now.

Version Check

You can verify you are on the latest version by looking at the splash screen when opening the app. It should display v1.2.26.
