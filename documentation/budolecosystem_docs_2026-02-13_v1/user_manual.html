User Manual: Managing Product Variations

For Sellers

1 Go to "Add Product" or "Manage Product" and click edit.

2 Scroll down to the "Product Variations" section.

3 Add a Variation Tier (e.g., Color). Add options like "Red", "Blue".

4 Add another tier if needed (e.g., Size). Add options like "S", "M", "L".

5 The system will automatically generate a table below. Fill in the price and stock for each combination.

6 Click "Save Product".

For Customers

When viewing a product with variations:

  • Select one option from each variation group.
  • The price and stock status will update automatically based on your selection.
  • The "Add to Cart" button will remain disabled until a complete selection is made.
