Future Recommendations

1. Variation-Specific Images

Allow sellers to upload different images for each variation (e.g., show the "Red" version when the "Red" variation is selected). This will require updating the variation_matrix to store image URLs.

2. Bulk Pricing

Integrate wholesale pricing based on variation quantities. Currently, the price is fixed per variation combination regardless of quantity.

3. Inventory Alerts

Implement low-stock alerts specifically for individual variations, as some combinations may sell out faster than others.
