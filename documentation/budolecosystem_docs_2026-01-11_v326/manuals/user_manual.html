Fixing Biometric Login Issues

If you tap the fingerprint icon and nothing happens, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Update your App

Ensure you are running version 1.2.50. You can see the version number at the bottom of the login screen. If you are on an older version (like 1.2.45), please download and install the latest APK.

Step 2: Check Phone Settings

Make sure you have registered at least one fingerprint or face in your phone's Settings > Security. The app cannot use biometrics if they are not set up on your device first.

Step 3: Enable in budolPay

Biometric login must be enabled inside the app after your first successful login:

  1. Login using your mobile number and 6-digit PIN.
  2. Go to Settings (Gear icon in top right of Home screen).
  3. Turn on the Biometric Login toggle.
