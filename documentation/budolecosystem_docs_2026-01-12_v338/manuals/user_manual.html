NEW SECURITY ENHANCEMENT

Securing Your BudolPay Experience

In version 1.2.57 (Documentation v337), we've introduced bank-grade communication security to protect your transactions and personal data.

Anti-Interception

Advanced technology prevents hackers from intercepting your data, even on public Wi-Fi networks.

Verified Servers

The app now strictly verifies that it is talking to the official BudolPay servers before sending any data.

Data Privacy

Enhanced encryption ensures your KYC documents and personal info remain private during upload.

What do I need to do?

Nothing! These security features work automatically in the background. Simply ensure you keep your app updated to the latest version to maintain the highest level of security.
