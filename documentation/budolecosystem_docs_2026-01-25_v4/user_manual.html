QR Payment Flow

The QR payment flow has been updated to provide better clarity for your transactions.

1. Generating the QR Code

When you proceed to checkout or view an order's payment status, a QR code will be displayed.

2. Identifying your Order

In the payment modal, you will now see the Order ID displayed prominently above the Reference ID. This helps you confirm that the payment is for the correct purchase.

3. Mobile App Usage

The latest version of the budolPay mobile app (v1.3.34) is now available. Use this app to scan and process your QR payments securely.

  • Open the app and log in.
  • Select the 'Scan QR' option.
  • Point your camera at the QR code displayed on the screen.
  • Confirm the payment details.
