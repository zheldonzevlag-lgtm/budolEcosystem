User Manual - v435

For Buyers

1. Placing an Order

Browse products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose between COD (Cash on Delivery), GCash, or Budol Pay.

2. Tracking Orders

You will receive real-time notifications when your order status changes (e.g., from ORDER_PLACED to SHIPPED). You can view your orders in the "My Orders" section.

For Sellers

1. Managing Orders

New orders will appear in your Seller Dashboard instantly. You can update the status of an order as you process it.

2. COD Payments

For COD orders, the payment is automatically marked as paid once you set the status to DELIVERED. The funds (minus the 5% platform fee) will be credited to your pending wallet balance automatically.

Support

If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact the store owner or our customer support team.
