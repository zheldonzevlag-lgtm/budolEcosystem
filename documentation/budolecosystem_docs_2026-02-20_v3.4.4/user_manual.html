User Manual

Guide for end-users regarding checkout changes.

Cart Behavior

When you place an order using any payment method (including GCash, Maya, etc.), the items you ordered will be immediately removed from your shopping cart.

This helps you keep track of what you still need to buy versus what you have already ordered.

Payment Failure

If your payment fails or you cancel the payment process, don't worry! Your order is saved in "My Orders" with a status of "Pending Payment". You can go to your order history to retry the payment without needing to add items to your cart again.

Multi-Store Orders

You can order items from multiple stores in a single checkout using Standard Delivery. However, "Same Day Delivery" (Lalamove) is restricted to single-store orders only due to logistical constraints.
