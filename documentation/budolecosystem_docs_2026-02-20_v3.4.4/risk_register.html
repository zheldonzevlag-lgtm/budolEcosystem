Risk Register

Identified risks associated with the changes in v3.4.4.

Risk ID Description Severity Likelihood Impact
R-344-01 User confusion due to cart being emptied after payment cancellation Medium High User might think they lost their items if they cancel payment.
R-344-02 Race condition if order creation fails but cart is cleared (unlikely as clear happens inside success block) Low Low Loss of cart data without order record.
