What's New?

Improved Transaction History

The "Recent Transactions" section on your Home screen now correctly displays your latest activity. Whether you've just cashed in or sent money to a friend, your transactions will appear instantly.

Fixes & Improvements

  • Resolved "No recent transactions" bug: Fixed an issue where the Home screen would show "No recent transactions" even if you had successful transfers.
  • Faster Loading: Optimized how transaction data is fetched to ensure your Home screen loads smoothly.
  • Accurate Amounts: Improved how the app reads transaction amounts to ensure your history always matches your wallet balance.

How to View History

  1. Open the budolPay app.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Scroll down to the Recent Transactions section on the Home screen.
  4. Your last 5 transactions will be listed. To see more, tap the See All button (Coming soon!).

Troubleshooting

If you still don't see your transactions:

  • Pull down on the Home screen to refresh the data.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Check the connection indicator (green dot) at the top of the screen to ensure you are connected to the budolPay network.
