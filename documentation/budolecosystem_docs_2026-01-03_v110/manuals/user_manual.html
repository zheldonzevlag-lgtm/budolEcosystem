User Manual - v110

What's New in v110?

This update connects your budolPay mobile app directly to our live banking and payment systems. You can now see your real balance and pay at merchants using QR codes.

Using the App

1. Logging In

Use your registered email and password to log in. The app now securely connects to our central authentication system.

2. Checking Your Balance

Your current balance is displayed on the Home screen. You can pull down to refresh the balance at any time.

3. Making a Payment

  1. Tap the Scan QR button or the center QR icon in the navigation bar.
  2. Point your camera at a budolPay QR code (usually found on checkout pages).
  3. Review the payment details (Merchant name and Amount).
  4. Tap CONFIRM PAYMENT.
  5. Wait for the "Payment Successful" confirmation. Your balance will be updated automatically.

Security Tips

  • Always log out if you are using a shared device.
  • Only scan QR codes from trusted sources.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection before confirming payments.
