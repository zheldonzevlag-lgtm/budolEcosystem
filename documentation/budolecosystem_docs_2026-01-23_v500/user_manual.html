Real-time Delivery Updates

Your order status will now update to "Delivered" almost immediately after the rider completes the delivery in the Lalamove app. This ensures you can confirm receipt and release escrow funds without delay.

What's New?

  • Improved accuracy of "Delivered" status.
  • Automatic order completion timer starts immediately upon delivery.
  • Better visibility for sellers on when their items are received.
