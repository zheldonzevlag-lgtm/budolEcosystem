User Manual: What's New in v1.3.58

1. Login with Email or Phone

You can now use your registered email address or your mobile number to log in to the Budol Ecosystem. The system will automatically detect which one you are using.

How to use:
  1. Open the BudolPay app or go to the BudolShap website.
  2. In the login field, enter either your Email (e.g., user@example.com) or your Phone Number (e.g., 09123456789).
  3. Proceed with your Password or Biometric authentication as usual.

2. Real-time Account Verification

When creating a new account or logging in, the system now checks if your email or phone number is valid and registered in real-time.

  • Green Check: The identifier is valid and ready to use.
  • Red Error: The identifier is either already taken (during registration) or not found (during login).

3. Security Improvements

All login attempts are now monitored more closely to protect your account from unauthorized access. If you use a new device, you will still be required to verify via OTP for your safety.
