Issues & Fixes (v1.3.81)

Issue: Transactions of ₱1,000+ failing

Cause: CurrencyInputFormatter adds commas, which double.tryParse cannot handle.

Fix: Switched to AmountUtils.parse which strips non-numeric characters (except decimals).

Issue: "Recipient not found" for numbers starting with 09

Cause: Numbers were sent as 09123456789 but DB stores +639123456789.

Fix: Added phone normalization to the transfer initiation flow.
