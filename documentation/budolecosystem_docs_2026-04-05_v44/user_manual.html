User Manual (v1.3.81)

Sending Amounts >= 1,000

With v1.3.81, you can now safely send amounts of 1,000 Pesos or more. The app will automatically format your input with commas (e.g., 1,234.56), and these commas will be correctly processed during the transaction.

Entering Phone Numbers

You can enter phone numbers in both local format (09XX-XXX-XXXX) and international format (+639XX...). The app will automatically ensure the number is correctly identified for the recipient.
