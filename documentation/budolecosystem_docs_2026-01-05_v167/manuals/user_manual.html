What's New in v1.1.7

The latest version of budolPay introduces enhanced transparency for your transactions.

1. Full Transaction History

Access your complete history by tapping the History tab in the bottom navigation or the See All button on the Home screen. You can now see all your past payments and received funds in one place.

2. Transaction Details View

Curious about a specific transaction? Simply tap on any item in your history or recent transactions list. You will see a detailed breakdown including:

  • Transaction ID: A unique code for every transaction.
  • Reference No: For easy tracking.
  • Date and Time: Exactly when the transaction happened.
  • Sender/Receiver Info: Know exactly who the money went to or came from.

Basic Navigation Guide

  1. Home: View your balance and recent transactions.
  2. History: View your full transaction list.
  3. Scan: Quickly pay via QR code.
  4. Wallet/Settings: Manage your account and app preferences.

Troubleshooting

If you see "No recent transactions," ensure you have an active internet connection and try pulling down to refresh the list.
