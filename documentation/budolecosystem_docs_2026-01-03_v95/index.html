Documentation v95: Database Connectivity & Ecosystem Recovery

Published: 2026-01-03 | Version: 95 Resolved

Purpose

This version documents the resolution of critical database connection failures in the budolID service and the subsequent recovery of the entire budolEcosystem central runner.

Problem Statement

The budolID (SSO) service was unable to connect to the PostgreSQL database, causing the ecosystem startup to fail. Additionally, stale processes were occupying the required service ports (8000, 3001), preventing a clean restart.

Implementation Highlights

  • Service Recovery: Restarted the postgresql-x64-18 Windows service.
  • Process Management: Identified and terminated conflicting processes on ports 3000, 3001, and 8000.
  • Verification: Confirmed DB access via check-apps.js and successfully launched start-ecosystem.js.
