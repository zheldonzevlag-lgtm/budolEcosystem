Technical Explanation

Added the budolAkawntng slide to the marketing carousel. This involved:

  • Updating the _slides list in MarketingAdsScreen.
  • Expanding the RegExp in _buildDescription to recognize "Akawntng" as a branded suffix.
  • Defining specific color mapping (Slate for "budol", Purple for "Akawntng") in the rendering logic.
