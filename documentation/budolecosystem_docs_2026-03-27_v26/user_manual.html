Budol Ecosystem - User Security Guide (v26)

What is the Budol Shield?

To protect your account from bots and unauthorized login attempts, we have introduced a simple "Shield Challenge" during Sign In and Registration.

How to use it:

  1. When you open the Login or Create Account screen, you will see a simple math problem (e.g., 4 + 3).
  2. Enter the correct answer in the box provided.
  3. Click Verify & Proceed.
  4. Your normal login fields (Email/Password) will then appear.

This challenge ensures that only human users can access our secure ecosystem.
