User Manual: KYC Verification with AI Smart Crop

Verifying your account is now easier than ever with our automated image processing system.

1 Choose Your Tier

Select the account type that matches your needs (Buyer, Individual Seller, or Business Seller).

2 Upload Your ID Card

Take a clear photo of your Government ID. Our AI will automatically:

  • Remove the background: To focus only on the ID card.
  • Crop the image: To make the text larger and easier to read.
  • Extract your details: Your name and ID number will be automatically filled for review.

Note: If the background is too busy, the system will use the original photo to ensure no data is lost.

3 Complete Liveness Check

Follow the on-screen instructions to perform a quick facial scan. This confirms that the person uploading the ID is the owner of the account.

4 Review & Submit

Check the extracted details. If everything looks correct, click submit. Your application will be reviewed by an administrator within 24-48 hours.
