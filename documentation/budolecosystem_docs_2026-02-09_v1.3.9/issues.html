Known Issues: v1.3.9

The following issues are known but unresolved in this release.

I-AI-01: Low Lighting Detection

In extremely low lighting, the smart crop utility may fail to distinguish the ID from the background, defaulting to the original photo. Users are advised to use a well-lit area.

I-OCR-02: Glossy ID Reflections

Flash reflections on glossy IDs can obscure text, leading to partial extraction failures. The system will mark these as "Manual Entry Required."
