User Manual - v194

New Automated Restrictions

To keep your account safe and comply with financial regulations, we've updated how basic accounts work. If you haven't verified your identity yet, you might see the following messages when using the app.

1. Sending Money

If you try to send money to a friend while your account is unverified, you will receive this error:

Verification required: BASIC accounts cannot send money P2P. Please upgrade to FULLY VERIFIED.

Solution: Complete your KYC verification in the Profile section to unlock Send Money.

2. Adding Funds (Cash In)

Basic accounts have a maximum limit of ₱5,000. If you try to add more than this, you will see:

Limit Exceeded: BASIC accounts have a maximum wallet balance of ₱5,000.

Solution: Spend some of your balance first, or verify your account to increase your limit to ₱500,000.
