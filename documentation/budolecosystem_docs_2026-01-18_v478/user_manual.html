User Manual - Version 478

What's New

Improved Biometric Login

We've updated the biometric login feature to be more responsive. If you enable or disable fingerprints/face recognition in your phone settings while the app is open, budolPay will now automatically detect the change when you return to the login screen.

How to Use

Enabling Biometric Login

  1. Open the budolPay app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Toggle the Biometric Login switch.
  4. Verify your identity using your fingerprint or face.

Troubleshooting

If the biometric option is not showing on the login screen, please ensure that:

  • Your device supports biometric authentication.
  • You have enrolled at least one fingerprint or face in your device settings.
  • You have not recently failed multiple biometric attempts (which may temporarily lock the feature).
