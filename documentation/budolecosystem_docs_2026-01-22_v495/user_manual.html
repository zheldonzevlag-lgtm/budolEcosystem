User Manual: Realtime Experience

How the realtime engine enhances your experience in the Budol Ecosystem.

Seamless Updates

You no longer need to manually refresh your browser to see the latest order status or notifications. The Budol Ecosystem uses advanced technology to push updates to your screen instantly.

  • Order Sync: When a seller updates your order status, it will automatically move to the correct tab in your order history.
  • Instant Notifications: Receive immediate alerts for status changes, new messages, or important system updates.
  • Dynamic Content: Prices, availability, and other critical data stay fresh without interruption.

What to expect

Depending on the system settings managed by the administrator, you might experience slightly different update speeds:

  • High Speed: Instantaneous updates with minimal battery impact.
  • Standard: Updates arrive every few seconds.

In all cases, the system is designed to keep you informed without requiring any action on your part.
