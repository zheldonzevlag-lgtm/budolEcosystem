What's New for Users?

1. Smoother Page Transitions

When you visit the Shop or Home page, products now "float" into view gracefully. This makes the shopping experience feel more high-end and responsive.

2. Smarter Loading States

Instead of seeing blank spaces or spinning icons while waiting for products to load, you'll see "Skeleton" placeholders that give you a preview of the layout. This reduces visual clutter during slow internet connections.

3. Improved Category Browsing

Categories have been refreshed with more intuitive icons:

  • Fashion: Now easily identifiable with a Shirt icon.
  • Toys & Games: Now uses a Controller icon for better clarity.

4. Visual Polish

Titles like "Shop by Category" are now easier to read and the overall interface has been decluttered by removing unnecessary borders on hover selection.
