The New budolPay Look

In this update, we've refreshed how our brand names appear in the app. You'll notice a more consistent and modern look for all Budol services.

Visual Example:

Old: BUDOL_PAY

New: budolPay

Where will I see this?

  • Transaction History: All your payments will now clearly show "budolPay".
  • Wallet Screens: Your balance and cash-in options will use the new branding.
  • Order Status: On the web, your payment methods will be clearly labeled.
