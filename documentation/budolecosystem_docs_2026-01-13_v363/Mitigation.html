Mitigation & Compliance Plan

Cybersecurity Law & DPA Compliance

The 1.2.66 build implements the following mitigations to ensure compliance with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) and BSP standards:

  • Data Minimization: Raw images are not stored; only 128-dimensional mathematical embeddings are persisted.
  • Hardware-Backed Security: Face templates are stored in the device's Secure Enclave/Keystore via FlutterSecureStorage.
  • Encryption at Rest: All local database files are encrypted using AES-256.
