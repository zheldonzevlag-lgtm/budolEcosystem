User Manual - Paying with QR Code (v487)

We've updated the payment experience to make it faster and easier. You no longer have to leave the page to complete your QR payment.

How to Pay

1 Place Your Order

Add items to your cart and proceed to the Checkout page. Choose your preferred QR payment method (e.g., GCash, Maya, or QRPh) and click "Place Order".

2 Scan the QR Code

A payment window will pop up instantly on your screen. Use your mobile banking or wallet app to scan the QR code displayed.

Note: You have 10 minutes to complete the payment before the QR code expires.

3 Wait for Confirmation

Once you've paid on your phone, wait a few seconds. The payment window will automatically close, and you'll see a success message.

Important Tips

  • Don't close the window: Closing the payment window manually might delay the processing of your order.
  • Check your connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection for the fastest confirmation.
  • Pay Now button: If you missed the payment during checkout, you can always go to your Orders page and click the "Pay Now" button to see the QR code again.
