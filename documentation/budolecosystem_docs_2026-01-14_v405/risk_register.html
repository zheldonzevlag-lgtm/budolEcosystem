Risk Register & Mitigations (v405)

Risk ID Description Impact Probability Mitigation Strategy
R-405-01 Dynamic health checks may increase dashboard load time if services are slow to respond. Medium Medium Implemented { next: { revalidate: 30 } } in fetch requests to cache health status for 30 seconds.
R-405-02 Service URLs in .env may be incorrect or missing, leading to 0% uptime reporting. High Low Added fallback URLs (localhost) and error logging to identify connectivity issues.
R-405-03 Inconsistent ledger entry creation leads to "PHP 0" Transaction Volume despite actual activity. High High Enforce Double-Entry Bookkeeping at the service level; all transactions must create ledger entries in ASSET/LIABILITY accounts.
