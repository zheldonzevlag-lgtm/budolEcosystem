Logging in to BudolShap

We've updated our login screen to give you more flexibility. You can now choose to log in using your registered email address or your mobile number.

Step 1: Choose Your Method

At the top of the login form, you'll see a toggle switch. Click on "EMAIL" if you want to log in with your email address, or "MOBILE" if you prefer to use your phone number.

Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

Depending on your choice, enter either your full email address or your mobile number (starting with 9, e.g., 917 123 4567). Then, enter your secure password.

Step 3: Secure Login

Click the "SIGN IN" button to access your account. The system will securely verify your identity using the method you selected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both methods?

Yes! As long as you have registered both an email address and a mobile number to your account, you can use either one to log in at any time.

What format should I use for my mobile number?

Simply enter the 10 digits of your number (e.g., 912 345 6789). You don't need to add the "0" at the beginning or the "+63" prefix, as the system handles that for you.
