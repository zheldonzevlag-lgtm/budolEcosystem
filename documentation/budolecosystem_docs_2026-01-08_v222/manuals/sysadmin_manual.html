SysAdmin Manual: Manual Verification Overrides

1. Overriding KYC Status

In cases where automated KYC fails or manual verification is required for test accounts, the upgrade-jon-pay.js script serves as a template for administrative overrides.

2. User Identity Integrity

Always verify the user's presence in both budolID and budolPay before attempting an upgrade. Discrepancies between the two can lead to "unverified" prompts in the application despite a "verified" status in one database.

3. Service Restarts

Verification status is often cached in JWT tokens. Users may need to log out and log back in to see their upgraded status reflected in the UI.
