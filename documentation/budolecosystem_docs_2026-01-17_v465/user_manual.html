BudolPay Security Guide

Your security is our priority. v465 introduces advanced protection features.

What's New in v465?

🛡️ Automatic Session Lock

If you leave the app for more than 30 seconds (e.g., to answer a call or check a message), BudolPay will automatically lock your session. You will need to use your Face ID, Fingerprint, or Phone PIN to get back in.

⏳ Idle Protection

If you don't use the app for 5 minutes while it's open, you will be automatically logged out. This protects your account if you leave your phone unattended.

📸 Anti-Screen Capture

To prevent sensitive information from being stolen, BudolPay now blocks screenshots and screen recordings on most screens. If you try to take a screenshot, you may see a "Could not capture screenshot" message or a black image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I keep getting logged out?

This is a security feature required by banking regulations (BSP). It ensures that even if your phone is lost or stolen while the app is open, your funds remain secure.

What if my biometrics (Face ID/Fingerprint) don't work?

Don't worry! You can always tap "Log Out" on the lock screen and sign back in using your email and password. Alternatively, the app will fall back to your device's security (Pattern/PIN) if biometrics are unavailable.

Need help? Contact BudolPay support via the settings menu.
