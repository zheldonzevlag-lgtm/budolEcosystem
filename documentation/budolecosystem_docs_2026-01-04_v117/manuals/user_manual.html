Paying with BudolPay

BudolPay allows you to pay for your orders using a QR code generated specifically for your transaction.

How to Pay

1 Select BudolPay: In the checkout page, choose "BudolPay" as your payment method.
2 Place Order: Click "Place Order". You will be redirected to a dedicated Payment QR page.
3 Scan QR Code: A QR code will be displayed. Scan this code with your mobile banking app or payment wallet.
4 Wait for Confirmation: After paying, stay on the page. The system will automatically detect your payment within a few seconds and redirect you to the success page.
Note: If you accidentally close the page, you can go to your "Orders" tab, find the order in the "To Pay" section, and click "Pay Now" to retrieve the QR code.

Troubleshooting

"Payment Data Missing" Error

If you see a screen saying "Payment Data Missing", it means the system couldn't retrieve the details for your QR code. Click the "Go to Orders" button and try initiating the payment again from your Order History.

QR Code Not Loading

If the QR code image is broken or doesn't appear, check your internet connection and refresh the page. If the issue persists, try choosing a different payment method like COD (Cash on Delivery) if available.
