Mitigation Strategies - v418

  • Risk R001 (Schema Sync): Use npx prisma db push regularly during development. Implement a check on startup to alert developers of missing tables.
  • Risk R002 (Data Loss): Maintain a seed-test-data.js script to quickly repopulate the database after a reset.
  • Risk R003 (Connection Failure): Ensure PostgreSQL service is monitored and automatically restarted if it fails.
