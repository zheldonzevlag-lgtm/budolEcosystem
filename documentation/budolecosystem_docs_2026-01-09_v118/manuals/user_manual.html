Sending Money with budolPay

New Features

  • Direct P2P Transfer: Send money instantly to any other budolPay user using their phone number or email.
  • Transaction History: Real-time updates for all incoming and outgoing transfers.

How to Send Money

Step 1: Open the budolPay app and navigate to the "Send Money" section.
Step 2: Enter the recipient's phone number or email address.
Step 3: Enter the amount you wish to send.
Step 4: (Optional) Add a description or note for the transaction.
Step 5: Confirm the details and tap "Send".

Important Requirements

  • KYC Verification: You must be "FULLY VERIFIED" to send money. BASIC accounts are limited to receiving funds and making payments.
  • Balance: Ensure you have sufficient funds in your wallet before initiating a transfer.

Interface Changes

The "Transactions" tab now correctly displays detailed history for all P2P transfers, including reference IDs and timestamps.
