Future Enhancements & Recommendations - 2026-02-26

1. Automated Cart Cleanup

Implement a background job (cron) to periodically remove CartItem records that are older than 30 days or belong to products that have been soft-deleted.

2. Mobile App OTA Updates

Integrate an Over-The-Air (OTA) update system like Shorebird for the Flutter app to allow pushing small bug fixes without requiring a full APK re-installation by the user.

3. Advanced Price Analytics

Provide sellers with a "Price Suggestion" feature based on marketplace trends for similar variation combinations.

4. Multi-Region CDN

Optimize image and video loading by implementing a multi-region CDN strategy for Cloudinary assets to reduce latency for international buyers.
