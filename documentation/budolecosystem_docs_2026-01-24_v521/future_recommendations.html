Future Recommendations - v521

Background Cleanup Job

Implement a scheduled task (cron job) to automatically cancel 'pending' orders that have been inactive for more than 30 minutes. This provides a safety net for users who close their browser during payment without triggering the frontend rollback.

Webhooks for Payment Status

Integrate BudolPay webhooks to receive real-time updates on payment status. This will allow the system to automatically mark orders as cancelled if the payment provider notifies us of a failed or expired transaction.
