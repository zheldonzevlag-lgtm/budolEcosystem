Mitigation Strategy - v521

M-521-1: Stale Order Cleanup

Risk: R-521-1 (Network failure during cancellation)

Mitigation: Implement a background cron job that automatically cancels any 'pending' orders older than 1 hour.

M-521-2: Status Verification

Risk: R-521-2 (Race condition)

Mitigation: The cancelOrder service checks isPaid and status === 'COMPLETED' before allowing cancellation. If paid, cancellation is blocked.
