Order Cancellation

If you decide not to proceed with a payment while the QR code is displayed, you can simply close the payment window. Your items will be automatically returned to your shopping cart, and you can continue shopping or try a different payment method later.

Failed Payments

If your payment fails for any reason, you will see a notification. Your cart will be restored so you don't have to pick your items again. You can check your "Order History" to see the status of any cancelled or failed attempts.
