User Manual: Your Path to Verification

Verification is not just a badge—it unlocks the full potential of your BudolPay wallet. We've simplified the process to match global standards.

1. Verification Tiers

BASIC

Phone number verified. You can receive money and pay at select merchants, but withdrawals are limited.

FULLY VERIFIED

Unlock P2P transfers, Bank Transfers, and higher transaction limits by uploading your ID and a selfie.

2. When will I be asked to Verify?

Unlike other apps, you can explore BudolPay with a Basic account. However, you will be prompted to "Get Fully Verified" when you try to:

  • Send Money to another user.
  • Cash Out to GCash, Maya, or your Bank.
  • Increase your monthly transaction limits.

3. The 3-Minute Verification Flow

1
Personal Info: Confirm your legal name and birthdate.
2
ID Scan: Take a clear photo of a valid government ID.
3
Liveness Selfie: A quick photo to ensure you are really you!

Note: Most applications are reviewed within 24 hours. You will receive a notification once your status is updated.
