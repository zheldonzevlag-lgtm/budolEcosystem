Order History

Viewing Your Orders

Your orders are now visible immediately after successful payment. Go to the Orders section in your profile to track your recent purchases.

Payment Status

Orders paid via non-COD methods will show as "Processing" once the payment is verified. If an order remains in "Awaiting Payment" for more than 30 minutes, it may be cancelled automatically.

Support

If you cannot find an order that you have already paid for, please contact support with your Transaction Reference ID.
