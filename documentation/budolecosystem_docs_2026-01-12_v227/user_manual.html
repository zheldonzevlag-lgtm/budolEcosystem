User Manual: Biometric Login

How to use:

  1. Open budolPay app.
  2. Enter your phone number.
  3. On the PIN screen, look for the biometric icons (Face or Fingerprint) inside the PIN box.
  4. Tap the icon to trigger your phone's biometric scanner.
  5. Once authenticated, you will be logged in automatically.

Note: You must enable Biometric Login in Settings after your first successful PIN login.
