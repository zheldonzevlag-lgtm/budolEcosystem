User Manual: Adding a Product

Overview

The new "Add Product" wizard allows sellers to easily list items with multiple images, variations (sizes/colors), and detailed descriptions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Basic Information

Enter the product name, category, and description. You can upload up to 8 images. Drag and drop images to reorder them - the first image will be the cover photo.

Step 2: Sales Information

Set the price and stock level. If your product has variations (e.g., Red/Blue, S/M/L), toggle "Enable Variations" to set prices and stock for each option specifically.

Step 3: Shipping

Input the weight and dimensions. Accurate values ensure correct shipping fee calculations for buyers.

Step 4: Review & Submit

Check all details and click "Submit". Your product will be live immediately.

Draft Management

If you leave the "Add Product" page before submitting, your progress is saved locally on your device. When you return:

  • A notification will appear at the top: "Unsaved Draft Found".
  • Click "Restore Draft" to continue where you left off.
  • If you ignore it and start typing, you can start a fresh product listing.

Tips

  • Use high-quality images for better sales.
  • Detailed descriptions help reduce customer questions.
  • Keep stock levels updated to avoid cancellations.
