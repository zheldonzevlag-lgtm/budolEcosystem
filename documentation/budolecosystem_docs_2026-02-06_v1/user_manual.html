budol₱ay Security Features

Your security is our top priority. We've implemented several features to keep your account safe even if you leave your phone unattended.

Understanding Session Lock

To protect your financial information, the budol₱ay app will automatically "lock" its screen in the following situations:

1. Inactivity Timeout

If you don't touch the screen for more than 3 minutes while the app is open, a blur screen will appear with a lock icon. This prevents anyone else from seeing your balance or transactions if you leave your phone on a table.

2. App Switching

If you switch to another app (like checking your messages) and don't return to budol₱ay within 3 minutes, the app will be locked when you return.

How to Unlock

When the session is locked, you don't need to type your full password again. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Tap the "Unlock with Biometrics" button.
  2. Use your Fingerprint or FaceID to verify your identity.
  3. You will be immediately returned to the screen you were previously viewing.

Note: If your biometrics fail multiple times, or if you prefer to log in with another account, tap "Log Out" at the bottom of the lock screen.

Security Tips

  • Always enable Fingerprint or FaceID on your device for the best experience.
  • Never share your MPIN or password with anyone.
  • If you lose your phone, contact budolPay support immediately to freeze your account.
