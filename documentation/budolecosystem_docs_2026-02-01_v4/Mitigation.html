Mitigation Plan: Security Controls for SSO & OTP

M-2.2.1: Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Requirement

Risk Addressed: SSO Account Takeover (R-2.2.1)

Action: Even with SSO login, sensitive actions (payments, withdrawals) still require the 6-digit Budol PIN. If no PIN is set, the SSO session is limited to 10 minutes and restricted to PIN setup.

M-2.2.2: Rate Limiting & Device Fingerprinting

Risk Addressed: OTP Interception & Brute Force (R-2.2.2)

Action: Backend implements strict rate limiting on OTP requests per phone number and IP. Each OTP is bound to a specific deviceId, preventing replay attacks from other devices.

M-2.2.3: Whitelisted SSO Providers Only

Risk Addressed: SSO Spoofing (R-2.2.3)

Action: Only verified SDKs and endpoints for Google and Facebook are integrated. The backend validates the integrity of the SSO response before initiating the linking process.

M-2.2.4: Conflict Resolution Logic

Risk Addressed: Automated Linking Conflict (R-2.2.4)

Action: If multiple accounts are found (unlikely due to unique constraints), the system defaults to the most recently verified account and logs the conflict for manual admin review.
