User Manual: v1.3.59

New Features: Quick Registration & Social Account Linking

1. Quick Registration

Step 1: Open Registration Screen

Navigate to the registration screen. You will see a toggle for "Quick Registration".

Step 2: Enter Phone Number

Provide your mobile number. The system will automatically format it to the standard Philippine mobile format.

Step 3: Verify OTP

Enter the 6-digit code sent to your mobile. Once verified, your account is ready for basic use!

2. Social Account Linking (SSO)

Step 1: Choose Provider

On the login screen, select "Login with Google" or "Login with Facebook".

Step 2: Automatic Matching

If your social account email or phone number matches an existing Budol profile, the accounts will be linked automatically. No extra passwords needed!

3. Login with OTP

Direct Phone Login

You can now login using just your phone number and an OTP. Simply enter your number on the login screen and select "Login with OTP".
