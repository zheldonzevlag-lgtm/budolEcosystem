User Manual: New Login & Linking Features

1. Logging in with OTP

If you forget your password or prefer a quicker login, you can now use your mobile number and an OTP:

  1. Open the BudolPay app and go to the Login screen.
  2. Enter your registered mobile number.
  3. Tap Login with OTP.
  4. Enter the 6-digit code sent to your phone.
  5. If you haven't set up a PIN yet, you will be prompted to do so.

2. Automated SSO Linking

You can now sign in using your Google or Facebook account. If your social account email or phone number matches your Budol account, they will be linked automatically.

Why link? Linking allows you to sign in with one tap in the future without needing to remember a separate Budol password.
  1. On the Login screen, tap the Google or Facebook button.
  2. Authorize the connection in the pop-up window.
  3. If a match is found, you will be logged into your existing account immediately.
  4. If no match is found, a new account will be created for you using your social profile details.

3. Quick Registration

New users can register using just their mobile number and an OTP. You can complete your profile (email, full name) and KYC verification later from the account settings.
