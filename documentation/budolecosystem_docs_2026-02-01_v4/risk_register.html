Risk Register: SSO & OTP Implementation

Risk ID Risk Description Impact Probability Level
R-2.2.1 SSO Account Takeover: If a user's Google/Facebook account is compromised, their Budol account is also at risk. High Medium High
R-2.2.2 OTP Interception: SMS OTPs can be intercepted via SIM swapping or malicious apps. High Low Medium
R-2.2.3 Email/Phone Spoofing in SSO: Malicious SSO providers (not Google/FB) could spoof user data for unauthorized linking. Medium Very Low Low
R-2.2.4 Automated Linking Conflict: Two users might share an email or phone number in some edge cases, leading to incorrect account linking. Medium Low Medium
R-2.2.5 Session Persistence: Long-lived SSO sessions (30 days) increase the window for unauthorized access if the device is lost. Medium Medium Medium
