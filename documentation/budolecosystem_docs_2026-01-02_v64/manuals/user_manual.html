User Manual - Paying with QR Code

You can now use your budolPay Mobile app to pay for orders on budolShap by simply scanning a QR code. It's fast, secure, and integrated with your budolEcosystem account.

Accessing the App

You can access the budolPay Mobile web preview through your browser at the following address:

Live Preview URL

http://localhost:8081

(Ensure the local server is running on port 8081)

How to Pay via QR

1
Checkout on budolShap:

Select "budolPay" as your payment method during checkout on the budolShap website. A QR code will appear on the screen.

2
Open budolPay Mobile:

Launch the app and tap the Scan QR button on the home screen (inside the balance card) or the Scan tab in the bottom menu.

3
Scan & Confirm:

Point your phone's camera at the QR code on the budolShap website. Review the payment summary (Amount and Merchant) and tap CONFIRM PAYMENT.

4
Success!

Once the payment is processed, you will see a success confirmation, and your budolShap order will automatically update to "Paid".

The app now matches the signature Rose Red (#F43F5E) brand color of the budolPay ecosystem, providing a consistent look and feel across all platforms.
