User Manual Updates - v197

Login Experience Restored

Fixed: Identification Failed

We have resolved the issue where entering your mobile number would result in an "Identification failed" error. You can now proceed to the next steps of the login process.

Updated Login Flow

When you enter your mobile number and click "Continue", the system now correctly identifies your account and triggers the appropriate security challenge:

  • OTP Verification: If you are logging in from a new device, you will receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS.
  • PIN Entry: If your device is already trusted, you will be prompted to enter your 6-digit secure PIN.
  • Biometrics: On supported devices, you may be offered the option to log in using fingerprint or face recognition.

Troubleshooting

If you still see the "Identification failed" error, please try the following:

  1. Close and restart the mobile app.
  2. Check your internet connection.
  3. Ensure you are entering the correct mobile number associated with your account.
