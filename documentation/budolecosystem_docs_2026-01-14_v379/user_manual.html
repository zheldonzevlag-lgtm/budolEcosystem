Face Registration

To register your face for biometric payments:

  1. Go to Settings > Face Registration.
  2. Ensure you are in a well-lit area.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to capture your face.

Troubleshooting: If you see an error saying "AI Inference failed to initialize," please ensure your phone has sufficient storage and restart the application.
