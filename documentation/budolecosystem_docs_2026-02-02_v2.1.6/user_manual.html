How to Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your password for any budolEcosystem application (budolPay, budolShap, or Admin Dashboard), follow these steps:

1
Go to the Login Page and click the "Forgot Password?" link.
2
Enter your registered Email Address and click "Send OTP".
3
Check your registered device for a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP).
4
Enter the OTP and your New Password on the verification page.
5
Click "Reset Password". You will be redirected back to the login page upon success.

Important Security Tips

  • Your OTP is valid for only 5 minutes.
  • Do not share your OTP with anyone, including system administrators.
  • Ensure your new password is at least 12 characters long with symbols and numbers.
