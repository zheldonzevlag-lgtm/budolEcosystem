Risk Register - v2.1.6 (SSPR Implementation)

Risk ID Description Impact Probability Severity Mitigation Strategy
R-011 Admin Lockout: Without SSPR, admins who forget passwords are locked out permanently without manual DB intervention. Critical Medium High Implement Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) with MFA/Out-of-band verification.
R-012 OTP Brute Forcing: Attackers might attempt to guess the 6-digit OTP for password reset. High Medium High Implement rate limiting and token expiration (5 minutes).
R-013 Reset Token Exposure: Reset tokens leaked in logs or browser history. High Low Medium Use short-lived tokens and clear them immediately after use.
