🚀 Future Recommendations - v2.1.6

SMS/Email Gateway Integration

Transition from console-logged OTPs to production-grade delivery using Semaphore (for local PH SMS) or SendGrid (for Email). This is critical for actual out-of-band verification.

Rate Limiting (WAF/Middleware)

Implement IP-based rate limiting on /auth/forgot-password to prevent denial-of-service and brute-force enumeration attacks.

Password History Policy

Prevent users from reusing their last 5 passwords to comply with stricter banking security standards.
