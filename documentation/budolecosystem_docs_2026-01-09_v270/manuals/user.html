What's New?

Improved Transaction Details

We've rearranged the transaction review screen to make it easier to read. The recipient or sender's name is now prominently displayed right above the Transaction ID.

Streamlined Button Layout

Action buttons like "Favorite" and "Receipt" are now neatly organized in a single row at the bottom of the transaction details, providing a cleaner and more modern look.

Enhanced Branding in Marketing

Our marketing slides now feature improved branding. You'll notice that ecosystem names like budolPay and budolShap are now highlighted in their signature colors, making it easier to identify our various services.

How to Use

Viewing Transaction Details

After completing a transaction, tap on it in your history. You will see the recipient's name at the top of the details list, followed by the transaction ID. Use the buttons at the bottom to favorite the transaction or view your receipt.

Exploring Marketing Ads

Swipe through the marketing slides on the home screen to see the new colorful branding in the descriptions. Each service (Loan, Shap, Express, Pay) now has its own unique color identity.
