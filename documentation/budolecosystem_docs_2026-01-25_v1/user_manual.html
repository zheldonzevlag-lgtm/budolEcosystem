User Manual - Paying with budolPay

Payment Steps

  1. Select budolPay at checkout.
  2. A QR Code will be displayed.
  3. Scan the QR code using your budolPay mobile app.
  4. Confirm the payment in the app.
  5. Once confirmed, budolShap will automatically show a "Verifying Payment" screen.
  6. Within seconds, you will see the "Payment Successful!" confirmation.

Troubleshooting

If the payment screen does not update after you've paid in the app, please wait up to 60 seconds. If it still doesn't update, check your My Orders page to see the updated status.
