Guide to new Order List features and improvements

1. Understanding Your Order List

We have improved the "My Orders" page to be cleaner and easier to understand.

Why did an order disappear?

To keep your list organized, we now automatically hide orders that are:

  • Cancelled by you or the system.
  • Failed Payments (e.g., GCash transaction failed).
  • Unpaid for over 30 minutes (unless you chose Cash on Delivery).

This ensures you only see orders that are active, processing, or successfully completed.

2. Customer Name vs. Shipping Name

You might have noticed confusion before about which name appears on the order card. We've fixed this!

What name do I see now?

Top Name: This is YOUR account name (the logged-in user). You will always see your name here.

Shipping To: Below your name, you will see "Shipping To: [Name]" which shows the person receiving the package (e.g., if you are sending a gift to a friend).

3. New Payment Experience (budol₱ay Mobile)

We've updated the budol₱ay Mobile App to give you a more modern and reassuring payment experience.

What's new?

  • Full-Page Status: Instead of small pop-ups, you'll see a clear, full-page confirmation for every payment.
  • Real-time Verification: You'll see a "Verifying Payment" screen while we securely process your transaction.
  • Detailed Receipt: After a successful payment, you'll see a complete breakdown including Order ID, Merchant Name, and exact Date/Time.

4. Payment Safety Tips

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): These orders will always appear in your list immediately.
  • Online Payment (GCash/Maya): Please complete the payment within 30 minutes. If you close the payment window or cancel, the order will be removed from your list to avoid clutter.

4. Comparison with Lazada/Shopee

Just like other major e-commerce platforms, we are moving towards a system where unpaid orders are automatically managed to ensure inventory is available for serious buyers.
