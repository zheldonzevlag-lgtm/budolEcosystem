User Manual Updates - v292

What's New in SSO?

The latest update focuses on the stability of the Single Sign-On (SSO) experience. Users will notice a more reliable transition between budolID, budolPay, and budolShap.

Faster & More Reliable Login

We've optimized the way our services talk to each other. This means fewer "Session Expired" errors and a smoother redirect back to your dashboard after signing in.

How to Sign In (Updated Workflow)

1
Navigate to the login page of any ecosystem app (e.g., budolPay Admin or budolShap).
2
Click the "Sign In with budolID" button. You will be redirected to the secure budolID login portal.
3
Enter your credentials (Email and Password).
4
Upon successful authentication, you will be instantly redirected back to the original application without needing to log in again.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter an "Invalid Token" error:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Ensure you are using the correct 127.0.0.1 IP address if testing in a local development environment.
  • Check with your System Administrator if your account has been properly synced with the ecosystem.
Pro Tip: Your budolID session is valid for 24 hours across all applications. You only need to sign in once per day!
