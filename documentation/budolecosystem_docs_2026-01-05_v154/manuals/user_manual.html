User Manual: budolPay Mobile Features

1. Cash In (Add Funds)

Step 1: Open Cash In

On the Home Screen, tap the "Cash In" button.

Step 2: Select Provider

Choose your preferred payment partner (e.g., 7-Eleven, GCash, BDO) from the dropdown list.

Step 3: Enter Amount

Input the amount you wish to deposit into your budolPay wallet.

Step 4: Confirm

Tap "Confirm Cash In". Your balance will be updated instantly, and you'll receive a success notification.

2. Send Money (P2P Transfer)

Step 1: Open Send Money

Tap the "Send" button on the Home Screen.

Step 2: Enter Recipient Email

Type the email address of the person you want to send money to. The system will verify the user automatically.

Step 3: Enter Amount & Message

Specify the amount and add an optional message (e.g., "Payment for lunch").

Step 4: Send

Tap "Send Money". The recipient will receive the funds in real-time.
