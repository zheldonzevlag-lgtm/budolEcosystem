User Manual: Improved App Login Experience

1. Persistent Sessions v1.2.96

Stay Logged In

With the v1.2.96 update, budolPay now remembers your session even if you close the app. When you reopen the app, your name and wallet details will be immediately available without requiring you to log in again (unless your session has expired after 30 days of inactivity).

2. Precision Amount Entry

Automatic 2-Decimal & Comma Formatting

When sending money or adding funds, the amount field now automatically ensures two decimal places and includes commas for thousands. Simply type the numbers, and the system will format it for you. For example, typing "100000" will result in "1,000.00", and "5000000" will result in "50,000.00". This makes it easier to read large amounts and ensures transaction accuracy.

3. Identifying the Correct Version

To ensure you have the session persistence and amount formatting fixes, check the version number on the app's Splash Screen (the screen that appears when you first open the app). It should display v1.2.96.

3. What to do if you see "Welcome back, User!"

If the app displays "User" instead of your name for more than a few seconds after opening, please check your internet connection. The app performs a background check to refresh your profile. If the issue persists, try logging out and logging back in once to reset your local session data.

4. Security Reminder

While we offer persistent sessions for your convenience, we recommend using Biometric Login or PIN protection on your mobile device to ensure that only you can access your budolPay wallet if your phone is lost or stolen.
