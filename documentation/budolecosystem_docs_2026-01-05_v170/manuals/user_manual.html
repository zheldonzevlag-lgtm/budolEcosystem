Version 170 Update

KYC & Account Security

New features to make your BudolPay account more secure and easier to manage.

📅 Easy Date of Birth Entry

We've replaced manual typing with a simple calendar picker. This ensures your birthdate is always in the correct format (MM-DD-YYYY).

1
Tap the Calendar Icon in the Date of Birth field.
2
Select your birth year, month, and day.
3
The date will automatically be saved with the required hyphens.

📍 Pin Your Address

Don't want to type your full address? You can now use the Pin feature to quickly set your location during KYC verification.

1
Navigate to the Address field.
2
Tap the Pin button.
3
Your location will be automatically filled in.

📱 Securely Change Phone Number

Following Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) security guidelines, changing your phone number now requires identity verification to protect your funds.

1
Go to Settings > Change Phone Number.
2
Enter your new mobile number and a reason for the change.
3
Take a photo of your Valid ID to prove your identity.
4
Submit the request. Our team will review it within 24-48 hours.
