User Manual

Information for users of the Budol Ecosystem platform.

Viewing Your Orders

In your order history, you will see your account name as the primary customer for the order. If you have provided a different shipping name (for example, when sending a gift), that name will appear in the shipping details section below your name.

Why is my name shown?

Your account name is shown to ensure the store can identify you as the purchaser for support and warranty purposes, even if the item is shipped to another person.
