New Features

1. Transaction History View

You can now see a full list of your past transactions. Access this by tapping "See All" in the Recent Transactions section on the home screen or by tapping the History icon in the bottom menu.

2. Detailed Transaction Reports

Tapping on any transaction in your list now opens a detailed view. This view includes:

  • Transaction ID: Unique identifier for your records.
  • Reference Number: Useful for customer support inquiries.
  • Exact Timestamp: The date and time the transaction was processed.
  • Sender/Receiver Info: Clearly see who you sent money to or received money from.

How to Use

  1. Open the budolPay app.
  2. Scroll to the "Recent Transactions" section.
  3. Tap on any individual transaction to see its full details.
  4. Tap "See All" to view your entire history.

Fixes

Improved the stability of the transaction list to prevent crashes when viewing older transactions with incomplete data.
