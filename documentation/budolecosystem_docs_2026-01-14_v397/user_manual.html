User Manual - v397

Zero-Action Face Login

Version 1.2.88 introduces the fastest way to log in to budolPay.

1
Tap the Face Icon

On the PIN screen, tap the face icon in the password field.

2
Instant Verification

The app will immediately trigger your phone's built-in face recognition or fingerprint scanner. No more manual photo capture or shutter clicks!

3
Automatic Login

Once verified, you will be logged in automatically. If no face is found, the system will fall back to your phone's registered fingerprint or ask for your PIN.
