Mitigation Plan

M-001: SIM Swapping

Implemented device fingerprinting (deviceId) and trusted device registration during quick-reg.

M-002: AML Risk

Restricted transaction limits for BASIC tier users. Full KYC required for higher volumes.

M-003: OTP Interception

Short OTP expiration (10 minutes) and audit logging of all attempts.

M-004: Duplicate Accounts

Strict phone normalization (BSP Circular 808) applied before database lookup.
