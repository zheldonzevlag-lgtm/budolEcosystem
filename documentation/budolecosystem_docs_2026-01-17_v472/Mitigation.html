Mitigation Strategy v472

M-472-01 (Inconsistent Branding):

Automated grep searches and comprehensive verification scripts (verify_comprehensive_branding_v472.dart) were used to ensure all user-facing strings were updated.

M-472-02 (UTF-8 Rendering):

The Philippine Peso sign (₱) is a standard Unicode character. Testing was conducted on multiple device emulators to ensure proper rendering.

M-472-03 (Regex mismatch):

The regex in UIUtils was updated to be case-insensitive and specifically include the ₱ character to avoid false negatives.
