Tracking Your Payment

When you pay for an order using the BudolPay app, your payment status will now be updated instantly across all sections of the order details page.

  • Order Progress: The payment step will turn green immediately after payment confirmation.
  • Tracking History: You will see a "Paid" event in your timeline as soon as the transaction is complete.

If you choose Cash on Delivery (COD), the payment status will still remain as "Pending" until the item is delivered to you.
