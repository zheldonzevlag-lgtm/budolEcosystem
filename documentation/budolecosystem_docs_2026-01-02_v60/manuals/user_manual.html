User Manual - v60

Improved Checkout Experience

Reliable QR Payments

We have improved the way QR code payments (like GCash/QRPh) are handled. You will now be taken to a dedicated payment page that stays active until your payment is confirmed. Even if you accidentally close the browser or refresh the page, the payment screen will remember your order details and show the correct QR code.

No More Premature Redirects

Fixed an issue where the website would sometimes send you to the "My Orders" page before the payment was actually finished. Now, you will stay on the payment screen until the transaction is successfully authorized by the bank or provider.

Automatic Order Updates

Once your payment is successful, the system will automatically detect it and update your order status. You no longer need to manually check if your payment went through—the screen will update in real-time to show your success confirmation.
