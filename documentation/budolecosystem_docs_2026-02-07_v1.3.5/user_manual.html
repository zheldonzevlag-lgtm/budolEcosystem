User Manual: What's New in v1.3.5

We've made some improvements to your registration experience and the way your account information is displayed.

1. Cleaner Account View

If you registered using your mobile phone number, you will now see a cleaner view in the top menu. We've removed technical identifiers to ensure only your relevant account information is shown.

Tip: Your phone number is your primary way to log in and receive updates!

2. Improved Registration Form

When you switch between registering with an email or a mobile number, the form will now automatically clear your previous entries. This helps prevent mistakes and ensures you're entering the correct information for your chosen method.

3. Secure Sign-Up

We've updated our forms to be more secure by preventing your browser from automatically filling in old or incorrect information during the registration process.
