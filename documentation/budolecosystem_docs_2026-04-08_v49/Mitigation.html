Mitigation Strategies - v49

1. Deployment Stability

To mitigate the risk of "NOT_FOUND" errors, we have implemented a standard export pattern for all Express services in the ecosystem. Every service must now export module.exports = app to ensure compatibility with Vercel's Node.js bridge.

2. Configuration Management

We use fix-vercel-root.js to programmatically ensure project settings are correct, reducing the chance of human error during manual dashboard configuration.
