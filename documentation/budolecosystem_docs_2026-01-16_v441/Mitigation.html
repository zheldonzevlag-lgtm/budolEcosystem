Mitigation Plan - v441

Mitigation of Scaling Issues

Action: Replaced RichText with Text.rich in the Flutter UIUtils class.

Rationale: Standard Text widgets in Flutter use MediaQuery to determine font scaling. RichText bypasses this by default. By using Text.rich, we ensure all components of the budol* brand scale in unison with the rest of the application UI.

Mitigation of Low Contrast

Action: Incremented slate color weight from 400 to 600.

Rationale: Slate 400 (#94A3B8) has a contrast ratio that may be insufficient for users with visual impairments on light backgrounds. Slate 600 (#475569) provides significantly better contrast while maintaining the professional slate aesthetic.
