How to use Face Login

  1. Ensure you have registered your face in Settings > Face Recognition Login.
  2. On the Login screen, enter your phone number.
  3. On the PIN screen, tap the Face Icon next to the PIN field.
  4. The camera will open. Look directly into the camera and blink your eyes.
  5. If the face matches, you will be logged in automatically.

Native Face ID vs. Custom Face Recognition

If your device supports native Face ID, you can use it via the Fingerprint/Biometric Icon. Tapping the Face Icon specifically uses budolPay's built-in face recognition technology.
