Risk Register & Mitigations (v406)

Risk ID Description Impact Probability Mitigation Strategy
R-406-01 AuditLog table growth may impact database performance over time. Medium High Implement automated log rotation and archiving to cold storage for entries older than 180 days.
R-406-02 Unauthorized access to AuditLogs could lead to data leakage of user activity. High Low Enforce strict RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) for the /api/audit-logs endpoint, restricted to ADMIN role only.
R-406-03 Sensitive data (like PII) might be inadvertently included in AuditLog metadata. High Medium Implement a metadata sanitizer utility to mask sensitive fields (e.g., card numbers, passwords) before persisting to the AuditLog.
