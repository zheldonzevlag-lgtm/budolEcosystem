User Manual: Security Transparency

🛡️ Your Security is Our Priority

The Budol Ecosystem employs advanced forensic auditing to ensure your account and data remain secure. Every major action within the system is recorded in an immutable audit trail, providing full transparency and accountability.

🔍 Audit Trail Transparency

Administrative actions that affect your account (such as profile updates or security setting changes) are logged with detailed information. This allows our security team to quickly investigate any unauthorized activity and protect your assets.

✅ Compliance & Trust

Our auditing system is designed to meet the highest international security standards, including PCI DSS and BSP regulations. This ensures that the Budol Ecosystem remains a trusted platform for your financial transactions.
