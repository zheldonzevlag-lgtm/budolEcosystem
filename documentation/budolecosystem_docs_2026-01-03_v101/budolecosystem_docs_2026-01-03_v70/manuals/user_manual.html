User Manual - v70

New: budolPay QR Checkout

You can now complete your budolShap orders using the budolPay mobile app by scanning a secure QR code on the checkout page.

How to Pay

1 Select budolPay

During checkout on budolShap, select budolPay as your payment method and click "Place Order".

2 Scan the QR Code

You will be redirected to the budolPay Checkout page. Open your budolPay Mobile App and use the "Scan" feature to scan the QR code displayed on your screen.

3 Confirm Payment

Once scanned, your mobile app will display the payment details. Review the amount and merchant, then tap "Confirm" to complete the transaction.

4 Order Completion

After a successful payment, the order status on budolShap will automatically update to PAID.

Troubleshooting

  • QR Code not appearing: Ensure you have an active internet connection and your browser is not blocking external scripts (CDN).
  • Failed to parse QR Code: Make sure you are using the latest version of the budolPay mobile app.
