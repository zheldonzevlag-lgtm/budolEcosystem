New Payment Experience

We have simplified the payment process. You no longer need to leave the page to complete your payment.

Step 1: Place Your Order

Click "Place Order" in the checkout page or "Pay Now" in your Order History.

Step 2: Scan the QR Code

A modal window will appear with a QR code. This modal is branded for budolShap and includes a 10-minute timer.

Step 3: Confirm Payment

Use your preferred app (budolPay, GCash, Maya, etc.) to scan and pay. Once confirmed, the modal will automatically close and refresh your order status.

Troubleshooting

If the QR code does not appear, please ensure your internet connection is stable and try clicking "Pay Now" again.
