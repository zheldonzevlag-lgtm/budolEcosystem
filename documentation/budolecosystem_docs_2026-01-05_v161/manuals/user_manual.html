User Manual - Real Activity View

Dynamic Transaction History

Your Home screen now shows your actual transaction history. No more sample data!

How to View

  1. Open the app and log in.
  2. On the Home screen, scroll down to the Recent Transactions section.
  3. The list shows your last 5 transactions.

Transaction Details

  • Green (+): Money you received (Cash In or Transfers).
  • Red (-): Money you spent or sent.
  • Description: The reason or note for the transaction.
  • Date: When the transaction occurred.

Refreshing Data

You can refresh your balance and transactions at any time by pulling down on the Home screen.
