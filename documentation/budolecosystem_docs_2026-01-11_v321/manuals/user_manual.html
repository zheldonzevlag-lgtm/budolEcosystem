User Manual

How biometric login works and why it may be unavailable.

Purpose

To help users understand when biometric login (fingerprint/face) will appear in budolPay and what steps to take if it is not available.

Requirements

  • Enable a screen lock on your device (PIN/pattern/password).
  • Enroll at least one fingerprint or face in your phone’s settings.
  • Enable “Biometric Login” in the app (Settings → Biometric Login).
  • After enabling, log in with your PIN at least once to activate biometric login.
Important: If your phone has biometric hardware but you haven’t enrolled any fingerprint/face, biometric login may not work.

Enable Biometric Login

  1. Log in normally (OTP → PIN).
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Turn on “Biometric Login”.
  4. Log out, then log in with PIN once more to complete activation.

Use Biometric Login

  1. Go to the Login screen and reach the PIN step.
  2. Tap the fingerprint icon.
  3. Complete the biometric prompt (fingerprint/face).

If successful, the app logs you in without typing the PIN.

Disable Biometric Login

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Turn off “Biometric Login”.

Disabling biometrics clears the saved login PIN from secure storage.

Troubleshooting

Biometric toggle not visible

  • Set up screen lock and enroll biometrics in your phone settings.
  • Restart the app and re-check Settings.

Biometric prompt appears but login doesn’t continue

  • Log in with PIN once after enabling biometrics.
  • If you recently changed your MPIN, log in with PIN again to refresh biometric login.

Recommendation for Future Enhancement

Add a simple “Biometric Status” message in Settings explaining what is missing (screen lock, enrollment, or activation login).
