User Manual: Completing Your KYC Verification

This version introduces guided camera frames to make your account verification easier and faster.

Step 1: ID Card Scan

Ensure your ID is flat on a clear surface. Align the card within the rectangle frame on your screen. Tap the camera button to capture.

Step 2: Selfie Verification

Hold your phone at eye level. Align your face within the circle frame. The frame will help ensure proper lighting and alignment for faster approval.

Tips for Success:

  • Avoid glare or shadows on your ID.
  • Use a neutral background for your selfie.
  • Ensure all four corners of the ID are visible.
