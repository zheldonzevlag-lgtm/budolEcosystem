Mitigation Strategies: v37

M37-01: Orientation Locking

Strategy: Hardcode lockCaptureOrientation(DeviceOrientation.portraitUp) in the CameraController initialization.

Status: Implemented in kyc_capture_screen.dart.

M37-02: Resolution Throttling

Strategy: Cap image capture at 1024x1024 and quality at 85% to ensure compatibility with 2GB memory devices.

Status: Implemented in kyc_verification_screen.dart and KYCCaptureScreen.

M37-03: Legacy Storage Support

Strategy: Enable requestLegacyExternalStorage in Android Manifest to bypass scoped storage restrictions on Android 9/10/11 emulators.

Status: Applied to AndroidManifest.xml.
