Issues & Fixes: v37

Issue: Camera orientaiton sideways on NoxPlayer Android 9.

Fix: Replaced ImagePicker with custom CameraController and forced portraitUp orientation lock during initialization.

Issue: "Camera keeps stopping" crash due to OOM on low-end emulators.

Fix: Lowered compileSdk to 35 and enforced 1024px maximum resolution for all captured images.

Issue: Absence of visual guidance for ID card scanning.

Fix: Implemented a custom KYCOverlayPainter that renders a centered, semi-transparent rounded rectangle frame.
