User Manual: How to Reset Your Password

Step 1: Request Reset

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address.

Step 2: Receive OTP

Check both your Email and SMS for a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP). The code is valid for 5 minutes.

Step 3: Verify and Reset

Enter the OTP and your new password on the reset page. Once successful, you will be redirected back to the login page.
