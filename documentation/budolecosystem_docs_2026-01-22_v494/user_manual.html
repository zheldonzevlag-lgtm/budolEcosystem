Realtime Notifications

Stay informed with our new professional notification system. Whenever your order status changes, you will receive a clean, easy-to-read alert in the top-right corner of your screen. These notifications use simple language (e.g., "Paid" instead of "PAID") and provide the specific order ID for quick reference.

Realtime Order Updates

Your orders now stay in sync across all tabs automatically. When a seller ships your order, or when it is delivered, you will see it move to the correct tab instantly without having to refresh the page.

Buyer Experience

In the "My Orders" section, you can watch your order progress through "To Pay", "To Ship", "To Receive", and "Completed" in realtime. If you are viewing the "To Receive" tab and an order is marked as delivered, it will disappear from that list and appear in "Completed" immediately.

Seller Experience

Sellers will see their order lists update automatically. If an order status changes (e.g., payment verified), it will move to the "Needs Booking" or "Booked" tab automatically, allowing for faster fulfillment workflows.
