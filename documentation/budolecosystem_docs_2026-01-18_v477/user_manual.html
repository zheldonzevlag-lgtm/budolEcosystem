Biometric Login Fix

This update ensures that your biometric login settings are always in sync with your preferences.

How to Enable Biometric Login

  1. Login to budol₱ay using your PIN.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Toggle Biometric Login or Face Recognition Login to ON.
  4. Follow the prompts to verify your identity.

Logging In with Biometrics

Once enabled, you can now tap the Biometric icon on the Login screen. The app will instantly recognize your preference, even if you just enabled it a few seconds ago.
