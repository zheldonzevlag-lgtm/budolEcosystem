Future Recommendations (v3.4.1)

Status: Active

High Priority

Recommendation: Implement Webhook Retry Logic

Rationale: Currently, if a webhook fails, there is no automatic retry mechanism on our end (though PayMongo might retry). We should log failed webhooks and have a scheduled job to re-process them.

Benefit: Improves payment reliability and cart cleanup consistency.

Medium Priority

Recommendation: Cart Item "Save for Later"

Rationale: Users might want to move items out of the active cart without deleting them entirely.

Benefit: Improves UX and potentially increases conversion rates later.

Low Priority

Recommendation: Advanced Stock Reservation

Rationale: Reserve stock for 10-15 minutes when a user enters the checkout flow to prevent "out of stock" errors during payment.

Benefit: Better user experience during high-demand sales (e.g., limited drops).
