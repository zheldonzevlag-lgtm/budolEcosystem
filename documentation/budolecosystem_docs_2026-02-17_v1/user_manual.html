User Manual (v3.4.1)

Topic: Shopping Cart & Checkout Experience

1. Shopping Cart Enhancements

We've improved how the shopping cart handles product variations (sizes, colors, etc.).

What's New?

  • Distinct Variations: Different sizes or colors of the same product (e.g., "Jordan Shoes - Size 9" vs. "Jordan Shoes - Size 10") now appear as separate items in your cart.
  • Accurate Stock: Each variation tracks its own stock level, so you won't be able to add more than what's available for that specific size/color.

2. Checkout & Payment Process

The checkout process remains streamlined, but with better reliability for online payments.

Step 1: Place Order

Select your items and proceed to checkout. Choose your preferred payment method (COD, GCash, Maya, etc.).

Step 2: Payment (For GCash/Maya/GrabPay)

You will be redirected to the secure payment gateway (PayMongo) to complete your transaction.

Step 3: Confirmation

After successful payment, you will be redirected back to the order confirmation page.

Update: Purchased items will now be automatically removed from your cart immediately after payment is confirmed. Items you didn't buy will remain in your cart for later.

3. Troubleshooting

My cart still shows items I just bought!

In rare cases where the internet connection is interrupted right after payment:

  • Refresh the page. The system usually cleans up the cart within a few seconds of receiving the payment confirmation from the bank.
  • Check your "My Orders" page. If the order is marked as PAID, you can safely manually remove the items from your cart if they are still there.
