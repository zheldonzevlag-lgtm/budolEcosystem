Paying via QR Code

Step 1: Open Scanner

Launch the budolPay app and tap "Scan QR to Pay".

Step 2: Scan the Merchant QR

Point your camera at the QR code displayed on the checkout page. The app will automatically detect the code.

Step 3: Wait for Confirmation

A loading spinner will appear while the payment is processed. Do not close the app.

Step 4: Success Feedback

Upon success, a green notification "Payment Successful!" will appear, and the app will return to the home screen.

If an error occurs (e.g., Insufficient funds), a red notification will appear with a "Retry" button.

Troubleshooting

If the scanner does not appear or shows a white screen, ensure you have granted camera permissions to the app. You can tap "Retry" on any error message to restart the camera.
