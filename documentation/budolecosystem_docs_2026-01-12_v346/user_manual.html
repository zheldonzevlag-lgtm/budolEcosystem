Improved App Startup

In this version, we have significantly improved the app's loading speed and reliability. You should no longer experience being "stuck" on the splash screen.

1. Faster Access to Home

If you are already logged in, the app will now take you directly to your Home screen after a short branding animation. This eliminates unnecessary navigation steps.

2. Robust Offline Handling

The app is now better at handling temporary network issues during startup. If the server is slow to respond, the app will still allow you to reach the login or home screen rather than hanging indefinitely.

Tip: If you find the app is not updating your balance immediately after opening, please wait a few seconds as it fetches the latest data in the background.
