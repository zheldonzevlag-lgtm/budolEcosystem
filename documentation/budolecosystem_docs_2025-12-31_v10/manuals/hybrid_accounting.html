Hybrid Accounting Model

We have refined the financial architecture to a Hybrid Model. In this design, each app maintains its own operational autonomy while budolAccounting acts as the ecosystem's "Consolidated Auditor."

Local Ledger: budolPay

Handles immediate wallet balances, P2P transfers, and financial transaction history for the payment app.

Local Ledger: budolShap

Handles e-commerce specific accounting: merchant payouts, sales commissions, and order refunds.

FUTURE ROADMAP

Global Observer: budolAccounting

A centralized "Watchtower" service that aggregates financial data from all local ledgers to provide ecosystem-wide financial reporting, tax compliance, and cross-app auditing.

How it Works:

  • Operational Autonomy: Each app is fast and self-contained because it manages its own ledger for day-to-day operations.
  • Asynchronous Sync: Local transactions are periodically (or via hooks) pushed to budolAccounting for consolidation.
  • Cross-App Reconciliation: budolAccounting ensures that if budolShap says a payment was made, budolPay also shows the corresponding wallet movement.
