User Manual

This guide explains how to use both budolPay and budolShap with a single account.

budolPay
For Your Wallet & Payments

Use budolPay to send money, pay bills, and manage your wallet balance. Your budolPay balance is shared with budolShap for seamless shopping.

budolShap
For Your Shopping & Selling

Use budolShap to buy products or set up your own shop. You can pay using your budolPay balance directly at checkout.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need two accounts? No. One account works for both apps.
  • Can I use my budolPay balance on budolShap? Yes, it is the primary payment method.
  • Where do I see my sales? Sales from budolShap are credited directly to your budolPay wallet.
