Improved Payment Experience (v526)

We've upgraded the payment experience to be faster and more transparent. You no longer need to wait or refresh your screen to see your payment status.

Seamless Payment Flow

1
Select budolPay

When checking out on budolShap, select budolPay as your payment method. A QR code will appear instantly.

2
Scan & Pay

Open your budolPay Mobile App, scan the QR code, and confirm your payment. You will see a "Verifying Payment" animation.

3
Instant Confirmation

The moment you confirm on your phone, both your phone and the budolShap website will simultaneously show "Payment Successful."

What to Expect

  • No More Refreshing: The budolShap website will automatically redirect you to your order summary once payment is confirmed.
  • Consistent UI: Whether you pay via GCash or budolPay, the "Verifying" and "Success" screens look and feel exactly the same.
  • Real-time Balance Update: Your budolPay wallet balance updates immediately after the transaction is complete.
Need Help? If your payment doesn't update within 10 seconds, the system will automatically double-check the status for you.
