User Manual: Face Recognition Guide

Version: v362

Purpose: Helping users with biometric enrollment and login.

1. Verification (KYC)

To ensure the security of your budolPay account, we use advanced facial recognition. Follow these steps for a smooth experience:

Step 1: Preparation

Go to a well-lit area. Remove glasses, masks, or hats that might obscure your face.

Step 2: Positioning

Hold your phone at eye level and center your face within the on-screen circle.

Step 3: Liveness Actions

Follow the prompts! You will be asked to Blink and Smile. This proves you are a real person and not a photo.

2. Enabling Face Login

After your KYC is submitted or when you log in with your PIN, you will be asked if you want to enable Biometric Login.

Step 1: Enable Fingerprint

Tap "Enable Fingerprint" to allow the app to store your PIN securely.

Step 2: Scan Now

Tap "Scan Now" to register your face for login on this device. Position your face in the camera view until the scan is complete.

3. Logging In with Face

Step 1: Mobile Number

Enter your mobile number on the login screen.

Step 2: Face Icon

On the PIN screen, tap the Face Icon (👤).

Step 3: Verify

The camera will open. Look at the camera and follow any prompts. If the match is successful, you will be logged in immediately.

Privacy Assurance

We do not store your photos for identification. Instead, we create a secure "digital fingerprint" of your face that cannot be turned back into a picture. Your data is encrypted and protected under the Data Privacy Act.
