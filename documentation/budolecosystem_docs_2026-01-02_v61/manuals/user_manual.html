User Manual - v61

New Checkout Experience (BudolPay + Lalamove)

We've streamlined the order placement process for a smoother "Budol" experience. Here's what's new for buyers:

1. Enhanced Payment with BudolPay

When you select BudolPay as your payment method, you'll be redirected to a secure QR payment page. The system now automatically recovers your payment details even if you refresh the page or lose your session.

2. Real-time Lalamove Delivery

Shipping quotes are now more accurate. Once your payment is confirmed, a Lalamove driver is automatically requested. You will receive a tracking share link immediately in your order summary, allowing you to watch your "budol" item travel to you in real-time.

3. Order Confirmation

After a successful payment, your order status will update from pending to paid within seconds. You no longer need to manually confirm your payment; the BudolPay webhook handles it for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I close the QR payment page?
A: You can find the link back to your payment in your Order History. The system remembers your order and amount automatically.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after payment?
A: Since Lalamove booking happens instantly after payment, please ensure your address is correct before completing the transaction.
