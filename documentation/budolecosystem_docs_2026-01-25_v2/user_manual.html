User Manual

Selecting a Delivery Provider

During checkout, you can choose between different delivery options such as:

  • Standard Delivery: Our default affordable shipping option.
  • Lalamove: Real-time on-demand delivery with tracking.
  • Bud Express: Our premium internal fast delivery service.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is placed, you can view the selected provider in your Orders page. If you selected Lalamove, you will see the Lalamove logo and a "Track Delivery" link once the booking is confirmed by the seller.
