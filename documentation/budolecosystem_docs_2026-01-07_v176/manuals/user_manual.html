How Backend Services Power Your Experience

The Budol Ecosystem uses several cloud services to ensure your experience is fast, secure, and reliable. Here is how these services affect your daily use:

Instant Notifications

Powered by Pusher and Brevo, you receive real-time alerts for payments, logins, and OTP codes without needing to refresh the app.

Secure Document Verification

Your KYC (Know Your Customer) documents are securely stored in Vercel Blob, ensuring your private data is handled according to bank-grade security standards.

Fast Checkout

Integration with Paymongo allows for seamless and secure credit card and e-wallet payments within the BudolShap application.

High Availability

By using Vercel Postgres and Redis, the platform remains fast and accessible even during periods of high traffic.
