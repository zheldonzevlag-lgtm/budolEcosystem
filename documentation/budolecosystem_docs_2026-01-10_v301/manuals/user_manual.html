User Manual: Understanding Your Access

In the BudolShap ecosystem, your access to features and data is determined by your assigned Role. This ensures a secure and streamlined experience for all users.

Standard Roles

🛒 Buyer

Primary Access: Can browse products, manage their cart, place orders, and track their purchase history. Buyers have access to their personal profile and address book.

🏪 Seller

Primary Access: In addition to Buyer features, Sellers can manage their own store, list products, process incoming orders, and view their sales transactions. Sellers have access to the Seller Dashboard.

🛠️ Staff / Manager

Primary Access: Internal administrative roles that assist in day-to-day operations. This includes viewing orders, managing users, and approving store/product listings. Managers have higher approval authority than Staff.

🛡️ Admin

Primary Access: Full system access. Admins can configure global settings, manage all users and stores, handle financial payouts, and review audit logs for security compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know what my role is?

Your role is automatically assigned based on your account type. If you are a Store owner, you are automatically granted the Seller role. You can see your account status in your Profile settings.

Can I change my role?

Standard roles are fixed based on your account's purpose. If you need administrative access, you must contact the system administrator for elevation.

Why can't I access certain pages?

If you encounter an "Access Denied" or "Unauthorized" message, it means your current role does not have the necessary permissions for that specific action or page. This is a security measure to protect the ecosystem.
