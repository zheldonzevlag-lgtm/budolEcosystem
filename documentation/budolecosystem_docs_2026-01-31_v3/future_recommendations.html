Future Enhancements & Recommendations

1. Dynamic Timeout Configuration

Implement a backend-driven timeout policy that allows admins to adjust the inactivity duration without requiring an app update.

2. Advanced Session Heartbeats

Introduce a heartbeat mechanism between the mobile app and backend to detect network-level session terminations and force logout even when the app is backgrounded.

3. Registration Form Analytics

Add telemetry to the registration form to track dropout rates and identify potential UI/UX friction points beyond technical errors.
