User Manual - Security Features

Automatic Session Lock

For your security, BudolPay will automatically lock your screen after 3 minutes of inactivity. This prevents unauthorized access if you leave your phone unattended.

1 Lock Screen: When the app locks, you will see a "Session Locked" screen.
2 Unlock: Use your biometric (Fingerprint/FaceID) or PIN to unlock and continue.
3 Manual Logout: If you wish to switch accounts or completely exit, tap the "Log Out" link at the bottom of the lock screen. You will be safely returned to the login screen.
