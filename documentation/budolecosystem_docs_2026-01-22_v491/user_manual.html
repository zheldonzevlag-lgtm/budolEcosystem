What's New in v491?

We've made significant improvements to how order statuses are updated. You no longer need to manually refresh your page to see if your payment went through or if your order is ready for shipping.

For Buyers: Instant "To Ship" Transition

Immediately after a successful payment (via QR code, Credit Card, or E-Wallet), your order will automatically move from the Unpaid tab to the To Ship tab. The system will also automatically navigate you to the "To Ship" section so you can track your order's progress right away.

For Sellers: Clearer Status Labels

We've renamed the statuses in your Order Dashboard to be more intuitive:

  • "Needs Booking" is now "To Ship (Pending)" - These are orders that are paid and waiting for you to arrange a courier.
  • "Booked" is now "To Ship (Booked)" - These are orders where a courier has already been assigned.

Realtime Notifications

Whether you are a buyer or a seller, you will see status updates (like "Paid", "Shipped", or "Delivered") happen in realtime without needing to reload the app. Look for the "Order Updated" toast notifications!

Tips for a Smooth Experience

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection to receive realtime updates.
  • If an order doesn't seem to update, try refreshing the page once; the system will sync with the latest server data.
  • Check your email for official order confirmations sent by BudolShap.
