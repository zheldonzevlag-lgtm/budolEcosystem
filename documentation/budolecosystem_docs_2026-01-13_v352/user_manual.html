1. Updated Onboarding Flow

When you open the budolPay app for the first time in this version, you will be greeted with a series of marketing slides showcasing our core services:

  • budolLoan: Easy cash loans.
  • budolShap: Exclusive marketplace deals.
  • budolExpress: Fast delivery and transport.
  • budolPay: Your secure digital wallet.

You can tap NEXT to cycle through the slides or GET STARTED to proceed to the login screen or your home dashboard if you are already signed in.

2. Version Visibility

The app version is now clearly displayed at the bottom of the splash screen. This version is 1.2.61.
