User Manual - v485

1. KYC Verification

The KYC process is now more stable. Users can verify their accounts by uploading a clear photo of their ID and a selfie.

Tip for Success: Ensure you are in a well-lit area when taking your selfie. Avoid wearing hats or glasses that may obscure your face.

2. Troubleshooting Upload Errors

If you previously encountered a "Status 500" error during selfie upload, please try again. This issue has been resolved in the latest backend update.
