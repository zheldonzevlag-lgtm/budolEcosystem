User Manual - v203

What's New in Notifications

1. Real-time Notifications

You will now receive instant notifications via Email and SMS for the following events:

  • Successful Account Creation
  • KYC Verification Approval
  • OTP (One-Time Password) Requests

2. Two-Channel Verification

For enhanced security, some actions may require verification codes sent to both your registered email and mobile number. Ensure both are kept up to date in your profile settings.

3. Troubleshooting

If you do not receive a notification:

  • Check your spam folder (for email).
  • Ensure your mobile signal is strong and the number provided is correct.
  • Wait at least 2 minutes before requesting a new OTP.
