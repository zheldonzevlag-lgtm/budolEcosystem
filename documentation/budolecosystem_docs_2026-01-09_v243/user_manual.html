User Manual - What's New for Users

Improved App Stability

This update focuses on making your wallet experience smoother and more reliable. You should notice fewer "spinning wheels" when checking your balance or making payments.

Reliable Balance Updates

We've fixed an issue where the app would sometimes fail to show your current balance correctly. Your wallet should now reflect your actual funds immediately upon opening the app.

Modified Workflows

Cash-In Security

The Cash-In process has been reinforced with better validation. When adding funds to your wallet, the app now ensures your chosen provider is always correctly selected before proceeding, preventing transaction errors.

Money Transfer

The "Send Money" feature is now faster and more consistent. We've optimized how the app communicates with our servers to ensure your transfers are processed without delays.

App Version

You can verify you are on the latest version by looking at the splash screen when the app starts. It should display v1.2.10.
