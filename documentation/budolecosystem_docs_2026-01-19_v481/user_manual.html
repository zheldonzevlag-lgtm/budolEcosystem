New Feature: Taking Screenshots

How to take a screenshot

You can now capture any screen in the budolPay app using your phone's standard screenshot buttons (usually Power + Volume Down).

Recommended Uses:

  • Payment Receipts: Capture the success screen after a transfer for your records.
  • QR Codes: Screenshot your "Receive" QR code to share it with others via messaging apps.
  • Transaction History: Keep a copy of your recent activity.
Security Tip: Avoid sharing screenshots that contain your full account number or sensitive personal information with unauthorized individuals.
