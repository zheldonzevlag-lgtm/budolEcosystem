User Manual

What's new in budolPay version 1.2.48.

New Features & Improvements

Improved Login Reliability

We've enhanced the login process to be more robust. If you've experienced issues logging in or being identified by the system, this update should resolve those problems.

Better Network Compatibility

The app now works better with custom network setups. If your organization uses non-standard ports for the backend services, the app will now connect correctly.

Version Visibility

You can now verify you are running the latest version (1.2.48) on the splash screen when you open the app.

Biometric Login Reminder

Don't forget to enable Biometric Login in the Settings menu! Once enabled, you can use your fingerprint or face to quickly fill in your PIN on the login screen.

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Toggle Enable Biometrics to ON.
  3. Follow the prompts to verify your identity and save your PIN securely.
  4. Next time you log in, tap the biometric icon next to the PIN field!
