User Manual: Quick Phone Signup

Signing up for Budol Ecosystem is now faster and cleaner. We use your phone number as your primary identity, similar to Lazada or Shopee.

Step 1: Enter Phone Number

Go to the Signup page and enter your 11-digit mobile number starting with 09.

Step 2: Verify OTP

You will receive a 6-digit code via SMS. Enter it to verify your account.

Step 3: Welcome!

Your account is ready. You will see your phone number in the top menu. You can update your name anytime in your Profile settings.
