Improved Document Review

We have improved the document rotation feature in the Admin Panel to ensure your adjustments are always saved and remembered.

How it works: When you rotate an image, a small loading icon will appear. Once it disappears, your change is saved permanently to our servers. You can now close the viewer or refresh the page without losing your work.

Workflow Impact

  • No more repeated rotations for the same document.
  • Consistent document orientation across all administrator accounts.
  • Faster verification process for documents uploaded sideways or upside down.
