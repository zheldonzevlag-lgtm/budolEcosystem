Mitigation Strategies - v351

Marketing Flow Restoration

To mitigate the risk of users missing critical ecosystem updates (Marketing Ads), we have implemented the following strategies:

  1. Navigation-Level: Re-inserted the MarketingAdsScreen into the primary SplashScreen navigation logic, ensuring it acts as a gate for unauthenticated, first-time users.
  2. Persistence-Level: Utilized SharedPreferences to store a persistent hasSeenAds flag. This prevents the marketing flow from becoming a nuisance to returning users while ensuring new users always see it.
  3. UI-Level: Added mounted checks in the _proceedToApp async method in MarketingAdsScreen to prevent crashes during rapid navigation transitions.
