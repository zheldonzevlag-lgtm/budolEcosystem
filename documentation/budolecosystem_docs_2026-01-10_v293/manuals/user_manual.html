v293 User Manual

New Features

LAN Connectivity

You can now access the budolEcosystem applications from any device on your local network. Instead of using localhost, you can use the server's IP address.

Example: http://192.168.1.24:3001 (budolShap)

Unified Login

The login experience is now consistent across all devices. When you click "Login with budolID", you will be redirected to the identity provider, and then back to your original app, regardless of which device you are using.

How to Access from Mobile

  1. Connect your phone to the same Wi-Fi as your computer.
  2. Open your phone's browser.
  3. Enter the IP address of your computer followed by the port (e.g., 192.168.1.24:3001).
  4. You can now use the full functionality of the ecosystem on your mobile device.

Common Questions

What is my server IP?

You can find your server's current IP address in the MOBILE_CONFIG.txt file located in the root folder of the project.

The page won't load on my phone?

Ensure that your computer's firewall is not blocking incoming connections on the specific ports (3000, 3001, 8000).
