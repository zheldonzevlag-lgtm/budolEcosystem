Accessing Your Order History

Follow these steps to view your recent purchases in the BudolShap application.

Step 1: Log In

Ensure you are logged into your account using your registered email address.

Step 2: Navigate to My Orders

Click on the "My Orders" link in the navigation menu or user profile dropdown.

Step 3: Check Order Status

Your orders should now appear under the "Processing" or "All" tabs immediately after payment is confirmed.

Troubleshooting

If your order does not appear within 5 minutes of a successful payment, please contact support with your Transaction ID from BudolPay.
