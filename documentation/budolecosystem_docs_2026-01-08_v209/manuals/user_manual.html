2. How to Log In

1

Visit an Ecosystem App

Go to budolShap or any other participating app.

2

Click "Login with budolID"

You will be automatically redirected to the secure budolID login page.

3

Enter Credentials

Enter your ecosystem email and password. Your account is the same across all apps.

4

Successful Redirect

Once authenticated, you'll be redirected back to your original app, already logged in!

3. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a different account for each app? ↓

No! One budolID account works for all budolEcosystem applications.

Is my password secure? ↓

Yes. We use industry-standard hashing (bcrypt) and secure session management (JWT) to protect your data.
