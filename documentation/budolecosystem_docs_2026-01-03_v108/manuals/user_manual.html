The Original budolPay is Back!

We've listened to your feedback! The budolPay mobile app has been restored to its original, premium design. You'll recognize the signature "Rose Red" colors and the iconic Philippine Peso logo.

Key Visual Changes

  • Circular Peso Logo: The login screen now features the stylish circular ₱ badge.
  • Rose Red Theme: All buttons, headers, and highlights are back in our signature Rose Red color.
  • Modern Typography: Clean, bold fonts for better readability of your balance and transactions.
  • App Icon: The app icon on your phone now correctly displays the budolPay brand.

Using the App

The core functionality remains the same but with a better look:

  1. Login: Use your budolID (email and password).
  2. View Balance: See your real-time wallet balance on the new home screen.
  3. Scan to Pay: Tap the red "Scan & Pay" button to scan a merchant's QR code.
