Improved Face Registration

The face registration process is now more reliable and provides better feedback:

  • Detection Feedback: The app will now explicitly tell you if it cannot see your face clearly in the photo.
  • Better Accuracy: Internal improvements ensure that your face profile is captured with higher precision for faster future logins.
  • Lighting Tips: For best results, ensure you are in a well-lit area and looking directly at the front camera.
