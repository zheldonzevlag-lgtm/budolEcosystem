Executive Summary

This release fixes a critical failure in the Face Registration process where the app would fail to generate a face embedding after capturing a photo. The fix involved correcting the internal asset path for the FaceNet TFLite model and integrating Google ML Kit Face Detection to ensure a face is actually present and clearly visible before attempting to process it. This significantly improves the reliability of the biometric setup process.
