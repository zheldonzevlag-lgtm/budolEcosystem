User Manual - v52

Topic: Improved Payment Experience & Profile Management

Reliable Checkout Process

We have improved the checkout experience to ensure that technical errors with payment providers do not block your orders during development and testing phases.

1. Flexible Payment Methods

The checkout system now accurately recognizes "BudolPay" and other payment methods regardless of how they are selected, reducing "invalid method" errors at the final step.

2. Smooth Mock Payments

In the event of a connection issue with our external payment partner (PayMongo), the system will now automatically guide you to a local payment verification page, allowing you to complete your test orders without interruption.

budolPay Profile Synchronization

Administrators of the budolPay platform can now update their profile details with confidence. Just like in budolShap, your name changes are now synchronized across the entire ecosystem.

1. Update Your Profile

Go to your user settings in the budolPay Admin Panel, update your name, and click save.

2. Unified Identity

Your updated name will automatically be reflected in budolID and any other connected services the next time you log in.
