Strategic Recommendations

  • Automated Schema Validation: Integrate a CI/CD check that validates the database schema against the Prisma definition before any deployment.
  • Enhanced Error Monitoring: Implement a centralized logging system (like Winston or Sentry) to catch and alert on 500 errors in real-time.
  • OTP Provider Fallbacks: Configure multiple SMS providers (e.g., ZERIX and iTextMo) to ensure high availability of the login service.
  • Device Trust Improvements: Refine the device identification logic in the Flutter app to reduce the frequency of OTP requests for trusted users.
