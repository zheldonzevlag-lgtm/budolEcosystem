End-User Login Guide

Logging into budolPay Mobile (Web)

Step 1: Access the Application
Open your web browser and navigate to http://192.168.1.17:8080.
Step 2: Enter Mobile Number
Enter your registered mobile number (e.g., 09484099388) on the login screen and click "Next".
Step 3: Verify OTP
If your device is not recognized or you haven't set a PIN, you will be prompted for an OTP. Enter the 6-digit code sent to your mobile.
Step 4: Enter PIN
Once verified, enter your 6-digit PIN to access your account dashboard.

Troubleshooting

If you see an "Identification failed: 500" error, please contact your system administrator. This usually indicates a temporary server-side issue that has been resolved in v342.
