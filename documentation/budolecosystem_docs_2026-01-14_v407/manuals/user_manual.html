User Manual: Mobile Security & Login Protection

📱 Secure Mobile Login

Your BudolPay mobile app uses bank-grade security for every login. Whether you use your 6-digit PIN, Face Recognition, or Fingerprint, your access is protected and monitored for your safety.

🛡️ Forensic Activity Tracking

Every time you log into the mobile app, our system creates a secure, permanent record of the login attempt. This includes the time of login and the specific device used. This "Forensic Audit Trail" helps us protect you by identifying any unusual login patterns from unknown devices.

✅ Trusted Device Policy

In line with our "1 Device → 1 Account" policy, the app verifies that you are logging in from your registered trusted device. If you log in from a new device, it is automatically recorded in your security history, allowing our team to verify the legitimacy of the access.

🤐 Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. While we log *when* and *how* you log in (e.g., via Face Recognition), we **never** store or see your actual face data or fingerprints. Those remain securely stored only on your own device.
