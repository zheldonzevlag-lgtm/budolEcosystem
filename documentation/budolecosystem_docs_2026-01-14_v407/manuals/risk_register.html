Risk Register (v407)

This register tracks risks specific to mobile authentication audit trails and biometric data handling.

Risk ID Description Impact Probability Mitigation Strategy
R-407-01 Mobile device ID spoofing could lead to inaccurate audit logs for trusted devices. High Medium Implement hardware-backed device attestation (SafetyNet/Play Integrity) to verify device authenticity in v415.
R-407-02 Increased volume of login audit logs from high-frequency mobile usage. Medium High Enable asynchronous audit logging in Auth Service to prevent blocking the login response (Implemented in v407).
R-407-03 Audit logs capturing failed biometric attempts could be used for brute-force analysis. Medium Low Implement rate limiting on biometric verification endpoints based on userId and deviceId.
R-407-04 Inconsistent deviceId reporting between different Android OS versions. Low Medium Standardized on android_id or uuid generated on first install and stored in secure storage.
