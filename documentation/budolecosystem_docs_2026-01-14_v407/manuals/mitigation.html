Mitigation Strategies (v407)

M-407-01: Device-Locked Audit Trails IMPLEMENTED

Risk: Mobile login events being attributed to the correct user but wrong device.

Mitigation: Every MOBILE_LOGIN_PIN and MOBILE_LOGIN_BIOMETRIC event now explicitly captures the deviceId from the request body. This ensures that forensic analysts can trace activity back to a specific physical handset.

M-407-02: Non-Blocking Audit Persistence IMPLEMENTED

Risk: High login volume causing latency in authentication responses due to synchronous audit logging.

Mitigation: The createAuditLog calls in the auth-service are executed asynchronously. The login response is sent to the user immediately, while the audit log persistence happens in the background, ensuring zero impact on UX performance.

M-407-03: Biometric Data Privacy Compliance VERIFIED

Risk: Accidental logging of biometric templates or raw data in audit logs (Violation of NPC/GDPR).

Mitigation: Auditing is strictly limited to the *fact* of authentication (success/failure) and the *method* used. No biometric features, face templates, or fingerprint hashes are permitted in the AuditLog metadata.
