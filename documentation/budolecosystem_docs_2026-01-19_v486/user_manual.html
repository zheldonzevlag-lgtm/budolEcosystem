User Manual - Universal Payment Interface

Welcome to the new budolEcosystem universal payment interface. This update introduces a standardized, more secure, and user-friendly way to pay across all our platforms.

Step 1: Initiate Payment

When you checkout on budolShap or any partner app, a Scan QR Code to Pay modal will appear.

Step 2: Check the Details

Review the amount, the merchant (budolShap Owner), and your unique Payment ID in the details card.

Step 3: Pay with budolPay

Open your budolPay app, select "Scan QR", and point your camera at the screen. Alternatively, you can take a screenshot of the QR code and upload it to the app.

Step 4: Complete within 10 Minutes

Each payment session is valid for 10 minutes. A timer at the bottom shows the remaining time. If the time expires, you will need to refresh the page to generate a new QR code.
