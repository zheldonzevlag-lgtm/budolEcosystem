User Manual: Accessing the Ecosystem

Overview

You can now access all budolEcosystem apps from any device in your home or office network without manual setup.

Step 1: Connect to LAN

Ensure your mobile phone, tablet, or secondary computer is connected to the same Wi-Fi or Ethernet network as the budolEcosystem server.

Step 2: Access via Browser

On any computer in the network, open your browser and go to:

  • budolShap: http://192.168.1.24:3001
  • budolPay Admin: http://192.168.1.24:3000

Step 3: Mobile App Discovery

Open the budolPay Mobile app. It will automatically search the network and connect to the server. No IP address entry is required!

SSO Experience

When you log in to budolShap, you will be redirected to the centralized budolID login page. After successful authentication, you will be returned to the app automatically.
