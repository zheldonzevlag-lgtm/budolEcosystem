Future Enhancements & Recommendations

Technical Recommendations

  • Event Compression: For high-traffic environments, compress the event payload or use binary formats (Protobuf) to reduce bandwidth.
  • Persistent Connections: Explore gRPC or long-lived WebSockets for server-to-server notifications to reduce overhead of HTTP POST triggers.
  • Admin-Only Channels: Move the 'admin' channel to a Private/Presence channel in Pusher to ensure only authorized users can listen to security events.

Compliance Recommendations

  • Audit Log Signing: Implement cryptographic signing for each audit log entry to ensure immutability (Digital Notarization).
  • Cold Storage: Automatically move audit logs older than 180 days to encrypted cold storage (S3/Glacier) to meet long-term retention requirements.
