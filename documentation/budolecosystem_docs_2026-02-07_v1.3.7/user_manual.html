User Manual: What's New in v1.3.7

We've introduced tiered seller verification to better support both individual entrepreneurs and established businesses.

1. Choose Your Seller Tier

When creating a store, you can now choose between Individual or Business tiers. This ensures you only provide the documents relevant to your business type.

Tip: Business sellers get access to professional branding and advanced tax reporting features!

2. Simplified KYC Process

Our updated verification process now includes Smart Data Extraction. When you upload your ID or business permits, the system automatically reads the information for you, making it faster to get your store approved and reducing typing errors.

3. Accessing Your Verification Status (Profile UI)

We've made it easier to stay compliant with our new Compliance Shield integrated into your profile dashboard and navigation menu.

  • Navigation Menu: Your verification status is now visible directly in the top-right user menu. A green shield means you're all set!
  • Profile Dashboard: Visit your Profile page to see the new Identity Verification section. This shows exactly what documents are needed and provides a clear path to full verification.

4. Seamless Phone Registration (v1.3.6)

Registering with your phone is now faster! After signing up, you'll be taken directly to the verification code screen without any extra steps.
