Future Recommendations: v1.3.7

1. Phase 2: Multi-Step KYC Wizard

Implement a reactive multi-step form for KYC submission that dynamically adjusts fields based on the selected sellerType. This will improve completion rates for business sellers who have more complex document requirements.

2. Government API Integration

Explore official API integrations with the BIR and DTI portals to automate the verification of TIN and Business Registration numbers, reducing the manual workload for system administrators.

3. AML/Sanction List Screening

Integrate an automated AML (Anti-Money Laundering) screening service to cross-reference new seller applications against global sanction lists, ensuring compliance with international financial regulations.

Gap Resolution Strategies

Resolving Gap 1: Operational KYC Triggers

To align with Shopee's compliance model, BudolShap will implement "Just-In-Time" KYC triggers. Instead of waiting for a withdrawal request, the system will prompt for KYC when a user attempts to activate features that involve financial movement, such as "Refund to Wallet" or "P2P Credit Transfer". This ensures the platform is never used for unverified financial transactions.

Resolving Gap 2: Legal Business Entity Onboarding

To resolve the lack of a formal business track, Phase 1 and 2 of the roadmap introduce the SellerType.BUSINESS entity. This track will require mandatory OCR-verified uploads of the BIR 2303 (Certificate of Registration) and DTI/SEC permits. Failure to provide these will restrict the store to the "Individual" tier with lower transaction limits and no tax-invoicing capabilities.
