Ecosystem Expansion: budoLoan

budoLoan is the lending arm of the budol ecosystem, providing credit facilities to users and merchants based on their shopping and payment history.

Fintech Synergy: budoLoan + budolPay

  • Instant Disbursement: Approved loans are instantly credited to the user's budolPay wallet.
  • Automated Repayment: Monthly repayments are automatically deducted from the budolPay balance.
  • Credit Scoring: budoLoan uses transaction history from budolPay to determine loan eligibility.

Technical Implementation:

  • Decoupled Database: budoLoan has its own database for loan terms, interest rates, and repayment schedules.
  • Shared Identity: Uses budolID for SSO, ensuring the userId is consistent across all apps.
  • API Integration: Uses secure server-to-server calls to wallet-service for funds transfer.
