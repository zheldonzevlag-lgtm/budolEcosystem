Mitigation Strategies

M-473-01: UX Clarity

Strategy: Provide a clear Logout button in the settings and ensure that other navigation items (History, Wallet) still allow back navigation to Home.

M-473-02: Legacy Compatibility

Strategy: Used PopScope which has a fallback mechanism for older Flutter/Android versions, ensuring the app doesn't crash even if the intercept fails on very old devices.
