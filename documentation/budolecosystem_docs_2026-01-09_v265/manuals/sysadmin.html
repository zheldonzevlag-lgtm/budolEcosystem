Updated Branding Standards

The budolEcosystem branding for marketing materials now specifies a "Slate" color for the "budol" prefix. This ensures a consistent look and feel across all platform services while allowing individual services to retain their unique identity through their specific theme colors.

Brand Color Palette

  • Prefix (budol): Slate 400 (#94A3B8)
  • Service Suffixes:
    • Loan: Blue (#3B82F6)
    • Shap: Emerald (#10B981)
    • Express: Amber (#F59E0B)

Operational Changes

No backend or infrastructure changes were required for this update. The branding logic is contained within the mobile application's presentation layer.
