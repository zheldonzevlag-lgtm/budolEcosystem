New Look for Service Names

Refined Branding

We've updated the appearance of our service names in the introduction slides. The "budol" part of our names now features a sleek, professional slate color, making the service name (like Loan, Shap, or Express) stand out even more.

Visual Examples

When you explore the app's features, you'll see the names styled like this:

  • budolLoan
  • budolShap
  • budolExpress

This subtle change provides a more premium feel to the app's onboarding experience.
