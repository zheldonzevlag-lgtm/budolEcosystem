GoTyme-Style Instant Login

Experience the fastest login flow yet. No buttons to press, no steps to waste.

1
Identify Yourself

Enter your phone number as usual.

2
Instant Biometric Prompt

As soon as you reach the PIN screen, the app will automatically trigger your face recognition or fingerprint scanner. No need to tap any icons!

3
Passive Fallback

If you prefer to use your PIN, simply cancel the biometric prompt and enter your 6 digits manually. The app respects your choice.
