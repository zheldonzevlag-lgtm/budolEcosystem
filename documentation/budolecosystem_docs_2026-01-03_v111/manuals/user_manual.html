budolPay 1.0 Official Release

Welcome to the official 1.0 release of budolPay! This version features the updated branding and the official app name.

How to Install

  1. Transfer the app-release.apk file to your Android device.
  2. Open the file on your device and follow the prompts to install.
  3. If prompted, allow installation from "Unknown Sources" in your device settings.

App Branding

  • App Name: The app will appear as "budolPay 1.0" on your home screen.
  • Icon: Look for the signature Peso badge logo.

Support

If you encounter any issues during installation or use, please refer to the technical support team or check your internet connectivity.
