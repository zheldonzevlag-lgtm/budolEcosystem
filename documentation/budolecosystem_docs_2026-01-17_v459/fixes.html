v459 Issue Fix Log

Issue: Post-Splash Black Screen (Web)

Description: App displays a black screen after the Flutter splash screen completes, failing to show marketing ads or login UI.

Root Cause: Potential race condition between HTML splash removal and Flutter engine paint, or zero-constraint layout in MarketingAdsScreen.

Fix: Rendering Guard & Robust Layout

Solution: Implemented a Material wrapper and LayoutBuilder safety checks. Added a diagnostic overlay and manual override buttons in SplashScreen to ensure the user can always proceed.

Result: Improved visibility and reliability of the transition from splash to marketing/login screens.
