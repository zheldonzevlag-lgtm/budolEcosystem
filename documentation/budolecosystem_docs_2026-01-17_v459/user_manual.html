What's New for Users

In this update, we have improved the initial experience when you first open the BudolEcosystem app on the web.

1. Smoother Marketing Experience

The marketing slides that introduce you to the ecosystem (BudolLoan, BudolExpress, etc.) now load more reliably. We've added a modern page indicator at the bottom to show your progress through the slides.

2. Faster Navigation

The transition from the splash screen (the initial loading screen) to the main app is now faster and more stable, especially on slower internet connections.

3. Clearer Branding

  • BudolLoan: Highlighted in Blue.
  • BudolExpress: Highlighted in Amber.
  • BudolEcosystem: Highlighted in Slate.

Common Troubleshooting

If you encounter a black screen after the loading animation, please refresh your browser. The app is now designed to recover automatically from these situations.
