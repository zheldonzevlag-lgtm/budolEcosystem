Payment Process Update

When paying via BudolPay QR, you will now see the correct merchant owner name displayed in the payment modal. This ensures clarity and trust during the transaction process.

How to Pay via QR

  1. Select your items and proceed to checkout.
  2. Choose BudolPay QR as your payment method.
  3. Click Place Order.
  4. A modal will appear with a QR code.
  5. Look for the budolShap Owner field. It should now show Jon Galvez.
  6. Scan the QR code with your BudolPay app to complete the payment.
