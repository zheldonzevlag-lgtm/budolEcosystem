User Manual: Consistent Amount Entry

1. Improved Cash In & Cash Out v1.2.97

Automatic Formatting Everywhere

Whether you are Cashing In to your wallet or Cashing Out to your bank account, the amount field now automatically handles commas and decimals. Just type the numbers, and budolPay will format it for you (e.g., "250000" becomes "2,500.00").

2. Why this matters

Consistency across all screens reduces the chance of entry errors, ensuring you always send or receive exactly the amount you intended.

3. Version Check

Ensure your app shows v1.2.97 on the splash screen to enjoy these latest improvements.
