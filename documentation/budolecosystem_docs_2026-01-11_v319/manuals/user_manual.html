How to use biometric login (fingerprint/face) in budolPay.

Introduction

Biometric login allows you to access your budolPay account quickly and securely using your fingerprint or face recognition, without having to type your PIN every time.

How to Enable Biometric Login

1
Open the budolPay app and log in with your PIN.
2
Tap on the Settings icon in the navigation bar.
3
Find the Enable Biometric Login toggle and switch it on.
4
A confirmation message will appear. Your biometric login is now ready!
Note: Your PIN will be securely linked to your biometrics the next time you log in successfully.

Logging in with Biometrics

Once enabled, follow these steps to log in:

1
On the Login Screen, tap the Fingerprint/Face icon located near the PIN entry field.
2
Follow your phone's prompt to scan your fingerprint or face.
3
If successful, your PIN will be filled automatically and you will be logged in.

How to Disable Biometric Login

If you wish to stop using biometrics, simply go back to Settings and toggle the Enable Biometric Login switch off. This will immediately erase any stored credentials from your device's secure vault.

Troubleshooting

  • Icon not responding: Ensure you have enabled biometrics in the Settings menu first.
  • Authentication failed: Make sure your fingers are clean/dry or your face is well-lit. You can always log in using your manual PIN if biometrics fail.
  • Device not supported: If your device doesn't have biometric hardware, the option will be hidden or disabled.
