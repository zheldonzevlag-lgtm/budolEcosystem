Accessing the Platform from other devices

To access BudolShap or BudolPay from your mobile phone on the same Wi-Fi:

  1. Open your mobile browser.
  2. Navigate to http://[HOST_IP]:3001 (for BudolShap).
  3. Login using your BudolID credentials.

Note: Ensure your computer and mobile device are on the same network subnet.
