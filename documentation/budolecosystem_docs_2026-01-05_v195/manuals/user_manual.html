Subject: Getting Started with your Universal budolPay Account

New Registration Flow

Joining the budolEcosystem is now easier than ever. Follow these steps to create your universal account:

Step 1: Mobile Number

Enter your active mobile number. This will serve as your primary identifier for receiving money and logging in.

Step 2: Personal Profile

Provide your full name and a valid email address. We'll use this for security notifications and account recovery.

Step 3: Secure Password

Create a strong password to protect your funds. Once completed, your account is ready to use!

One Account, Many Apps

Your new budolPay account is Universal. This means you can use the same login credentials to access any application within the budolEcosystem (e.g., budolStore, budolPay Admin, etc.) without having to register again.

Security Tips

  • Never share your password or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from budolPay.
  • Enable biometric login in the settings menu for faster and more secure access.
  • Verify the identity of recipients before sending money.
