Scanning QR Codes (Web/Mobile)

To use the "Scan QR" feature on your mobile browser, you must ensure camera permissions are enabled. Since the app is currently served over HTTP, most browsers will block camera access by default.

⚠️ Camera Access on HTTP

If you see a camera error icon, follow these steps on your mobile Chrome browser:

  1. Open chrome://flags in your mobile browser.
  2. Search for "Insecure origins treated as secure".
  3. Enable the flag and add http://92.168.1.24:8081 (or your current server IP) to the text area.
  4. Relaunch Chrome.

Once enabled, the app will be able to access your device camera for scanning.

Alternatively, for a seamless experience without configuration, the app should be deployed to an HTTPS environment (like Vercel).

Logging In

  1. Open the app.
  2. Enter your registered email (e.g., diana.prince@budolpay.com).
  3. Enter your password (default for test accounts: asakapa).
  4. Tap "Login" to access your wallet.
