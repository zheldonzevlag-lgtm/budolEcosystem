User Manual - Version 149

We've introduced new features to help you stay informed about your wallet connection and ensure your balance is always accurate.

1. Connection Status Indicator

Look for the small colored dot in the top right corner of your screen (next to the notification bell):

  • Green: Connected. Your balance will update automatically.
  • Orange: Connecting/Disconnected. The app is trying to reach the server.

2. Pull-to-Refresh Balance

If you feel your balance is not up-to-date, simply swipe down on the home screen. This will manually fetch your latest balance from our servers.

3. Manual Refresh after Scanning

The app now automatically refreshes your balance every time you return to the home screen after successfully scanning a QR code and making a payment.
