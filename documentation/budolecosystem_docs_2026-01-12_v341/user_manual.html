BudolPay Web Experience

You can now access the full BudolPay mobile experience directly from your web browser without installing an app.

How to Access

1. Connect your device to the local network.
2. Open your browser and navigate to http://192.168.1.17:8080.
3. Log in with your BudolPay credentials.

Key Features

Wallet Management: Check balances, cash in, and cash out via the web interface.
QR Scanner: Scan payment QRs directly from your webcam or mobile browser camera.
Marketplace: Browse and purchase items from the BudolShap ecosystem.

PWA Support

The web application supports Progressive Web App (PWA) features. On most mobile browsers, you can select "Add to Home Screen" to install BudolPay as a standalone web app with its own icon.
