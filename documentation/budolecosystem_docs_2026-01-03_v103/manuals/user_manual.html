User Manual: Seamless QR Payments

New Checkout Workflow

Paying with budolPay is now faster and more intuitive. Follow these steps for a seamless checkout experience:

Step 1: Generate QR

On the merchant site, select "Pay with budolPay". A secure QR code will be displayed on your screen.

Step 2: Scan & Pay

Open the budolPay Mobile App and scan the QR code. Confirm the payment details and enter your PIN.

Step 3: Automatic Redirection

Do not close your browser. As soon as the payment is confirmed on your mobile app, the checkout page will automatically update and redirect you back to the merchant's success page.

FAQs

Q: What if the page doesn't redirect?

A: If the page remains on the QR code for more than 10 seconds after payment, please refresh the page or check your transaction history in the mobile app.

Q: I scanned the code but the app showed a white screen.

A: This is a known issue currently being fixed. Your payment may still have gone through. Check your balance in the app. If the balance was deducted, the merchant page will eventually redirect.
