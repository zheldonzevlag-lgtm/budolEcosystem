User Manual: budolID SSO Login

1. Logging in with budolID

Your budolID provides a single, secure identity for all apps in the Budol ecosystem, including BudolPay and BudolShap.

1
On the BudolPay Admin login page, click the "Login with budolID" button.
2
You will be securely redirected to the budolID central login portal.
3
Enter your registered email and password. Your data is protected by budolShield and compliant with the Philippine Data Privacy Act.
4
Upon successful login, you will be automatically redirected back to the app you were originally using (e.g., BudolPay Admin).

2. Security Features

  • Single Identity: No need to remember multiple passwords for different Budol apps.
  • Verified Redirection: The system automatically verifies that you are being redirected to a legitimate Budol application.
  • NPC Compliant: Your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is handled with the highest security standards.
