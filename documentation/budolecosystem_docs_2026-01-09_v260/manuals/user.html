User Manual - Version 260

What's New in v1.2.24?

Personalized Success Notifications

When you successfully send money, the app now displays the Full Name of the recipient instead of their email address. This makes it easier to verify that you've sent money to the right person.

How to Use

  1. Open the budolPay app.
  2. Go to the "Send Money" screen.
  3. Enter the recipient's email or scan their QR code.
  4. Enter the amount and tap "Send".
  5. Upon success, a notification will appear at the bottom: Successfully sent ₱XX.XX to [Full Name].

If the recipient has not set their first or last name, the app will continue to show their email address as a fallback.

Support

For any issues, please contact the system administrator or check the "About" screen to ensure you are running version 1.2.24+24.
