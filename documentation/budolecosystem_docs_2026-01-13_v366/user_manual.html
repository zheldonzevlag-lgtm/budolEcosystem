Setting up Face Login

If you haven't enabled biometrics during your initial login, you can now do so directly from the Settings screen:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Tap on Setup face login.
  3. When prompted, enter your 6-digit MPIN.
  4. Position your face in front of the camera and follow the instructions.

Once registered, you can use your face to log in to the app securely.
