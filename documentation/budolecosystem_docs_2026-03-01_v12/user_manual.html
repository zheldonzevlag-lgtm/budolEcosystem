User Manual: Processing Profile Change Requests

Instructions for Administrator and Staff on managing identity changes using the Maker-Checker workflow.

PCI DSS & BSP COMPLIANCE: Direct edits to staff PII (Phone, Email, Role) are strictly prohibited. All changes must be reviewed by a second authorized personnel (Checker).

1. Initiating a Change (The Maker)

1 Open the Internal Workforce page and click the Edit icon next to a staff member.

2 Verify your identity via the multi-channel OTP (Sent simultaneously to BOTH your registered SMS and Email).

3 Modify the desired fields and click Update Profile.

4 A blue banner will confirm: "Maker-Checker Phase 1 Complete. Awaiting Checker..."

2. Reviewing and Approving (The Checker)

1 Navigate to the Staff Registry. A yellow Approval Queue will appear at the top if there are pending requests.

2 Review the "Proposed Changes" box. Ensure the new values (e.g., role upgrade) are valid according to your department's policy.

3 Important: You cannot approve a request that YOU initiated. The Approve button will be disabled or hidden in such cases.

4 Click Approve to apply the values. The staff profile is now officially updated and synced.

4. Regulatory Rationale (The "Why")

You may wonder why you cannot simply edit a profile directly. BudolPay operates within the regulated financial ecosystem of the Philippines, and we must comply with several legal standards:

  • BSP Circular 808: Requires "Segregation of Duties" to prevent any single person from having enough power to commit fraud without detection.
  • PCI DSS v4.0: Demands strictly controlled access to any system that handles sensitive user data to prevent data breaches.
  • NPC Data Privacy Act: Ensures that every change to a user's personal information is accountable, tracked, and verified.

By using the Maker-Checker workflow, we protect both the company and the staff from unauthorized identity manipulation.
