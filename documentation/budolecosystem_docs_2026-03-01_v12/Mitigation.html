Mitigation Plan: Workforce Security

Insider Threat Mitigation

Control: Dual-Key Authorization (Maker-Checker)

Description: No single administrator has the power to change their own role or another admin's email/phone without secondary verification. This prevents "Account Takeover" by a compromised internal credential.

Data Privacy Assurance

Control: Forensic Audit Trails

Description: Every change request stores the "Details" as a JSON snapshot. This captures the state of the change at the moment of request, preventing "hidden" edits after a Checker has reviewed the initial proposal.

Regulatory Compliance (BSP/NPC)

Control: Soft-Deletion & Archival

Description: Instead of hard-deleting staff records, we use the DEACTIVATED role. This preserves all historical audit logs for 7 years as required by financial regulations.
