User Manual Update v157

P2P Transfer Improvements

Users can now reliably send money to other users using their email address or phone number. The issue where a "Successful" message was immediately followed by a "Failed" message has been resolved.

How to Send Money

  1. Open the Send Money screen in the budolPay app.
  2. Enter the recipient's Email or Phone Number.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to send.
  4. Tap Send.
  5. You will receive a single "Transfer successful" notification once the transaction is complete.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter a "Recipient not found" error, please double-check the email or phone number you entered. Ensure the recipient has a registered budolPay account.
