SSO Re-authentication Guide

Overview

We have updated the security protocols for the budolEcosystem Single Sign-On (SSO). If you were previously logged in, you may experience a one-time "Session Expired" notification.

Steps to Fix Login Issues

1

Clear Browser Cookies

Clear cookies for 192.168.1.24 or budolshap.com to remove old session data.

2

Sign in with budolID

On the login screen, click "Login with budolID". You will be redirected to the secure identity provider.

3

Automatic Redirect

After successful login, you will be automatically returned to your original application with a fresh, secure session.

Need Help?

If you continue to see the "Session Expired" message after following these steps, please contact the system administrator.
