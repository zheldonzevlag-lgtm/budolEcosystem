User Manual - v58.0.0

We have improved the reliability of the checkout process, especially when using modern QR-based payment methods like budolPay.

🚀 Improved Connection Reliability

When placing an order, the system now ensures you stay securely connected to your account throughout the entire payment process. This prevents unexpected "Session Expired" messages when being redirected to payment pages.

1. What to Expect During Checkout

  • Seamless Redirection: After clicking "Place Order", you will be smoothly transitioned to the payment page.
  • Order Continuity: Your cart will be cleared only after the order is successfully registered, and you will find your new order in the "My Orders" section.
  • Email Confirmations: You will receive an email confirmation immediately after placing your order, containing your order details and total amount.

2. Troubleshooting

If you are still redirected to the login page during payment:

  • Ensure you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox).
  • If you are testing on a local network, ensure your browser allows cookies for the IP address you are using.
