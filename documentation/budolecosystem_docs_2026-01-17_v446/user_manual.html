Marketing Ads & Onboarding

When you first open the budolPay web app, you will be presented with our ecosystem services overview.

Automatic Slideshow: The app will automatically cycle through our featured services (budolLoan, budolShap, budolExpress, budolPay, budolAkawntng) every 5 seconds.
Manual Navigation: You can click the NEXT button to advance slides manually, or GET STARTED to proceed to the login screen.
Skip: If you've seen these before, use the Skip button in the top right to go straight to the app.
