Version: 2026-01-01_v37

What is Quick Registration?

Quick Registration allows you to create an account using only your phone number. This is the fastest way to join the Budol Ecosystem.

Step 1: Sign Up

Choose "Quick (Phone Only)" on the registration form. Enter your mobile number and click "Create Account".

Step 2: Receive Your Code NEW

The system will automatically send a 6-digit verification code to:

  • SMS: Direct to your mobile phone.
  • Email: To the temporary email associated with your quick profile.

Step 3: Verify

Enter the 6-digit code on the verification screen. Once verified, you can immediately start using BudolShap!

Step 4: Complete Profile (Optional)

You can upgrade your account to a "Standard" profile anytime in your settings to unlock more features like higher credit limits and merchant capabilities.
