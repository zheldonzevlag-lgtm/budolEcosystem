User Manual: Account Security (1 Device Policy)

Your budolPay account is now more secure than ever. To prevent unauthorized access, you can only use your account on one device at a time.

1. What is the "1 Device" Policy?

Similar to GCash's "Account Secure", budolPay links your account to your specific phone. If you get a new phone or try to log in on another device, you must first "Unlink" your old phone.

2. Switching to a New Phone

1
Log in on your New Phone: Enter your mobile number. The app will detect that you are using a new device.
2
Verify OTP: Enter the 6-digit code sent to your registered mobile number.
3
Face Verification: Follow the on-screen instructions to take a selfie. Our AI will verify your identity to ensure it's really you.
4
Success: Your old phone is now unlinked, and your new phone is the primary device!
Screenshot: Device Unlink Flow

3. Lost or Stolen Phone?

If you no longer have access to your old phone, don't worry! You can still log in on a new device by following the Face Verification steps mentioned above. This ensures that even if someone has your SIM card, they cannot access your account without your face.

5. Rationale & Security Benefits

Purpose: To keep your money and identity safe from hackers who might steal your login details.

Technical Rationale: By locking your account to your phone, even if someone knows your PIN and steals your OTP, they still cannot log in from their own phone. This "Device Binding" acts like a digital key that only you have.

Compliance: This security feature follows the guidelines of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) and the Data Privacy Act (NPC), ensuring that budolPay meets the highest standards for mobile banking security.
