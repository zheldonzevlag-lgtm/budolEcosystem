🔑 Security Best Practices

Even though accounts are synced, your security is our priority. We recommend the following:

  • One-Time PINs (OTP): If you log in from a new device, we'll send a code to your registered phone number for your protection.
  • Biometrics: If your phone supports it, use Fingerprint or Face IDs for even faster and more secure logins!
