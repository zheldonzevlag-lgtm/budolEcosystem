Editing Your Profile

The app now automatically refreshes your profile data when you open the Edit Profile screen. If your email or name was updated elsewhere, it will appear here automatically.

Privacy & Security

Name Visibility: By default, your First Name and Last Name are masked (hidden) for privacy. Tap the 👁️ (eye icon) next to the field to reveal or hide your name.
