User Manual: Optimized Cash Out Experience

1. Improved Cash Out Layout v1.2.98

Amount First, Method Second

We've reordered the Cash Out screen to put the most important step first: entering your amount. Once the amount is set, you can easily select your preferred withdrawal method below it.

Smooth Scrolling and Better Visibility

The screen now supports scrolling, ensuring that the "Confirm Cash Out" button is always reachable and visible, even on smaller devices. We've added extra space at the bottom so that your system navigation buttons won't block the app's action buttons.

2. Version Check

Ensure your app shows v1.2.98 on the splash screen to experience these layout enhancements.
