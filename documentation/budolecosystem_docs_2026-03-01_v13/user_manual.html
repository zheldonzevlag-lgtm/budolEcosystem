User Access Manual

This guide provides the official URLs for accessing the BudolEcosystem services in the production environment.

🛒 Shopping Marketplace (BudolShap)

Access the main marketplace to browse products, manage your cart, and place orders.

https://budolshap.duckdns.org

💳 Payment & Wallet (BudolPay)

Manage your balances, perform transactions, and complete KYC verification.

https://budolpay.duckdns.org

🔐 Identity Provider (BudolID)

Manage your ecosystem account and Single Sign-On (SSO) settings.

https://budolid.duckdns.org

Dashboard Access

Your existing login credentials (email and password) will work across all subdomains seamlessly via BudolID SSO.
