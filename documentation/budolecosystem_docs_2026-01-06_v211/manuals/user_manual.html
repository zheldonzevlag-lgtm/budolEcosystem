User Manual: New Security Features (GoTyme-Aligned)

Purpose

To guide budolPay users through the new, more secure login and registration process designed to protect their financial data.

New Features
  • 6-Digit PIN: Your security code is now 6 digits long, providing 100x more security than a 4-digit code.
  • Device Recognition: Once you verify your phone with an OTP, we'll remember your device for faster login.
  • Biometric Support: You can now use your fingerprint or face to log in on supported devices.
Workflow: First Time Login
  1. Enter your mobile number.
  2. Verify your identity with a 6-digit code sent via SMS.
  3. Set your 6-digit security PIN.
  4. Enable biometrics (optional) for even faster access.
Issues & Fixes

Issue: Not receiving the OTP code.
Fix: Ensure you have a stable signal and click "Resend Code" after 60 seconds.

Risk & Mitigation

Risk: Someone stealing your phone and trying to guess your PIN.
Mitigation: After 3 failed attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes.

Recommendation for Future Enhancement

Purpose: User-controlled security levels.
Explanation: Allow users to toggle "Always require OTP" for all transactions, regardless of device trust.
Reason: Provides peace of mind for high-balance users.
Issues: Higher SMS costs for the platform.
Fixes: Offer this as a premium feature or use email OTP as a free alternative.
Risk: Users forgetting they enabled this and being annoyed by OTPs.
Mitigation: Clear UI indicator when "High Security Mode" is active.
Dependencies: Profile settings UI update.
