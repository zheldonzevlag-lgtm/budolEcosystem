Enhanced Visuals

Vibrant Service Branding

The onboarding screens have been updated with more vibrant and distinct colors for each service. This helps you quickly identify the different features available in the budolEcosystem.

Service Highlighting

When you open the app, you will see the following highlighted services:

  • budolLoan: Highlighted in Blue for financial services.
  • budolShap: Highlighted in Green for shopping and deals.
  • budolExpress: Highlighted in Orange for delivery services.

These colors are designed to make your journey through the budolEcosystem more intuitive and visually engaging.
