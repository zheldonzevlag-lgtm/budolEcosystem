Architectural Decision: Centralized SSO (budolID)

To support a multi-app ecosystem (budolPay, budolShap, budolExpress, budolCare), we have decoupled identity management into a standalone service: budolID.

The "Identity-First" Strategy: Instead of apps sharing a user database, budolID acts as the single source of truth for authentication. Domain-specific apps only store a reference to the userId.

Why this is better:

  • Database Isolation: budolPay handles wallets, budolShap handles orders, and budolID handles credentials. Each app has its own optimized database.
  • Single Sign-On (SSO): Once a user logs into budolID, they are automatically recognized by all authorized ecosystem apps.
  • Unified Security: Security updates (like MFA or password policies) are implemented once in budolID and protect the entire ecosystem.

New Component: budolID-0.1.0

This service is now located at /budolID-0.1.0 and includes:

  • Prisma Schema: Models for User, EcosystemApp, and Session.
  • SSO API: /auth/sso/login for cross-app authentication.
  • Verification API: /auth/verify for apps to validate incoming JWTs.
