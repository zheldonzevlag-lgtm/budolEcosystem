CIC Credit Reporting Compliance Analysis

Subject: Alignment of budolPay's Credit Reporting with the Credit Information Corporation (CIC) Standards.

Version: v208 (2026-01-05)

1. CIC Regulatory Landscape

As a financial institution (EMI/Digital Bank), budolPay is mandated under the Credit Information System Act (CISA) to submit basic credit data of its borrowers to the Credit Information Corporation (CIC).

Credit Information System Act (CISA)

Republic Act No. 9510

Requirement: Mandatory submission of both positive and negative credit data of all borrowers to the CIC centralized database.

Implementation Strategy: Automated monthly batch processing of loan performance, credit limits, and repayment history in the credit-service.

✓ ALIGNED

Monthly Data Submission

CIC Circular No. 01, Series of 2015

Requirement: Submitting entities must transmit data in a specific format (XML/Flat File) by the 20th day of the following month.

Implementation Strategy: Cron-job based data aggregator that generates CIC-compliant XML files. Secure transmission via CIC's dedicated portal.

✓ ALIGNED

Data Quality & Accuracy

CIC Circular No. 2017-01

Requirement: Ensuring the accuracy and timeliness of credit information. Entities are liable for damages caused by incorrect data.

Implementation Strategy: Automated data validation layer that checks for TIN/SSS/GSIS consistency and valid payment dates before submission.

✓ ALIGNED

2. Technical Requirements for CIC Integration

Feature Technical Requirement Data Mapping
Borrower Profile Mandatory TIN, SSS, or GSIS number verification. User.identityNumber
Credit Data Aggregator Aggregating loan disbursements and repayments. Loan.history / Transaction.repayments
XML Generator Mapping internal schema to CIC XML schema. CreditService.generateCICXml()
Secure File Transfer SFTP or HTTPS upload to CIC Portal. CicConnector.upload()

Credit Score Integration (Future Benefit)

By complying with CIC, budolPay can eventually access the CIC database (as an Accessing Entity) to perform better credit risk assessments, allowing us to offer lower interest rates to users with good credit histories from other banks.

3. Security & Data Privacy (CIC Context)

Data transmitted to CIC is encrypted using AES-256. Per the Data Privacy Act (v206), users must be informed during the loan application process that their credit data will be shared with the CIC. This is integrated into the Terms and Conditions of budolPay's credit features.

4. Final Verdict on CIC Compliance

BudolPay v208 is designed to meet the rigorous reporting standards of the CIC. The automated batch submission logic ensures that the platform fulfills its legal obligations under RA 9510 while positioning itself as a responsible lender in the Philippine ecosystem.
