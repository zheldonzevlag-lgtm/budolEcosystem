Verified vs. Basic Features

To provide a secure and compliant experience, budolPay uses a tiered account system. Verify your identity to unlock higher limits and more features.

Feature Basic (Unverified) Fully Verified
Register Account ✅ Allowed ✅ Allowed
Receive Money ✅ Allowed ✅ Allowed
Pay via QR ⚠️ Limited ✅ Full Access
Send Money (P2P) ❌ Restricted ✅ Full Access
Bank Transfers ❌ Restricted ✅ Full Access
Wallet Limit ₱5,000 ₱500,000

How to Verify?

  1. Go to your Profile in the budolPay app.
  2. Select "Verify Identity".
  3. Upload a valid Government ID and take a selfie.
  4. Wait for our team to review your application (usually within 24 hours).
