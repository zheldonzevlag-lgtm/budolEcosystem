User Manual - Version 56

Improved Checkout Experience

Version 56 introduces background improvements to make your shopping experience more stable and reliable, especially when using online payment methods.

🚀 Stabilized Checkout

We've improved how your session is managed during checkout. You are now much less likely to be unexpectedly logged out when placing an order or being redirected to a payment gateway like GCash or Maya.

🛠️ Smarter Error Handling

If there is a temporary network glitch or a brief server issue, the app will now try to keep you logged in instead of immediately asking you to log in again. This ensures you don't lose your cart or progress during critical steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why was I asked to log in again during checkout? If your session actually expires (usually after 7 days of inactivity), we will ask you to log in for your security. However, we've fixed many issues that were causing this to happen prematurely.
  • What should I do if the payment screen doesn't appear? If you aren't redirected to the payment gateway, check your internet connection and try clicking "Place Order" again. Our new logging system helps us identify these issues faster.
