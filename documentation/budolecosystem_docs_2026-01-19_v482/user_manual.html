Managing Your Balance Privacy

Learn how to use the new balance masking feature.

What's New?

To keep your money matters private, your balance is now hidden by default when you open the app.

How to view your balance:

  1. Look for the ₱ •••••• on your home screen.
  2. Tap the Eye Icon (👁️) located just after the masked amount.
  3. Your balance will be revealed instantly.
  4. Tap the icon again to hide it whenever you're in a public place.
Pro Tip: Keeping your balance hidden is a great way to stay safe while commuting or in crowded areas!
