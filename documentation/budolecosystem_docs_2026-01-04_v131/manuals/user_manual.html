User Manual Updates - v131

Real-time Balance Updates

The Home Screen now accurately reflects your wallet balance immediately after a payment. If you previously saw 0.00 after a successful transaction, this has been resolved through improved data synchronization.

Cross-Device Status Sync

When you complete a payment on your mobile app for a budolShap order, the desktop website will now update to "Payment Successful" much more reliably. You no longer need to refresh the page to see your updated order status.

UI Consistency

The budolLoan service icon is now perfectly aligned with other services like "Load" and "Bills", providing a cleaner and more professional look to your mobile dashboard.
