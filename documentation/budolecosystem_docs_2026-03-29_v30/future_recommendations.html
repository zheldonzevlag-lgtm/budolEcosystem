Scalability: Redis Support

Transition from local Socket.io memory to Redis-backed store to support horizontal scaling across multi-region Render deployments.

Priority: High

Observability: Ecosystem Dashboard

Implement a real-time monitor for fallback events to identify how often Pusher/Polling is active compared to Socket.io.

Priority: Medium

Service Availability: Keep-Alive Microservice

Develop a simple "heartbeat" service that pings the Render instance every 14 minutes to prevent spin-down during critical business hours.

Priority: Medium
