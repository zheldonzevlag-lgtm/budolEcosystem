User Manual

This guide explains the changes in the BudolPay Mobile App and BudolShap Web Login.

1. Logging in to BudolShap Web

New! You can now log in using your registered Email Address even if you created your account via BudolPay or BudolID.

How to Log In

  1. Go to the BudolShap login page.
  2. Enter your registered Email Address.
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Click Login.

Note: If you recently changed your password on another Budol Ecosystem app, use that new password here. The system will automatically update your local credentials.

2. Using Phone Numbers in BudolPay Mobile

The BudolPay app now automatically formats your phone number correctly.

Registration & Login

  • You can enter your number in any format (e.g., 09171234567, 9171234567, +639171234567).
  • The app will automatically convert it to the standard format (+639171234567) before sending it to the server.
  • Ensure your number starts with a valid Philippine prefix (09, 9, 639, +639).

Updating Your Profile

Currently, you can update your Name and Email via the "Edit Profile" screen. To change your phone number, please contact support as this feature is coming soon.

3. Troubleshooting

  • "Invalid Phone Number": Check if you entered the correct number of digits. The app expects 10 digits after the country code.
  • "Login Failed": Ensure your email is verified. Check your inbox for a verification link if you just registered.
  • "Session Expired": Log out and log back in to refresh your session.
