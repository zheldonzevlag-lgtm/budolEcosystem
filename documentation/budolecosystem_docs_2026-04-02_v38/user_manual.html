v38 User Manual: Updating Your Profile

How to Update Your Profile Picture

In v38, updating your profile picture is easier and more secure with our new guided capture system.

STEP 1

Open the BudolPay app and navigate to your Profile tab.

STEP 2

Tap Edit Profile at the top of the screen.

STEP 3

Tap your Profile Picture or the small camera icon next to it.

STEP 4

The camera will open with an Oval Frame. Align your face within the frame and tap the camera button.

STEP 5

Your photo will be uploaded automatically. Once complete, your new profile picture will appear immediately!

Tips for a Great Profile Selfie

  • Ensure you are in a well-lit area.
  • Remove glasses or hats if possible for better clarity.
  • Keep your face centered inside the pink glow frame.
