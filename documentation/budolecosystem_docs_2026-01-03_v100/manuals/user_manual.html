User Manual: Your Transaction IDs

What's New in Your Receipts?

Starting with this version, your transaction reference numbers will look slightly different. This change is part of our commitment to security and system reliability.

New Reference Format

Example: JON-1767385000000-A7B2

  • JON-: Our internal identification prefix.
  • 1767385000000: A timestamp representing when your transaction was started.
  • A7B2: A unique random code to ensure no two transactions ever get mixed up.

Why did this change?

This "random code" at the end ensures that even if millions of people shop at the exact same millisecond, every single order gets its own unique identity. It prevents errors during payment and makes tracking your orders faster and more secure.
