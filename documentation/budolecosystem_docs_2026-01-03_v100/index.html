Published: 2026-01-03 | Version: 100 Robust Uniqueness Improvement

Purpose

This version addresses the uniqueness and safety of the referenceId generation. By adding a random alphanumeric suffix to the timestamp-based ID, we ensure that even under high concurrency, collisions are statistically impossible. This version marks a significant milestone in system stability.
