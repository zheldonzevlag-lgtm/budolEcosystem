User Manual

Welcome to the budolPay Ecosystem. This guide helps you navigate the platform's core features.

Getting Started

1
Login: Access the portal using your email and password. Admins use the primary admin portal.
2
Dashboard: View your transaction summary, active disputes, and system status at a glance.
3
Security: Always ensure you are on a secure connection (HTTPS).

Key Features

Wallet Management

Monitor your balance in real-time. Supports PHP by default. All transactions are logged for transparency.

Dispute Resolution

If a transaction has issues, you can open a dispute. Provide evidence and track resolution status in the Disputes tab.

Ecosystem Apps

Seamlessly switch between budolPay, budolShap, and budolExpress using your single sign-on (SSO) account.

Common Issues

  • Forgot Password: Contact your system administrator for a password reset.
  • Transaction Pending: Most transactions settle instantly. If pending for > 24 hours, open a dispute.
  • Access Denied: Ensure your KYC status is "VERIFIED" to access full ecosystem features.

Support

Need help? Contact our support team at support@budolpay.com or visit the admin office during business hours.
