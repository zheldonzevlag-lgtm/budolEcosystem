User Manual - Payment Guide

Choosing Your Payment Method

BudolShap supports a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient. Here is how each method works:

GCash

When you select GCash, you will be redirected to the official GCash login page. Enter your mobile number and MPIN to authorize the payment. Once successful, you will be automatically returned to BudolShap.

Maya / GrabPay / QRPh

Selecting these methods will generate a unique QR code on your screen. Open your payment app (Maya, Grab, or any QRPh-compliant banking app), scan the code, and confirm the payment. The page will update automatically once the payment is detected.

BudolPay

Our internal wallet system. You will be redirected to the BudolPay portal to complete your transaction using your wallet balance.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Pay for your items when they arrive at your doorstep. No online payment is required at checkout.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is QRPh? QRPh is the national QR code standard in the Philippines. You can scan our QRPh codes using almost any local banking or e-wallet app (BPI, BDO, UnionBank, GCash, Maya, etc.).
  • What if I close the QR code page? Don't worry! You can find your pending order in the "My Orders" section and retry the payment if it hasn't expired.

Support

If you encounter any issues during payment, please take a screenshot of your transaction and contact us at support@budolshap.com.
