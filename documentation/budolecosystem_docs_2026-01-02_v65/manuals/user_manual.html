1. New Brand Identity

The budolPay mobile app has a refreshed look! The new branding features the Philippine Peso (₱) sign, emphasizing our commitment to local financial services.

budolPay

1.1 What's New?

  • Philippine Peso Logo: The login screen now prominently displays the ₱ sign in a stylish circular badge.
  • Refined Colors: The "budol" part of our name is now a subtle slate gray, while "Pay" remains in our signature rose red.
  • Improved Web View: When accessing the app via a browser, the view is now perfectly sized to match a real iPhone 17 Pro, making it easier to navigate on a laptop or desktop.
