User Manual: KYC ID Verification

This guide explains how to complete your identity verification in the BudolPay app.

Verification Steps

Step 1: Document Selection

Open the verification screen and select your ID type (Passport, Driver's License, or National ID).

Step 2: Upload Document

Submit a clear photo of your ID. Ensure all text is readable and the photo is not blurry.

Step 3: Status Tracking

Your verification will be marked as PENDING. Once reviewed by the compliance team, your status will update to VERIFIED.
