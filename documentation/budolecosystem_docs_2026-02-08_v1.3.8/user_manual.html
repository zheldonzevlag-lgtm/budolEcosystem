User Manual: Simplified KYC Process

Welcome to the new, simplified BudolShap KYC verification process. We've reduced the steps to make your onboarding as fast as possible.

1 Choose Your Tier

Select the account type that best fits your needs. Each tier has different requirements and benefits.

  • Standard Buyer: Basic account for purchasing.
  • Individual Seller: For solo entrepreneurs.
  • Business Seller: For registered companies.

2 Upload Documents

Provide the required documents and complete the security verification.

  • Government ID: Upload a clear photo of your ID (UMID, Passport, or Driver's License). We've enhanced the preview size to help you ensure the photo is readable.
  • Liveness Check: Follow the prompts for a quick facial scan to verify your identity.
  • AI Extraction: Watch in real-time as our AI extracts data from your ID.

Click Review & Continue once your documents are uploaded and liveness is verified.

3 Review & Submit

In the final step, review a summary of your verification request.

  • Confirm the detected Name and ID Number.
  • Verify the attached document previews.

Click Confirm & Submit to send your application to our administrative team.

4 Wait for Approval

Our administrative staff will review your submission within 1-3 business days. You will receive a notification once your account is verified.
