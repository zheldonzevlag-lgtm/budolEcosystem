Mitigation Plan: KYC Risk Management

M-KYC-001: Mandatory Admin Audit

Risk: OCR Accuracy issues.

Mitigation: No account is automatically verified based on OCR data alone. All submissions must be reviewed and approved by an admin staff member who can cross-reference the raw OCR lines with the uploaded document photo.

M-KYC-002: Client-Side Processing

Risk: Privacy of sensitive ID data.

Mitigation: All OCR processing (Tesseract.js) is performed locally in the user's browser. Sensitive text data is only transmitted to our servers after the user clicks "Submit", ensuring no third-party OCR services are used.

M-KYC-003: Biometric Liveness Simulation

Risk: Liveness check bypass.

Mitigation: The system uses Shopee-style biometric liveness prompts (e.g., animated pulse, real-time facial framing) to discourage the use of static photos. Future updates will include server-side liveness validation.

M-KYC-004: Audit Log (ocrLines)

Risk: Compliance and auditing.

Mitigation: The raw output of the OCR scan (ocrLines) is stored alongside the extracted fields. This provides a complete audit trail of what the AI saw vs. what was ultimately approved by the admin.
