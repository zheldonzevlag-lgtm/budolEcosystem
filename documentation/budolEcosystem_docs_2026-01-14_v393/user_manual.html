Faster Face Login

In version v393, we've optimized how you log in with your face. You no longer need to take a picture every time you open the app if your phone supports Face ID or Android Face Unlock.

How it works now:

1. Tap the Face Icon

On the login screen, tap the face recognition icon as usual.

2. Automatic Scan

If your phone has built-in face recognition, the system will automatically scan your face. You don't need to press the camera shutter button.

3. Logged In!

Once the scan is successful, you'll be taken straight to your dashboard.

Pro Tip: If your phone doesn't support built-in face recognition, the app will still let you take a quick selfie to verify your identity.
