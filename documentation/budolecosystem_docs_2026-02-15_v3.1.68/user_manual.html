Quick Registration

Step 1: Phone Verification

Enter your mobile number on the login screen. You will receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS.

Step 2: OTP Entry

Enter the received code to verify your identity. If you are a new user, you will be redirected to complete your profile.

Mobile App Branding

The app now displays the "budolPay" logo on the splash screen, reflecting our official branding. You can find the version number (v3.1.68) at the bottom of the splash screen during startup.
