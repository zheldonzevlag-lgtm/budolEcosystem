User Manual - Version v1.2.11 Highlights

New: Cancel Button in Scan to Pay

We've added a highly requested feature to improve your navigation experience when using the "Scan to Pay" feature.

Easier Navigation

If you accidentally open the scanner or decide not to proceed with a payment, you can now tap the CANCEL button at the bottom of the scanner screen to return to the home page immediately.

Improved App Performance

This version includes under-the-hood optimizations that make the app feel snappier and more responsive, especially during the login and payment processes.

How to Check Your Version

The app version is displayed at the bottom of the login screen. Ensure you are seeing v1.2.11 to enjoy these new features.
