User Manual

For Sellers

Order Status Tabs

Orders are automatically moved between tabs based on their status:

  • To Ship: Orders that are paid and ready for fulfillment.
  • Shipping: Orders currently in transit (Lalamove).
  • Completed: Successfully delivered orders.
  • Cancelled: Failed or cancelled orders.

Updates happen in real-time. You do not need to refresh the page to see if a driver has been assigned or if a delivery is complete.

For Buyers

Real-time Tracking

When your order is shipped via Lalamove, you can track the status in real-time from your Order Details page. You will see updates such as "Looking for Driver", "In Transit", and "Delivered" immediately as they occur.
