Mitigation Strategy - v352

Strategies for addressing R-352-01 through R-352-04

Index Risk Register Mitigation

Mitigation for R-352-01 (UI Session Error)

Action: Implemented isAuthenticated guards in HomeScreen and WalletScreen.

Result: Completed. The snackbar error is no longer triggered after logout.

Mitigation for R-352-02 (Performance Polling)

Action: Use SWR (Stale-While-Revalidate) with reasonable refresh intervals and explicit mounted checks.

Ongoing: Monitoring battery usage and considering migrating to WebSocket-only updates in future releases.

Mitigation for R-352-03 (Session Leakage)

Action: Explicitly clear _tokenKey and _userKey in _clearSession() within ApiService.

Result: Verified. Tokens are removed from persistent storage on logout.

Mitigation for R-352-04 (Data Privacy)

Action: Ensure no PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is stored in logs and sensitive data in SharedPreferences is minimal.

Ongoing: Regular audits of local storage contents.
