Stability Improvements

In this version, we have focused on making the logout process smoother and error-free.

Seamless Logout

Previously, some users might have seen a "Session expired" message immediately after logging out. This was a technical glitch and did not affect your account security. In version 1.2.58, this message has been removed, and you will be taken directly to the login screen with a "Logged out successfully" confirmation.

Accurate Balance Updates

We've improved how the app handles your balance display when moving between different screens, ensuring you always see the most up-to-date information without unnecessary error messages.

How to update?

No action is required. The app will automatically update to version 1.2.58 on your next launch or via your app store.
