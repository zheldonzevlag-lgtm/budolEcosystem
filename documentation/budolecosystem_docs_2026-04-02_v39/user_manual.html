User Manual: Scanning QR Codes in v39

1. Open Scanner

Tap the Scan button on the home screen. You will see a new high-tech alignment frame with pink corners and a scanning laser.

2. Align & Scan

Point your camera at a budolPay QR code. Ensure the code stays within the pink guidelines. The animated laser will turn green (conceptually) and vibrate once the code is captured.

3. Low Light Mode

In dark environments, tap the Flashlight icon in the top right corner to toggle your device's torch.

4. Scan from Photo

If you have a QR code saved in your gallery, tap the Gallery icon in the top right and select the image.
