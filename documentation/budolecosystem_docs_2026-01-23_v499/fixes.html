Fixes Applied

Detailed record of bug fixes and architectural improvements in v499.

F-499-01 Critical

Intl.DateTimeFormat TypeError in dateUtils.js

Resolved conflict where dateStyle/timeStyle were provided alongside individual date components (year, month, day), which is prohibited by the Intl API.

File: budolshap-0.1.0/lib/dateUtils.js
F-499-02 Standardization

Elimination of Hardcoded +8h Offsets

Replaced 15+ instances of new Date().getTime() + (8 * 60 * 60 * 1000) with standardized getNowUTC() calls across Lalamove Webhook, Order Service, and BudolID API.

Services: budolshap-0.1.0, budolID-0.1.0, budolpay-0.1.0
F-499-03 Feature

Standardized CSV Export Formatting

Implemented formatManilaTime in the Transactions Page CSV export to ensure exported dates match the UI display and adhere to local regulatory requirements.

File: budolpay-0.1.0/apps/admin/app/transactions/page.tsx
F-499-04 Compliance

Audit Log Timestamp Integrity

Standardized createAuditLog helper in wallet-service and settlement-service to use UTC timestamps, ensuring compliance with BSP Circular 808 and PCI DSS 10.2.2.

Services: wallet-service, settlement-service
