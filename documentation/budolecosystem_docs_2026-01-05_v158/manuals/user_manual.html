What's New in Version 1.1.4

📱 Phone Number Transfers

You can now send money or cash in using just a phone number! No need to remember long email addresses anymore.

How to use: Simply enter the recipient's 11-digit phone number in the "Recipient Email or Phone" field when sending money.

⚡ Performance & Stability

We've optimized the app internals and fixed several minor bugs to ensure a smoother and faster experience.
