User Manual: PIN Recovery & Security Verification

This update adds self-service PIN recovery and enforces higher security standards for money transfers. This keeps your account safe from unauthorized access.

🆘
Forgot your PIN?

No more calling support. You can now reset your own PIN securely using your phone's SMS verification code.

PIN Reset Instructions

  • 1 Open BudolPay and enter your phone number.
  • 2 On the PIN Entry screen, click the "Forgot PIN?" link at the bottom.
  • 3 Confirm the reset and check your messages for a 6-digit OTP code.
  • 4 Enter the code correctly, then choose a new 6-digit PIN.
  • 5 Your PIN is now updated instantly!

Sending Money (P2P Transfers)

🛡️
Verification Required

To prevent fraud, BASIC accounts are now barred from sending money to other users. You can still Cash-In and Pay Bills.

If you see an error say "Verification required: BASIC accounts cannot send money", please visit your account settings to upload your ID and upgrade to FULLY VERIFIED status.
