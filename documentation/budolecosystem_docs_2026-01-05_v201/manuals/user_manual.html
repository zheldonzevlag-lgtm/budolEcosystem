User Manual: Notification Settings Layout Refactor

Version: v201 | Date: 2026-01-05

1. Introduction

Welcome to the updated Notification Settings dashboard. We've redesigned the layout to make managing your Email and SMS providers easier and more intuitive.

2. What's New?

  • Full-Width Sections: Email and SMS providers are now organized into their own dedicated rows, giving you more space to configure settings.
  • Improved Visibility: Active providers are clearly highlighted with a blue (Email) or green (SMS) border and an "Active" badge.
  • Responsive Design: The dashboard now automatically adjusts to your screen size, making it easy to manage notifications from your phone or tablet.

3. How to Configure Providers

  1. Navigate to Settings: Go to the "Notification Settings" page from the Admin Sidebar.
  2. Choose a Category: Scroll to either the "Email Providers" or "SMS Providers" section.
  3. Select a Provider: Click the "Use This" button on the provider you wish to activate.
  4. Enter Credentials: Fill in the required API keys, SMTP hosts, or other credentials in the provider's form.
  5. Save Settings: Click the "Save Settings" button within the provider's card to apply your changes.
Tip: You can only have one active provider for Email and one for SMS at a time. Activating a new provider will automatically deactivate the previous one.

4. Troubleshooting

If you don't see the "Active" badge after clicking "Use This", try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, ensure that all required fields for that provider are correctly filled out.
