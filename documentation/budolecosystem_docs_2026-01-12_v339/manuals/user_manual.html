NEW BIOMETRIC FEATURE

Facial Recognition Login

In version v338, we've introduced Facial Recognition for a faster and more secure way to access your BudolPay account.

Fast Login

Access your wallet in seconds without typing your password or PIN.

Liveness Check

The system verifies you are a real person with blink and smile detection, preventing photo spoofing.

Secure Storage

Your facial data is converted into a secure mathematical model and stored using industry-standard encryption.

How to use it?

1. Open the Login screen.
2. Tap the "Face Login" button.
3. Look at the camera and perform the requested actions (blink or smile).
4. You're in!
