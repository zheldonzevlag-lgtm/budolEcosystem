Audit Summary

As of Version 337, the Budol Ecosystem has successfully mitigated 12 out of 13 identified primary security risks. The Refresh Token Rotation (SEC-007), SSL Pinning (SEC-337-01), and Biometric Hardening (SEC-337-05) implementations have significantly reduced the attack surface for session hijacking, traffic interception, and local storage compromise.

The remaining "Partial" risk (SEC-004) is being addressed through upcoming Device Trust Level 2 enhancements in Phase 8.
