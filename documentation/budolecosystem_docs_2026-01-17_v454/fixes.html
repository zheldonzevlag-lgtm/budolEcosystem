Issues & Fixes - v454

Issue: Marketing Ads Screen Persistent Skip

Symptoms: Despite previous fixes, the Marketing Ads screen is still reported as not showing after the splash screen.

Root Cause: Potential race conditions on Web platform or persistent hasSeenAds flag causing the logic to skip the onboarding flow.

Fix: Diagnostic Panel & Stability Improvements

  • DEBUG TOOLS Panel: Added a more prominent debug panel to SplashScreen with RESET ADS and FORCE ADS buttons.
  • Lifecycle Logging: Added debugPrint statements to MarketingAdsScreen to verify it is actually being reached and rendered.
  • Web Stability: Added extra delays in initState of MarketingAdsScreen to ensure the PageController has clients before starting auto-scroll, which improves rendering reliability on Web.
