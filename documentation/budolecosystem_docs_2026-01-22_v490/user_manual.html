User Manual - What's New in v490

Full Order Numbers

You can now see your entire order number in your order history and details page. This makes it easier to track your orders and provide information when contacting support.

Improved Dispute Resolution

For sellers and admins, the full order and transaction IDs are now visible in the disputes dashboard, ensuring faster and more accurate resolution of payment issues.

Clearer Shipping Arrangement

The shipping modal now displays the full order ID, allowing for better verification when arranging pickups or drop-offs with couriers.
