1. Getting Started

Registration & Login

  1. Open the BudolPay app.
  2. Enter your mobile number to register or login.
  3. You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) via SMS (or console in dev mode).
  4. Enter the OTP to verify your account.

2. Home Screen Features

Viewing Your Profile Name

For your privacy, your name is hidden by default on the home screen (shown as ********).

To view your name:

  1. Locate the "Welcome back, ********" text at the top of the screen.
  2. Tap the Eye Icon 👁️ next to the asterisks.
  3. Your full name will be revealed.
  4. Tap the icon again to hide your name.

Note: If your name still shows as asterisks after tapping, ensure you are fully logged in and connected to the internet.

3. Troubleshooting

I didn't receive an OTP

  • Check if your phone has a signal.
  • Wait for 60 seconds and try the "Resend OTP" button.
  • If testing in a development environment, ask the administrator to check the system logs.
