Setting up Face Recognition

To enable Face Recognition login, follow these steps:

  1. Open Settings from the home screen.
  2. Scroll down to the Account section.
  3. Tap on Face Recognition Login (indicated by a camera icon).
  4. Grant camera permissions if prompted.
  5. Hold the phone in front of your face and tap Scan Now.
  6. Once the scan is complete, you will see "Face Registered".

You can now use your face to log in from the PIN screen by tapping the face icon.

Clearing your Face Profile

If you wish to remove your face profile, simply tap the trash icon next to "Face Registered" in Settings.
