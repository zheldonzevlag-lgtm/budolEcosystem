User Manual - BudolPay v1.3.14

App Version: 1.3.14+110

Feature: Secure Biometric & Face Login

How to Enable Biometric/Face Login

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and your face is well-lit before starting.

  1. Open BudolPay and navigate to the Settings screen.
  2. Locate the Security section.
  3. Tap on Face Recognition Login.
  4. Enter your 6-digit MPIN for security verification.
  5. Position your face within the camera frame when prompted.
  6. Wait for the "Face login enabled successfully!" confirmation.
  7. You can now use Face ID or Fingerprint for future logins.

Troubleshooting

  • Face Not Detected: Ensure you are in a bright area and remove sunglasses or masks.
  • PIN Verification Failed: Double-check your MPIN. If forgotten, use the "Forgot PIN" link on the login screen.
  • Option Missing: Ensure your device supports biometric hardware and it is enabled in Android System Settings.
