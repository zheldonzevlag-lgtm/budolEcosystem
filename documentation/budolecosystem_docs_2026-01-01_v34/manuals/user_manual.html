User Manual: Universal Verification

What has changed?

We have simplified how you verify your identity. Instead of verifying yourself on every app (Shap, Pay, Loan), you now have a single Budol Trust Status.

New Requirements:

  • Phone Number: A valid phone number is now required for all new registrations.
  • Profile Picture: We encourage uploading a profile picture during registration. This increases your initial Trust Score and speeds up future verification for BudolPay and Budoloan.

Registration Changes

The registration form now includes new fields to help us verify your identity faster:

  • Phone Number: Enter your active mobile number. This is used for account recovery and secure login.
  • Profile Picture: You can now provide a link to your profile picture or upload one during registration. High-quality photos help reduce security flags on your account.

Advanced Verification (Face ID)

For sensitive activities like withdrawing funds from your wallet or applying for credit, you will be required to complete a one-time Face Verification. This involves taking a live photo to match against your registered profile, ensuring your account remains secure.

Merchant Workflow (KYB)

  1. Register your store in budolShap.
  2. Submit your business permits and documents.
  3. Once the admin approves your store in Shap, your Business Trust is automatically updated across the ecosystem.
  4. You can now instantly activate your budolPay merchant wallet without submitting documents again.

Anti-Fraud Security

To protect our marketplace, we use advanced security checks during registration. If your account is flagged for suspicious activity (e.g., using a disposable email or multiple registrations), you may be asked to complete additional verification steps before you can start selling or buying.
