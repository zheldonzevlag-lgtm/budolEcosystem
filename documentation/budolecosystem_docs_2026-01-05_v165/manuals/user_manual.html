Welcome to Version 1.1.6

This update is a major stability release that addresses common issues reported by our users. We recommend all users to upgrade to 1.1.6 for the best experience.

✨ Key Improvements:

  • No More White Screens: We've implemented a new recovery system that prevents the app from getting stuck on a blank screen.
  • Fixed Transaction History: Your recent activity (money sent and received) now loads reliably and displays the correct amounts.
  • Improved Error Messages: If something goes wrong, the app now provides clear instructions on how to fix it (like resetting your session).
  • Faster App Start: We've optimized the login process to get you to your wallet faster.

Installation Guide

  1. Download the app-release.apk from your organization's portal.
  2. If prompted, allow "Install from unknown sources" in your Android settings.
  3. Install the update. Your settings and login will be preserved.
  4. Launch the app and verify the version 1.1.6+1 is displayed in the settings or app info.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter an "Oops! Something went wrong" screen:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Tap the "Reset App & Logout" button. This is a safe way to clear temporary glitches.
  • Log back in with your credentials.
