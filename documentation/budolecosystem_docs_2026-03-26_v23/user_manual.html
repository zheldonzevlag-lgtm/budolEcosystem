User Manual: Shopping & Account Access

Accessing the BudolShap Store

The BudolShap production environment is hosted on Vercel and is fully operational.

URL: https://budolshap.vercel.app

Viewing Products

To view all restocked items:

  1. Navigate to https://budolshap.vercel.app/shop.
  2. You should see categories like Electronics, Health, and Furniture on the sidebar.
  3. Click on a category to filter products.
  4. Restored items (Smart Watch, Office Desk, etc.) are available for purchase.

User Login & Profiles

If you have an existing account from the local development environment, your credentials have been synchronized to Vercel.

  1. Click "Sign In" at the top right corner.
  2. Enter your registered email and password. (Account data restorted as of Mar 26).
  3. Your Wallet Balance should reflect your last known local balance.

Support

If products do not appear after logging in, please try clearing your browser cache or wait 2 minutes for the Vercel Edge Cache to update.
