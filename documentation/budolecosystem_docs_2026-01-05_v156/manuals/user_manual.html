User Manual - BudolPay Cash In & Send Money

Introduction

The BudolPay mobile application now supports full Cash In and Send Money (P2P Transfer) functionality. These features are synchronized in real-time with the Admin Dashboard for transparent financial tracking.

New Features

1. Cash In New

Users can now add funds to their wallet using various payment providers. The balance is updated instantly upon completion.

  • Navigate to the "Cash In" screen from the home dashboard.
  • Select a provider and enter the desired amount.
  • Confirm the transaction. Your balance will update in real-time.

2. Send Money (P2P Transfer) New

Transfer funds instantly to other BudolPay users using their email address.

  • Navigate to the "Send Money" screen.
  • Enter the recipient's email address. The system will automatically verify the user.
  • Enter the amount and an optional description.
  • Confirm the transfer. Both the sender and receiver will receive real-time balance updates.

Real-time Notifications

You will receive instant feedback for every transaction:

  • Success Messages: Appear immediately after a transaction is processed.
  • Balance Updates: The balance shown on your home screen refreshes automatically without needing to pull-to-refresh.
  • Transaction History: New transactions appear at the top of your recent activity list instantly.

Troubleshooting

If a recipient is "Not Found" during Send Money, ensure that the email address is spelled correctly and that the user has an active BudolPay account.
