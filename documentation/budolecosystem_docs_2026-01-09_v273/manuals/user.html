Update: Version 1.2.33 Release

Highlights of Version 1.2.33

This version focuses on standardizing the user experience and improving visual clarity throughout the application.

1. Refreshed Marketing Icons

Enjoy a cleaner look with new Lucide icons across our service slides, providing a consistent modern feel.

2. Enhanced Branding

The "budol" ecosystem services (Pay, Shap, Loan, Express) now feature distinct brand colors in their descriptions, making it easier to identify different services at a glance.

3. Improved Transaction Review

The Transaction Details screen has been reorganized to prioritize recipient information, making it easier to verify your payments before finalizing them.

How to Update

Download the latest budolPay_1.2.33.apk from your provided installer link and install it on your device. The version number will be displayed on the splash screen and within the app settings.
