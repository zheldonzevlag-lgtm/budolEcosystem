Managing Security Settings

Your security is our priority. You can manage your biometric login preferences directly from the Settings screen.

1. Accessing Settings

Tap on the Profile or Settings icon from the main dashboard.

2. Biometric Login (Fingerprint)

Toggle the Biometric Login switch to enable or disable fingerprint authentication. Once enabled, you can log in using your device's fingerprint scanner.

3. Face Recognition Login

Tap Setup face login to register your face profile. You will be asked to enter your 6-digit MPIN for security verification before the camera opens.

4. Removing Face Data

If you wish to remove your registered face, tap the Trash/Delete icon next to "Face Registered".
