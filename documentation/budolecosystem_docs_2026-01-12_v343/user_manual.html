Using the budolPay Dashboard

1. Viewing Your Balance

After a successful login, your current balance is displayed at the top of the dashboard. The balance is retrieved in real-time from the Wallet Service.

Troubleshooting: If you see "Failed to fetch balance", please ensure your internet connection is active and the system services are online.

2. Transaction History

Your recent activities, including P2P transfers and cash-ins, are listed in the Transaction History section. Each entry includes the amount, type, and status.

Note: Transaction history displays the most recent 50 activities. If the list is empty, it may be because no transactions have been performed on this account yet.

3. Data Privacy

Your financial data is protected under the NPC Data Privacy Act. Only you can view your balance and transaction history after providing valid authentication (PIN/Password).
