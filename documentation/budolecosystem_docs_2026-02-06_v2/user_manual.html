Joining BudolShap

Welcome to the Budol Ecosystem! Getting started with BudolShap is now easier than ever with our optimized registration process.

Starting Your Registration

To create a new account, follow these simple steps on any device:

1. Open the Sign-Up Form

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the login screen. A registration window will appear. On mobile phones, this window is now fully scrollable, so you can easily reach all fields from top to bottom.

2. Fill in Your Details

Enter your phone number, name, email, and choose a secure password. You can swipe up and down to navigate through the form smoothly.

3. Verify Your Identity (Selfie)

For your security, we require a quick selfie. The camera window will automatically adjust to fit your screen. Simply look at the camera and click "Capture." You'll see a preview of your photo before submitting.

Registration Tips

  • Stable Connection: Ensure you have a good internet connection when capturing your selfie.
  • Clear Lighting: Make sure your face is well-lit for the best verification results.
  • Scroll to Bottom: Don't forget to scroll all the way to the bottom to click the "Create Account" button!
