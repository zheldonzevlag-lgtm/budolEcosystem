Implemented Fixes

Details of the bugs resolved in this release.

F-004: Registration Modal Visibility Cutoff

Problem: On small screens or mobile devices, the long registration form (Standard Registration) exceeded the viewport height, making the bottom fields (Selfie, Submit) unreachable.

Fix: Implemented max-h-[90vh] and overflow-y-auto on the modal content container in LoginModal.jsx. Added custom-scrollbar utility for a clean UI.

F-005: Non-Responsive Registration Form Width

Problem: The modal had a fixed max-w-sm, which felt cramped on medium screens and potentially too wide on very narrow devices.

Fix: Updated to w-full max-w-sm sm:max-w-md to provide more horizontal breathing room on larger screens while remaining full-width with padding on mobile.

F-006: Selfie Capture/Preview UI Scaling

Problem: The camera feed and selfie preview image had fixed max-widths that didn't adapt to the slightly wider modal on tablet/desktop.

Fix: Added responsive sm:max-w classes to both the video and image containers in AuthForm.jsx.

F-007: Close Button Hidden by Scroll

Problem: When the form content was long, the close button (X icon) could be overlaid by form elements or get lost in the scroll.

Fix: Added z-10 to the XIcon in LoginModal.jsx to ensure it remains clickable and visible above all scrollable content.
