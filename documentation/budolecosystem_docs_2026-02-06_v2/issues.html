Known Issues

Issues identified during the development and testing of the BudolShap UI Optimization (v2).

Minor

Scrollbar Aesthetics

The custom-scrollbar styling is functional but may appear slightly different across browser engines (WebKit vs Gecko) due to varying support for ::-webkit-scrollbar pseudo-elements.

Minor

Camera Feed Aspect Ratio

On extremely narrow screens (< 320px), the camera preview may letterbox to maintain aspect ratio, which is expected behavior but can be improved with smarter cropping in future versions.

Major

Keyboard Overlap on iOS

When the virtual keyboard is active on iOS Safari, the bottom-most fields of the registration form might still be partially obscured until the user manually scrolls. This is a known mobile browser viewport behavior.
