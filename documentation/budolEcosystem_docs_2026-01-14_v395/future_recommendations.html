Future Recommendations - v395

Proposed Enhancements

  • Unified Settings UI: Provide clear toggles for "Use App Face Scan" vs "Use System Face Scan" to give users explicit control over which flow they prefer.
  • Background Liveness: Explore background liveness detection (passive) during normal app usage to periodically verify the user without interrupting the flow.
  • Server-Side Cross-Check: Periodically send captured embeddings to the backend for auditing against the KYC-registered face model to detect device-level tampering.
