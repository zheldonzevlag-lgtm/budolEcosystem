Understanding the Face Icon

The Face icon on the login screen now intelligently switches between the app's custom security scan and your phone's built-in facial recognition.

Scenario 1: Custom Face Login (App Scan)

If you have registered your face profile within the budolPay settings:

1
Tap the Face Icon on the login screen.
2
The camera will open. Take a clear selfie and perform liveness checks (blink or smile).
3
You will be logged in securely.

Scenario 2: System Face Login (Native)

If you haven't scanned your face into the app, but your phone supports Face ID or Android Face Unlock:

1
Tap the Face Icon.
2
Your phone's system biometric prompt will appear.
3
Look at the sensor to unlock the app instantly.
