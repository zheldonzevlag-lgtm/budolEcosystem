User Manual: Enhanced Payment Experience

User Guide Version 121 | 2026-01-04

What's New?

We've updated the Payment Summary screen to be clearer and easier to use. You can now see more details about your purchase before you confirm the payment.

New Features

1. More Detailed Summaries

When you scan a QR code, you will now see:

  • Store Name: The full name of the merchant you are paying.
  • Reference ID: A unique tracking number for your order.
  • Payment Method: How the money will be deducted (e.g., budolPay Wallet).

2. Improved "Cancel" Button

The "Cancel" option is now a large, easy-to-tap button at the bottom of the screen. We've also made sure it's always visible and never cut off by your phone's navigation bar.

Issues & Fixes

  • Issue: User received a generic "User not logged in" error despite having logged in.
    Fix: We fixed a confusing error message. If your session expires, the app will now clearly tell you to log in again instead of showing a generic error.
  • Issue: "Cancel" text was cut off on some devices.
    Fix: The payment confirmation and cancel buttons are now properly spaced and visible for all screen sizes.

Risk & Mitigation

  • Risk: Session timeout during the scanning process.
    Mitigation: The app now provides a clear message if you need to log in again, ensuring you don't lose track of your payment status.

Workflow Example

  1. Tap Scan from the home screen.
  2. Point your camera at a budolPay QR code.
  3. Review the Payment Summary screen (Check the Amount and Store Name).
  4. Tap CONFIRM PAYMENT to complete the transaction, or Cancel to go back.

Recommendation for Future Enhancement

Adding a "Recent Merchants" list to the home screen for quick access to frequent stores without needing to re-scan a QR code.
