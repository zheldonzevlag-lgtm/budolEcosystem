🚀 Implementation Plan - v59

QR Payment Flow & Cart Persistence

🎯 Purpose

The primary objective of this implementation was to resolve critical issues in the checkout and payment flow of Budolshap, specifically:

  • Session Logout/Cart Clearance: Prevent loss of state during payment.
  • Premature Redirects: Fix early navigation to /orders for QR payments.
  • Missing PayMongo Authorization: Ensure QR flows aren't interrupted by unmounting.
  • Server Errors: Fix host mismatches and email async handling.

🛠️ Technical Implementation

1. Unified QR Payment Flow

Migrated from modal-based QR to a dedicated page at /payment/qr.

  • OrderSummary.jsx: Redirects to dedicated QR page.
  • QRPaymentPage.jsx: Handles polling and state recovery.

2. Cart Persistence

Introduced isProcessing state in cart/page.jsx to keep OrderSummary mounted during payment redirects.

3. Payment & Webhooks

  • paymongo-adapter.js: Added orderId to metadata.
  • budolpay-adapter.js: Fixed host mismatch in checkout URLs.
  • email.js: Added missing await to sendMail.

🏗️ Architectural Decisions

Decoupled Payment Pages

Moved away from "In-Page" modals for complex flows to prevent component unmounting from breaking the payment lifecycle.

Multi-Layered State Recovery

Implemented "LocalStorage First, URL Fallback" to ensure payment data can be recovered even after page refreshes.
