A Smoother Way to Shop

We've upgraded the BudolPay marketplace with professional visuals and smooth animations to make your shopping experience more intuitive and enjoyable.

Visual Navigation

Categories now feature clean, vector-based icons that are easy to identify. This helps you find the products you need faster, whether you're browsing on a desktop or mobile device.

Dynamic Feedback

When you hover over category sections on the homepage, you'll notice subtle animations. These provide clear feedback on where you're clicking and add a premium feel to the platform.

Consistent Experience

From the homepage to the individual shop pages and even your order updates, symbols are now consistent across the entire app. No more confusing emojis—just professional icons.
