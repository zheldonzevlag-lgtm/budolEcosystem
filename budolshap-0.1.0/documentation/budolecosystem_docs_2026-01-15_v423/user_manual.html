User Manual - v423

Information for Users

Your account now supports more detailed profile information. When an administrator creates or updates your account, they can include your phone number and address.

Profile Features

  • Verified Phone Number: Used for account notifications and security.
  • Saved Address: Used for delivery of orders and logistical coordination.

Data Privacy

Your phone number and address are stored securely and are only accessible by administrators and for processing your orders. We comply with the Data Privacy Act (NPC) to protect your information.
