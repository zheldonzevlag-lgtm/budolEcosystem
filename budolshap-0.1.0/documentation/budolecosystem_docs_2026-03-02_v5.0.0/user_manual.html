Purpose

This manual guides end-users through the features and functionalities of the BudolEcosystem application, from browsing products to completing purchases and managing orders.

Getting Started

Account Creation and Login

To access all features, you need to create an account. Click on "Sign Up" and follow the prompts. If you already have an account, click "Login" and enter your credentials.

Browsing Products

You can browse products by category, search for specific items, or explore featured products on the homepage. Product cards display key information, and videos can be viewed by hovering over them.

Shopping Experience

Product Details

Click on any product to view its detailed page, including descriptions, images, videos, variations, and reviews.

Adding to Cart

Select desired variations (if any) and quantity, then click "Add to Cart". You can view your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navigation bar.

Checkout Process

From the cart, proceed to checkout. You will be guided through steps to confirm your shipping address, choose a shipping method, and select a payment method.

Payment Methods

BudolEcosystem supports various payment methods, including:

  • **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Pay upon delivery of your order.
  • **GCash:** Secure mobile payment via GCash.
  • **GrabPay:** Mobile payment via GrabPay.
  • **Maya (formerly PayMaya):** Mobile payment via Maya.
  • **Credit/Debit Card:** Secure payments processed via payment gateways.

Payment Verification

For online payments, you may see a "Verifying Payment" modal while your transaction is being processed. If a payment fails or times out, you will be notified with appropriate messages.

Order Management

Viewing Orders

You can view your past and current orders in your account dashboard. Each order will show its status (e.g., "Order Placed", "Processing", "Shipped", "Delivered").

Order Status Updates

You will receive notifications regarding changes in your order status.

Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please refer to the FAQ section or contact our customer support.
