Purpose

This document identifies and assesses potential risks to the BudolEcosystem application, outlining their impact, likelihood, and current status. It serves as a proactive tool for risk management and mitigation planning.

Impact and Likelihood are rated on a scale of 1 to 5, where 5 is the highest. Risk Score = Impact * Likelihood.

Risk Assessment Key

  • Impact:
    • 1: Negligible (Minor inconvenience)
    • 2: Low (Minor disruption, easily recoverable)
    • 3: Medium (Moderate disruption, some data loss possible, recoverable)
    • 4: High (Significant disruption, major data loss, difficult recovery)
    • 5: Critical (Catastrophic, severe data loss, business continuity threatened)
  • Likelihood:
    • 1: Very Low (Rare, almost impossible)
    • 2: Low (Unlikely, but possible)
    • 3: Medium (Possible, could happen)
    • 4: High (Likely, expected to happen)
    • 5: Very High (Almost certain, will happen)
