User Manual: Transparency & Security

Your Data & Security

At BudolEcosystem, we prioritize the security and integrity of your transactions. We have implemented a comprehensive audit logging system that records every critical action, ensuring:

  • Transparency: All order updates, payments, and shipping changes are logged.
  • Accountability: Every action is traceable to a specific user or system event.
  • Security: Our logs are tamper-proof, meaning no one can alter the history of your transactions.

Dispute Resolution

In case of any disputes (e.g., incorrect refund, missing shipment), our support team can access these detailed forensic logs to verify exactly what happened and when, ensuring a fair resolution for all parties involved.
