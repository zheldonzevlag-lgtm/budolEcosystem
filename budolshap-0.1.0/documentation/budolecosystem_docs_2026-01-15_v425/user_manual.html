User Manual - Release v425

For Store Owners

Your store verification process is now faster and more reliable. Once an administrator approves your store, you will receive an immediate notification in your dashboard without needing to refresh the page.

How to check your status:

  1. Log in to your Seller Dashboard.
  2. Look for the verification badge next to your store name.
  3. If your store is newly approved, you will see the status update in realtime.

For Customers

This update ensures that only verified and active stores are visible and operational on the platform, providing a safer shopping experience.
