Phase 4: Platform Mediation & Risk Assessment

Beyond the buyer-seller interaction, the platform (BudolShap Admin) acts as the ultimate authority in the "Shopee Guarantee" model. This phase documents the arbitration logic, risk mitigation strategies, and the technical safeguards protecting the marketplace's financial integrity.

1. Platform Approval (Admin Mediation)

Dispute Arbitration Flow

When an order enters the RETURN_DISPUTED state, it is flagged for manual platform review. The Admin Mediation process follows these steps:

  • Evidence Audit: Admin reviews Buyer's photos (damaged item) vs. Seller's counter-evidence (packing video/proof of shipping).
  • Chat History Review: Accessing internal buyer-seller chat logs to identify verbal agreements or admissions.
  • Final Verdict: Admin triggers respondToReturn with a platform-override status (either REFUNDED or REJECTED).

3. Performance Mitigation (Seller & Buyer Performance)

A. Seller: Non-Fulfilment Rate (NFR)

To mitigate seller-side risk, BudolShap tracks:

  • NFR Impact: Returns due to seller error (Wrong Item, Damaged) increase NFR.
  • Penalty Points: Exceeding NFR thresholds triggers visibility restrictions.
B. Buyer: Buyer Return Rate (BRR) & Fraud Score

To prevent marketplace abuse by buyers, the system tracks performance metrics similar to Shopee's "Fraud Prevention" suite:

  • Buyer Return Rate (BRR): Calculated as (Approved Returns / Total Orders) over 90 days. Users with >20% BRR are flagged as "Serial Returners".
  • COD Unpaid Rate: Tracks how often a buyer refuses COD parcels. High rates lead to COD Payment Restriction.
  • Abuse Categories:
    • False Evidence: Submitting doctored photos to claim damages.
    • Voucher Farming: Creating multiple accounts to abuse return-and-rebuy discounts.
  • Sanctions: Repeat offenders may have their Shopee Guarantee window shortened or be permanently banned from the "Refund Only" path.

4. Mitigation Safeguards (Technical)

A. The Escrow Safety Net

BudolShap implements a Deferred Payout model. Funds from a completed sale are held in a PENDING state for 7 days (the "Shopee Guarantee" period). This ensures that if a return is requested, the funds are still available for refunding without needing to "claw back" money from a seller's bank account.

B. Evidence Immutability

Once a return request is submitted, images are stored with a timestamped link to the Return record. Neither party can delete or alter evidence once a dispute is officially raised, ensuring the Admin has a clean audit trail.
