Returns & Refunds

Fairness for both buyers and sellers. Our Return Shipping Fee (RSF) policy ensures that the responsible party covers delivery costs.

1. The RSF Policy

Return Shipping Fees are automatically calculated based on the reason for the return. This ensures a transparent and fair marketplace.

Return Reason RSF Payer Refund Amount
Damaged / Faulty Item Seller 100% Refund
Wrong Item Received Seller 100% Refund
Change of Mind Buyer Refund - RSF
Part of order missing Seller 100% Refund
✅ Expected Result: If the seller is the payer, the return shipping cost is deducted from their payout. If the buyer is the payer, it is deducted from their refund.

2. Step-by-Step Process

Follow these steps to ensure your return request is processed quickly.

Step 1: Initiate Request

Click "Return / Refund" in your order details before the guarantee expires.

3. Disputes & Mediation

If a seller rejects your request, it becomes a dispute. A budolShap Admin will step in to review evidence.

🛡️ Admin Mediation

Admins will review the unboxing video and photos provided. Their decision is final and ensures that the platform remains trustworthy for everyone.
