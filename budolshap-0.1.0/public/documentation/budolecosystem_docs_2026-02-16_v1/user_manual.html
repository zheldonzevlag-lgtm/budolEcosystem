1. Getting Started

Creating an Account

Click "Register" in the top right corner. Fill in your details. You can choose to be a Buyer or a Seller.

Logging In

Click "Login" and enter your credentials. Your session will be kept active unless you log out.

2. Shopping Experience

Browsing Products

Use the search bar or browse categories. Click on a product to view details.

Managing Your Cart

Add items to your cart. You can view your cart by clicking the cart icon. Your cart is saved automatically if you are logged in.

Note: If you add items as a guest and then log in, your guest items will be merged with your saved account cart!

3. For Sellers

Adding Products

Go to your dashboard and click "Add Product". You can upload multiple images and set variations (sizes, colors).

Managing Orders

View incoming orders in the "Orders" tab. Update status as you ship items.
